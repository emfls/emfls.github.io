❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How do I play the Ladder Game?
A. Tap a number at the top to let the line flow down. See where you end up!
Q2. What does Level Up mean?
A. You passed the current stage and move to the next wave.
Q3. What ends the game?
A. If your result is a red "X", it’s Game Over!
Q4. Is this a skill-based game?
A. Nope 😎 — the ladder is random every time. Just try your luck!
Q5. How can I retry?
A. Tap the Restart button after a Game Over to play again from Wave 1.
Q6. Can I share the game with friends?
A. Yes! Tap Share to copy the link or use mobile share features.
