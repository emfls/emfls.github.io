FAQ ‚Äì BMI & Health

What is BMI?
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It estimates body fat and assesses health risk.
Is BMI accurate?
BMI is a general guideline. It may not reflect body composition (muscle vs fat), so athletes or very fit people may have high BMI but low body fat.
What is a normal BMI?
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered normal for most adults.
Does BMI differ by age or gender?
BMI ranges are the same for adult men and women, but for children and teens, percentiles by age and sex are used.
How can I improve my BMI?
Healthy diet and regular exercise can help achieve a normal BMI. Always consult a medical professional for personalized advice.
BMI is a simple health indicator. For precise health, consider other factors.
