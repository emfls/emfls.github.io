❓ FAQ – Text to Speech (TTS)
Is my text uploaded to a server?
No. All text is processed locally in your browser. Nothing is sent or saved.
Can I download the voice as an MP3?
This tool records TTS as WAV (browser support only). For MP3, use third-party converters if needed.
Why are some voices missing?
Voice options depend on your browser and OS. Try using Chrome or Edge for more voices.
Is there a character limit?
Not enforced, but long texts may fail to play or save. Split into paragraphs for better results.
Does this work on mobile?
Yes, but audio playback and download features may vary depending on your device/browser.
