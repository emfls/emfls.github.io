Crime:In Phuket, crimes such as robbery, violence, and raids often occur

  • Scam Artist:Be careful because you can try a fraud for tourists

  • Supplies Management:Always be careful about your belongings and personal safety
  • False sales:Some private figures may buy tourists, so you should be familiar with local laws.
🌡️ Health problem
  • Tropical climate:In summer, health problems such as fatigue, dehydration, and heat stroke can occur due to strong sun and high humidity
  • Dengue:In some areas of Thailand, there is a dengue heat that occurs irregularly every year, so it is recommended to vaccine before departure.
  • Prevention measures:Prevention measures for daylight and mosquitoes using daylight blockers and mosquito flavors
  • Proper prevention:Tourists must take appropriate preventive measures and check their health.
🌊 Natural disasters, marine safety
  • Natural disasters:In Phuket, natural disasters such as typhoons and rainfall may occur, so local weather conditions are required.
  • Marine safety:Some beaches are very strong, so be careful when swimming
  • Marine life:Dangerous creatures such as sharks and abalone can exist, so swim only at the designated beach.
  • Safety Priority:When deciding on a schedule, safety should be prioritized.

  • Exchange Service:Since Phuket is a famous area for tourists, there are many shops and banks that provide exchange services.

  • Food poisoning risk:Thai food is very delicious, but there is a danger of causing food poisoning.

  • Clean restaurant:When eating, select a clean restaurant, and survey it before taking food.
