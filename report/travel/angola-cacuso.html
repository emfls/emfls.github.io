⚠️ Kakuso travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Use of mosquito repellents, wear long -sleeved clothes, prevention drugs
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • Wildlife:Do not get off the car during the safari, follow the guide instructions
  • Kang:Since it is a crocodile and hama habitat, it prohibits swimming without a guide
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, recommend using 4WD vehicles
  • Public transportation:Because it is limited, the tour guide or car rental
  • fuel:The spacing of the gas station is far, so the fuel is well prepared
  • communication:Mobile phones signal weak areas, consideration of satellite phones
  • Night movement:Avoid driving night driving due to lack of road lighting
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (May-September):The temperature is 15-25 degrees, dry and dusty
  • Woogi (10-April):Temperature 20-30 degrees, wet and heavy rain
  • ultraviolet rays:Ultraviolet rays are very strong, sunscreen must be near the equator
  • Daily crossroads:At night, the temperature drops significantly, prepared for warm clothes
  • Rainy Travel:Road flooding and traffic blockage
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:The price of daily necessities is high due to dependence on imported goods
  • Cash transaction:Limited credit card use, fully prepared for cash
  • Exchange:Exchange the dollar as a Quanza, use the official exchange office
  • Tip Culture:Tips for guides and drivers

💡 Kakuso Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:The dry season in May-September is optimal for wild animal observation and outdoor activities
  • Angola Traditional Food:Muffette (grilled fish), Mompa (palm oil stew), Pungge (cassava), Kaluru (fish stew) essential experience
  • Quanza River Boat Tour:Early morning or late afternoon is the best for wild animal observation.
  • Local language:Portuguese learning official language and basic greetings
  • Cultural experience:Participation in traditional culture of Angola such as Kimbo Dance and Semenba Music
  • Photo shooting:Baobab and sunset scenery is the best photo spot
  • Local guide:Required for professional knowledge of wild animals and culture
  • souvenir:Traditional basket, woodcarce crafts, Angola coffee recommended
