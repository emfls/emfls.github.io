⚠️ Kayanja travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Border area, late at night, city center center

  • Political anxiety:Burundi political situation instability, prohibition of access to assembly or protest areas
  • robbery:Prohibit to go alone at night, to minimize valuables
  • Border area:Prohibiting unauthorized access to the Rwanda border area, mandatory accompanying local guides
  • Race Conflict:Conflict regionalism between the Hutu and Tutchi
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, unable to pass in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Wearing overloading, seat belt when using minibus
  • taxi:Reliable taxi company use, preliminary negotiations
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, local driving culture
  • fuel:Lack of gas station, securing sufficient fuel
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (October-May):Intensive heavy rains and flood risks, moving plan adjustment
  • Dry (June-September):Dry and dusty, enough water intake
  • Highlands:Big and cold at night preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen near the equator
  • lightning:Lightning risks in the rainy season, avoiding high places
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Mosquito -mediated diseases, prevention drugs and mosquito repellents use
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water, avoid ice
  • food:Only intake of completely cooked food, street food attention
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical facilities, preparation for emergency situations
  • helminth:Prohibition of walking barefoot, wearing shoes
