Core points:Explore abundant history and culture from the historical city of Veria in the Macedonian region of Greece to the ancient Macedonian kingdom to Byzantine and the Ottoman Empire. Day 2: Vergina Royal Tomb and Ancient Ruins Tour

Vergina Royal Tomb → Philip II Tomb → Vergina Museum → Alia Kymon River → Nowa Village

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:Experience the glorious history of the ancient Macedonian kingdom in Bergina royal tomb, one of the most important archaeological discoveries in the world. Enjoy valuable relics such as the golden pavilion, armor, and jewelry found in the tomb of Philip II, the father of Alexander the Great, and explore the magnificent tombs and elaborate murals of the Macedonian royal family in the 4th century B.C.E. Gain a deep understanding of the history and culture of the Macedonian kingdom at the Vergina Museum, drive along the Alia Kyemon River, one of the longest rivers in Greece, and enjoy the beautiful natural scenery of the Macedonian plains. In Nausa, a traditional Macedonian village, try local wine and traditional foods and experience the unique culture of northern Greece.
