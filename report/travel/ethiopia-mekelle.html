⚠️Mekele Travel Precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Border area, late at night, city center center

  • Political anxiety:Political situation change in Tigra, required to confirm travel alerts before traveling before travel
  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at the mayor and bus terminal, the valuables are stored in the accommodation.
  • Night going out:Don't go alone after the sun and recommend using a group tour
  • Photo shooting:Military facilities, prohibition of shooting of government buildings, and seeking permission when shooting locals
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, worsening road conditions in the rainy season (June-September)
  • Public transportation:Wearing speed and overload, seat belt when using minibus
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, knowing local traffic laws
  • flight:1 hour and 30 minutes from Adis Ababa to Mekele
  • Desert Tour:Be sure to prepare GPS and satellite calls with local guides
🌡️Checks related to weather

  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays and sun creams near the equator
