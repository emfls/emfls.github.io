🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Late time for the city of Apia, a secluded beach

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing in tourist destinations and markets, bags of bags and zipper lock
  • Beach safety:Prohibition of visiting a secluded beach alone, recommended with locals
  • Fraud:Excessive tour prices or fake traditional craft sales attention
  • Going out late at night:Being alone after 9 pm, active only near the hostel
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The bus operation is irregular and the timetable is not accurate, securing leisure time
  • Rent -a -car:The left side traffic, the road condition is poor, and special care when driving
  • Taxi fee:There are many cases in the absence of a meter, preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Boat Tour:Cancellation can be canceled according to the weather, alternative schedule preparation
  • road sign:English and Samoao mixed, difficult to find
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (11-4):Temperature 24-30 degrees, moist and rainy, umbrella and waterproof products required
  • Dry (5-10):Temperature 22-28 degrees, relatively dry but still wet
  • Cyclone:November-April Cyclone Season, Check the weather information occasionally
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays near the equator, sunscreen should be applied occasionally
  • High humidity:More than 80% humidity throughout the year, wearing good clothes
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Tourist destination restaurant and souvenir store price 2-3 times expensive
