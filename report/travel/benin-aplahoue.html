⚠️Aplahou travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Use of mosquito repellents, wear long -sleeved clothes, prevention drugs
  • drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • hygiene:Washing hands often, street food attention, avoiding raw
  • Emergency situation:Local hospital location in advance, preparation for emergency contact information
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Boat safety:Wearing life jackets, boarding after checking the weather
  • Public transportation:Wearing helmet when using Gemijang (motorcycle taxi)
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, restrictions on moving in rainy season
  • Night movement:Late time moving and using a reliable driver
  • fuel:Lack of gas stations, checking fuel in advance
