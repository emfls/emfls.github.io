⚠️Caroy travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:The city center is late at night, outskirts

  • robbery:Prohibition of going alone at night, cash and valuables at least
  • Wildlife:In the Safari Tour, the guide order should be strictly observed, do not go out of the car
  • Political Assembly:Prohibition of access to political rally or protest areas
  • Local law:Absolutely prohibited from shooting government buildings or military facilities
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, recommended 4WD vehicles
  • Public transportation:Speed and overloading when using minibus
  • fuel:There are few gas stations, so secure enough fuel
  • drive:Traffic on the left, the crosswalk of the wildlife road
  • taxi:Use of official taxi, preliminary negotiations required
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (5-10):The temperature is 15-25 degrees, dry and dusty
  • Woogi (11-4):Temperature 20-30 degrees, heavy rain and flood risk
  • ultraviolet rays:African strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
  • Daily crossroads:At night, the temperature drops significantly, prepared for outerwear
  • mosquito:Malaria parameter, insect repellent and long -sleeved wear
💰 Price related precautions
  • inflation:Increased inflation, confirmation of local exchange rates occasionally
  • Cash payment:Most of them are limited to cash transactions, credit card use
  • Tip Culture:Tips for guides and service workers
  • Gourds:Removes for tourists, price negotiations required

💡 Karoy Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:The dry season in May-September is the best for sightseeing with Safari
  • Local food:Local traditional food experiences such as lions (corn porridge)
  • language:English and Showa Preparation of official language, basic English conversation
  • Culture courtesy:When greeting locals, handshake and use respect
  • Safari tip:Early morning and late afternoons are the best time to observe wild animals

  • electrical energy:Since the power outage is frequent, the flashlight and the auxiliary battery are required
