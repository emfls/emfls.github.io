⚠️Stong Kram Travel Precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Song Cram is a hot and humid tropical climate all year round, so it is especially important in summer.

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of the water market and the riverside area, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquitoes and long -sleeved clothes
🚂 McClon Railway Market Note
  • Train operation time:Whenever the train on the McClon railway is approached, the market is pulled back from the railway, so check the train schedule in advance.
  • Safety distance:When the train passes, maintain a safe distance and not stand on the railroad.
  • Photo shooting:Be careful not to be safety when shooting the moment when the train passes, and to avoid interfering with other tourists.
  • Local merchant consideration:Recognize that it is a living place for market merchants
🚤 Precautions when using water market
  • Boat safety:Seat belt is required when using paddle boats in Damnoen Saduak Water Market
  • Personal belongings:The belongings that can fall into the water when using the boat are stored in the waterproof bag.
  • Bargaining:In the water market, bargaining is common and required to negotiate appropriate price
  • Early time visit:If you visit early in the morning, there are few tourists and a more true water market.
🚗 Precautions when using transportation
  • Access from Bangkok:It takes about 1 hour and 30 minutes by vehicle from Bangkok to the southwest.
  • Guide Tour:Hotel pickup service is available as a comfortable exclusive vehicle when using a guide tour from Bangkok
  • Local transportation:Move in the city using Song Tae -woo or tuk -tuk, use after negotiations
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, local traffic laws and regulations required

  • Iceism:It is safe to avoid the ice of drinks sold on the street
