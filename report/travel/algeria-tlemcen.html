üö® Security and Safety Precautions

Major Areas of Concern: Night outings, outskirt areas, near borders

  • Terrorism Risk: Terrorism risk exists throughout Algeria, check foreign ministry travel advisories
  • Night Activities: Prohibited to go alone after sunset, stay inside hotels
  • Photography: Absolutely prohibited to photograph military facilities, government buildings, and police
  • Local Guides: Travel with trustworthy local guides recommended
  • Valuables Management: Store passport and cash in hotel safes
üöå Transportation and Movement Precautions
  • Public Transport: Bargaining essential when using buses and taxis, check meters
  • Rental Cars: International driving permit required, poor road conditions
  • Train: About 7 hours from Algiers to Tlemcen, advance booking recommended
  • Domestic Flights: 1.5 hours from Algiers to Tlemcen Airport
  • Road Conditions: Unstable road conditions due to mountainous terrain, night driving prohibited
