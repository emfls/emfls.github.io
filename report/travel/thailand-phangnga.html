⚠️Phangyeong -ah travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Pier, market, downtown, late beach

  • pickpocket:Personalism when boarding tourist attractions, markets, and boats
  • Gourd/Scam:Tour reservation, preliminary confirmation when using taxi
  • Night movement:Late beaches, rare people move alone
  • Water play safety:Wearing life jackets in activities such as snorkeling and kayaking
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Pick -up vehicle:Pick -up time for each tour, waiting in the hotel lobby
  • Traffic strike/delay:Sudden strike, secure a certain amount of time for traffic jams
  • Taxi/Grab:Checking the meter, required for negotiations for preliminary charges
  • Ship/Boat Move:Coming on departure time, knowing safety rules
  • Water Village:Note that the floor is slippery
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (November -April):Travel optimal, clear and comfortable weather
  • Rainy (May -October):Skall, sudden rain, waves care when moving offshore
  • ultraviolet rays:Because it is very strong, sunscreen required, hats/sunglasses wear
  • air conditioning:Indoor and outdoor temperature difference is large to prepare thin outerwear
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:James Bond Island, Pangaman Tour, Water Village Restaurant Price 2-3 times expensive
  • Admission fee/Tour fee:Similan Island Tour 2,000 ~ 3,500 Baht, Panga Bay Tour 1,500 ~ 3,000 Baht
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 10%, tour guide 50 ~ 100 baht, hotel housekeeping 20 ~ 50 baht
  • Local food:Seafood, tropical fruits, etc. are fresh but price fluctuations

💡 Pangunga Travel Tips

  • Free tourist destination:Little Amazon Mangrove Walk, Lampi Waterfall Walk
  • Local food:Yum Yum, Pad Thai, Fresh Seafood, Tropical Fruits Essential Experience
