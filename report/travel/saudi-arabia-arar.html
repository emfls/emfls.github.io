⚠️ Arard travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Borders, Outer Desert

  • Border area:There is a military facility near the Iraq border, so it is prohibited from taking pictures, accessing only licensed areas.
  • Desert Safety:Do not go deep into the desert without a guide, and secure enough water and communication means
  • Religious discipline:Dress regulations and respect for prayer time as an Islamic state
  • Female travelers:Recommended for long sleeves, long pants in conservative areas
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation limit:Arard is a small town, limited public transportation, recommended to use rental cars or taxis
  • Desert Road:GPS required when driving in the desert area, securing enough fuel
  • Taxi fee:Recommendation for use of meters, recommendation of pre -fee negotiations
  • Border pass:Many checkpoints near the Iraq border, ID card always portable
  • Desert Tour:4WD vehicle required, accompanied by an experienced driver
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature 35-45 degrees, extremely hot, recommended early or evening activities
  • Winter (12-2):5-20 degrees of temperature, preparation
  • Spring/Autumn:Sand breeze frequent, preparation of masks and goggles
  • Desert:Extremely dried, enough water intake and moisturizer
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Hotel fee:Optional restriction, preliminary reservation required
  • Restaurant price:Local restaurants are cheaper, and restaurants in the hotel are expensive
  • Desert Tour:Guide tour cost is high, group tour recommended
  • Fuel cost:Saudi Arabia is a low fuel cost and advantages when using a car rental
