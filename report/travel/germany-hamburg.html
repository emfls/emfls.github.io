⚠️ Hamburg travel precautions

🌧️Checks related to weather

Optimal travel period:This is the best month to travel in Hamburg from June to August

  • Xi'an Maritime Protection Climate:Humidity, wind blowing a lot, and many days of rain
  • Spring (March-May):It is raining about 9-12 days of every month, so it is essential for umbrellas, and in May, there are few tourists.
  • Summer (June-August):The daytime average temperature is gentle at 21-25 degrees, but in the evening, it falls to 10-12 degrees and requires a jacket.
  • Winter (12-2):There are a lot of gentle but humid and cloudy days, so the temperature can be lower.
🚲 Care
  • Bicycle city:Hamburg is one of the cities that ride a lot of bicycles, and the bicycle roads are well developed.
  • Pedestrianism:Be careful not to walk on a bicycle -only road.
  • Bicycle rental:If it does not exceed 30 minutes, it will not be charged.
🏪 Note that Sunday and holidays
  • Commercial facility closed:On Sundays and holidays, almost all commercial facilities, such as department stores, are closed.
  • Restaurant and Museum:Tourist spots such as dining and museums are open on holidays and are not inconvenient to travel except shopping.
  • Special operation of fish markets:Altona Fish Market is open from 5 am on Sunday
🚇 Public transportation
  • Hamburg Card:Unlimited use of public transportation and discounts at many attractions
  • Efficient transportation network:You can easily move major tourist attractions using subways, buses, and ferry
