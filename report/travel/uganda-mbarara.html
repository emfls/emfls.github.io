⚠️Mbarara travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Prevention drug required, mosquito repellent, regular use, long -sleeved wear
  • drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • Wildlife:Do not go out of the car during safari, not feed to animals.
  • Emergency situation:Local emergency room location in advance, storage of travel insurance contacts
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, deterioration of road conditions during rainy season
  • Public transportation:Speed and overloading when using Matotu (minibus)
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommendation, local driving habits need
  • Campala connection:4-5 hours by bus, 3-4 hours by taxi
  • Night movement:Avoiding as much as possible and using a reliable driver if it is inevitable
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (June-August, December-February):The temperature of 15-28 degrees, safari optimal period
  • Woogi (March-May, September-November):Green 18-25 degrees, road condition
  • altitude:A large cross at 1,400m above sea level
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen near the equator
  • Sudden shower:Preparation of umbrella and waterproof products
