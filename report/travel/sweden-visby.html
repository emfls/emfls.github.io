📅 Bis V 2 nights 3 days travel course

Day 1: Middle Ages and Old Towns

Bis -V Wall → Bis V Cathedral → Old Town Walk → Almedalen Park

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:Walk along the 3.5km of Bis V, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and watch stained glass at the Gothic Bis V Cathedral, walk around the nordic medieval alleyway and Almedalen Park.
📍Bis V Wall 📍Bis V Cathedral 📍 Old town 📍Almedalen Park

Day 2: History and Culture Experience and Beach Walk

Gortrand Museum → Middle Ages Ruins → Hoglint Observatory → Bis V Beach

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:Learn the trade history of Vikings and Bis Vui at the Gotland Museum, and at the Middle Ages Ruins (St. Nikolai, St. Mary, etc.), the ancient atmosphere experience, the view of the coastal cliff at the Hoglint Observatory
📍Gotland Museum 📍Saint Nikolai Ruins 📍Hogclint Observatory 📍Bis V Beach

Brosbo Nature Protection Zone → Botanical Garden → Medieval Week Experience (Limited in August)

Brosbo Nature Reserve:Walk and bicycle tour along the forest and coastal cliffs
Bis V Botanical Garden:Various roses and Nordic plants

⚠️ Bisquiry Travel Precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Bus Terminal, some of the old towns late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at the bus terminal and tourist attractions, the bag is carried forward and the zipper lock
  • Fake guide:Fake ticket sales attention in tourist destinations
  • Going out late at night:Be careful to go alone after 10 pm old town
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Ferry and aviation strike:Due to the nature of Sweden, traffic strikes can occur, alternative means of transportation
  • Airport-Movement in the city:Airport-Bis V City Bus (6 ~ 10 minutes, 18 ~ 90 SEK)
  • Taxi fee:About 150 ~ 200 SEK from the airport to the city, the meter check is required
  • Train connection:Stockholm (45 minutes airline, 3 hours of ferry)
  • Walking:Bis V is optimal for walking trips, and there are many stone roads and hills.
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 18-25 degrees, long sun, strong sunlight, water intake required
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature 0-5 degrees, cold, cold, winter clothes required
  • Spring/Autumn:A large crossover, preparation of outerwear and umbrella
  • sea breeze:The coastal wind may be low in temperature.
  • ultraviolet rays:It is Nordic, but sunscreen is essential because it is strong in sunlight
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Old town cafe, restaurant price 2-3 times expensive
  • Museum entrance fee:Gortrand Museum 150 SEK, Castle Tour 100 SEK
