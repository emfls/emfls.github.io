⚠️Savha travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Outside the city, the entire night time zone

  • Political instability:Since 2019, due to conflict between political forces, security conditions are unstable and local guides are required
  • Terrorism:The possibility of terrorist organization activities such as ISIS
  • Lack of conflict:Avoid the conflict between the Tuarec and the tribe
  • Prohibition of going out at night:After sunset, it is absolutely prohibited to go out alone
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Recommended for use of 4WD vehicles in the desert area
  • Lack of fuel:Gas station limited and sufficient fuel is required
  • Lost deserts:Prohibition of entry into the desert without GPS and local guides
  • Public transportation:Bus operation irregularities, recommended use of personal vehicles
  • Airport access:640 km from Tripoli, recommended to use flights
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Outdoor activities restricted over 40 degrees and extreme heat
  • Winter (12-2):Daily cross large desert climate, down to 5 degrees at night
  • Sand Storm:Sudden sand storm can occur
  • Dryness:Annual precipitation 8mm, extremely dry climate
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong UV rays, skin protection required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash -oriented:Limited credit card use, enough cash preparation
