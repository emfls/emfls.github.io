⚠️ Nicholstown travel precautions

🚨 Marine safetyism

Main precautions:Strong currents, coral reefs wounds, marine life contact

  • Diving Safety:Be sure to dive with professional guides and prohibit personal diving
  • Blueholeism:Deep depth, strong current, and after confirming swimming skills
  • Coral reef protection:Do not touch coral reefs, protect wound risks and ecosystems
  • Jellyfishism:Immediately first aid when contacting transparent jellyfish
  • Ultravioletism:Caribbean strong ultraviolet rays, underwater burns risk
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Airlines limit:Naso diesel essential, use of small aircraft (large weather influence)
  • Local transportation:Limited to public transportation, mainly using taxis and car rentals
  • Boat Tour:Reservation and cancellation after checking the weather
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, 4WD car recommendation
  • Fuelism:Gas station limited, confirmation of fuel in advance
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Hurricane Season:June-November Hurricane Danger, Check the weather before the trip required
  • rainy season:Preparation of Scoles, Rainfall, and Waterproof Waterproof Supplier in May-October
  • Dry:11-April optimal travel, gentle and dry
  • Temperature:24-32 degrees throughout the year, high humidity
  • wind:It may be low in the streets of trade winds
