⚠️Tambakunda travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the central market, the city center center late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at the central market, bus terminal, packing bags in the future and locking zipper
  • fraud:People who are demanding fake guides or excessive tips
  • Night going out:Prohibited to go alone after 9 pm, recommend group tours
  • Political Assembly:Prohibition of access to local political rally or protests
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Buses and taxis are poor, wearing seat belts
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, deterioration of road conditions during rainy season
  • Taxi fee:No meter, preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Train connection:Takar-Bamako Railway can be used, but delayed frequent
  • Safari Tour:Must be accompanied by professional guides, prohibition of individual behavior
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (11-5):Temperature 25-40 degrees, very dry, enough water intake
  • Rainy (June-October):Temperature 24-35 degrees, high humidity and concentrated heavy rain
  • Harmatan:December-February Sahara Desert sand breeze, respiratory attention
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong, sunscreen required throughout the year
  • malaria:Essential risk of annual occurrence, prevention drugs required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Price inflation of foreigners
  • Safari Tour:One-day tour of $ 50-100, more expensive when including accommodation
  • Tip Culture:Guide $ 10-20/Sun, $ 2-5 hotel staff
