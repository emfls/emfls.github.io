  • Coast:Waves and tidal flat mud to the coast of Gangjin Bay
  • Wildlife:Wildlife, such as snakes and bees in the forest area
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Gangjin city bus dispatch interval is long, checking the timetable in advance
  • Taxi:Due to the distance between tourist destinations, the burden of taxi costs, rental car recommendation
