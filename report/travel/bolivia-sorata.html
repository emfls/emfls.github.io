⚠️Sorata travel precautions

🏔️ altitude disease and healthism

Altitude altitude:Sorata 2,695m, Ilyamani Base Camp 4,200m

  • History symptoms:Headaches, nausea, dizziness, shortness of breath immediately descending
  • Adaptation period:La Pas (3,500m) recommended to visit Sorata after adaptation for at least 2-3 days
  • Moisture intake:About 3-4 liters of water per day required for water intake
  • An alcoholic ban:Drinking in alpine areas causes the worsening of alpine disease
  • Slowly move:Avoiding rapid movement and excessive exercise
🌦️ weather and climate precautions
  • Dry (5-10):20 degrees during the day, -5 degrees at night, 25 degrees or more
  • Woogi (11-4):Afternoon showers, eyes, mud roads
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong UV rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
  • Dryness:Humidity is low, so dehydration and dryness
  • Sudden weather change:Prepared to change rapidly due to mountain climate
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Access from La Pas:3-4 hours by bus, motion sickness on mountain roads
  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, and unable to pass in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Using minibus and trupi, no set schedule
  • Guide required:Ilya Mani trekking must accompany the local guide.
  • Emergency contact network:There are many weak areas of mobile phone signal, emergency plan establishment
💰 Price and living precautions
  • Cash -oriented:Difficult to use cards, enough cash preparation
  • ATM lack:ATM in Sorata very limited
  • drinking water:Purchase of bottled water or integer filter
