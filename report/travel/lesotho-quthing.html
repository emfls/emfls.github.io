⚠️ Cutting precautions

🚨 Safety and security attention

Major areas:Night night city center central district, outskirts of mountainous areas

  • Risk of isolation:Prohibition of trekking alone in the mountainous areas, must be accompanied by local guides
  • Wildlife:Wildlife such as leopards, hyena, etc., refrain from going out at night
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, it is impossible to pass on roads in the rainy season
  • Emergency situation:Lack of medical facilities, transfer to South Africa for emergencies
🌡️ Climate and advanced precautions
  • Altitude disease:More than 3,000m above sea level, enough rest and water intake required
  • Daily crossroads:During 25 degrees during the day and minus 5 degrees at night, required for winter products
  • Woogi (11-3):Heavy rain and hail, risk of landslides
  • Dry (4-10):Dry and dusty, respiratory protection
  • ultraviolet rays:Highlands strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Most of them are dirty roads, 4WD vehicles required
  • Public transportation:Use a minibus taxi, check the seat belt wearing
  • fuel:Gas station spacing, secure enough fuel
  • communication:Signal weakness
  • Border pass:Check the operation time of border checkpoint with South Africa
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Risk of malaria, preventive drugs
  • drinking water:Only drink bottled water or boiled water, purified water purification
  • food:Only eat enough food
  • Medical facilities:Restrictions on the couture area medical facilities, emergency medicine preparation
