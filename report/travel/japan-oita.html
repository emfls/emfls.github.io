⚠️ Oita travel precautions

♨️ Precautions when using hot springs

Hot Spring etiquette:Since Oita is a hot spring area, it is good to know some rules if you plan to use a hot spring while traveling.

  • Before bathing:Wash your body thoroughly before entering the bath, tie the towel not to take it into the bath, tie your hair from touching the water or use a shower cap
  • Quietly use:Use quietly in hot springs and not talk loudly
  • Tattoo limit:If you have a tattoo, there is a hot spring facility that is limited to admission.
  • Family bath:Family bath is relatively free and good to use with children
🚗 Transportation planning precautions
  • Recommended car rental:Major tourist attractions such as Beppu and Yufuin are relatively good access, but in other areas, public transportation is rare, so it is convenient to use car rental.
  • International driver's license:International driver's license required when using a car rental
  • Bus schedule:Beppu-Yufuin bus/train schedule check in advance when using public transportation
  • Using a bus pass:Beppu downtown uses bus passes like 'My べ ぷ ぷ ぷ'
🏨 Precautions when booking
  • Reservation in advance:Yufuin and Beppu are popular tourist destinations, so there may be no rooms in the peak season (cherry blossom iron, autumn season, holiday, etc.) a month ago.
