🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever

  • malaria:Constant use of risk areas of the year, required to take preventive drugs, and regular use of mosquito repellents
  • yellow fever:Required vaccination, checking certificate of inoculation when entering Korea
  • drinking water:Only drinking water, avoid ice, fruits peel off
  • Wildlife:Maintain safe distances when encountering dangerous animals such as elephants and buffalo
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, unable to pass in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Restricted, recommended to use taxi or car rental
  • fuel:Gas station spacing, securing enough fuel before travel
  • GPS:Signal instability, offline map download required
  • Night driving:Avoiding as much as possible, lack of road lighting
