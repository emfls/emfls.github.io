⚠️ Altai travel precautions

🏔️ altitude and healthism

Main danger altitude:Area 3,000m above sea level

  • Altitude symptoms:Headaches, nausea, dizziness, shortness of breath immediately descending
  • Eat enough water:More than 3-4 liters a day, ban on alcohol and caffeine
  • Slowly climbing:Prohibition of rapid high altitude, enough rest
  • Blood pressure attention:Patients with hypertension are required for pre -doctor consultation
  • Preparation for emergency situations:Preparation of satellite telephone or emergency signal
🌡️ Extreme climate precautions
  • Daily crossroads:Even in summer, it can fall below freezing at night.
  • gale:Strong winds of the Altai Mountains, tent fixing required
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, SPF 50+ sunscreen required
  • Sudden weather change:Hail, blizzard
  • Dryness:Extremely dry air, moisturizers and lip balm
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Domestic flights:Aero Mongolia alone, only three times a week
  • Unpaved road:4WD vehicle required, preparation of motion sickness
  • Lack of fuel:The spacing of the gas station is so far that it is important to secure fuel
  • GPS limit:Satellite signal instability, compass and maps required
  • Vehicle failure:Preparation of spare parts because there are few maintenance centers
