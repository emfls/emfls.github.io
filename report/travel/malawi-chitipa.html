⚠️ Chitipa travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Mosquito prevention required, prevention drugs, long -sleeved clothes, insect repellent
  • drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • hygiene:Washing hands often, street food attention, avoiding raw
  • Emergency situation:Local hospital location in advance, preparation for emergency contact information
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, deterioration of road conditions during rainy season
  • Public transportation:Overcrowition, seat belt confirmation when using minibus
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, local driving habits
  • Night movement:Late time moving late at night, lack of lighting
  • Border pass:Thoroughly prepared documents when passing the tanzania border
