⚠️ Langbarene travel precautions

Main dangerous diseases:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, sleep disease

  • malaria:The most dangerous disease required to take prevention drugs, regular use of mosquito repellents
  • yellow fever:Request for vaccination, checking certificate of vaccination when entering Korea
  • Water quality:Drink only bottled water and prohibit ice intake, avoid raw food
  • Wound management:Small wounds are immediately disinfected and risk of infection
