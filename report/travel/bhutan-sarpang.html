🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Near the Indian border, around the night market market

  • Border area:Near the Indian border, ID card is always possessed, photographic attention
  • Wildlife:Personality of wild animals such as bears and leopards in the forest area
  • Guide accompanying:Restrictions on foreigners alone, must be accompanied by certified guides
  • Going out late at night:After sunset, you are alone, only near the accommodation
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:As a mountainous terrain, the road is steep and winding, preparing for motion sickness
  • Public transportation:Bus operation irregularities, preliminary time confirmation required
  • Taxi:No meter, preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Road closed:The possibility of closing the road as a landslide in the rainy season
  • Lack of fuel:There are few gas stations, so fuel shortages can occur
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Monsoon period (June-September):Intensive heavy rains and risks of landslides
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature 10-20 degrees, large and dry daily
  • Summer (March-May):Temperature 20-30 degrees, moist and hot
  • Altitude difference:200-1500m above sea level, large difference in temperature according to altitude
  • ultraviolet rays:Highlands strong sunlight, sunscreen required
