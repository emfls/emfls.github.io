⚠️Samura travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Train station, crowded market, late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing in the mayor, public transportation, and tourist attractions
  • Taxi/Rikisha:Official app (Uber, OLA) or hotel recommended vehicle, check the meter
  • Fake guide/tour:Recommended for purchasing on -site rights by using only certified guides
  • Going out late at night:Late time alone, restricting up alone, moving to bright places
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Traffic jams:Most commute time congestion, move time to spare time
  • Public transportation:Taxi/Rikisha recommended rather than bus
  • Train connection:Connection with major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, and Kolata, and pre -booking required
  • airplane:It takes about 30-40 minutes to taxi from Doomna Airport to downtown
  • Taxi fee:5km about 180inr, 10km about 225inr, 30km about 400inr
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (April-June):30-45 degrees of temperature, very hot, dry, water intake required
  • Woogi (July-September):Rain and humidity, umbrella and waterproof shoes preparation
  • Winter (11-2):10-25 degrees, morning evening chrysanthemum, outerwear preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:Sunlight strong, sunscreen and hat required
💰 Price and other precautions
  • Tourist destination:Marvel Rocks, Shops and restaurants around Dar Falls 2-3 times expensive
  • entrance fee:Main attraction 50 ~ 300INR, boat and cable car separately charges
  • Tip Culture:5-10%restaurant, hotel housekeeping 20-50inr
