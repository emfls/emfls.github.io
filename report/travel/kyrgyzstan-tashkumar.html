altitude:3,000-4,000m altitude

  • History symptoms:Headaches, nausea, and dizziness immediately descend

  • No drinking:Alcohol intake in alpine areas worsened alpine disease
  • Slowly move:Avoid rapid high altitude and gradual adaptation
