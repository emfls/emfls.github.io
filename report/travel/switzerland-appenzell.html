📅 Appenzel 2 nights 3 days travel course

Day 1: Appenzel Village and Traditional Experience

Appenzel Village Walk → Appenzel Museum → Lanzugama, Plats → Traditional Bakery Visit

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:Walk the streets decorated with wooden buildings and frescoes with a living Swiss tradition, experience the history and culture of the locals at the Appenzel Museum, and feel the traditional democratic culture at Plats (Outdoor Rally Square). You can taste Appenzeler Viberi (Swiss Ginger Bread) and local specialties in a traditional bakery.

Day 2: Alps Observation and Cheese Experience

Eban Alp Trekking → Hohe Casten Observation Deck → Appenzeler Cheese Factory Tour

Recommended time:10 hours
Core points:Enjoy the scenery of the Alps at Ebben Alp (Cable Car Movable) and enjoy the 360 -degree panoramic view at Hohe Casten (Rotary Restaurant) Observation Deck. You can visit and experience the cheese production process at the Appenzeler Cheese Factory (Shawaserai).
📍Ebulp 📍Hohe Casten 📍Apenceler Cheese Factory

Day 3: Natural Experience of Naturally (Optional)

Brulisa → Alpstein Mountain Hiking → Swiss Farm Experience

Brulisa:Ride the cable car and enter the Alpstein Mountain Zone, Hiking and Scenic views
Alpstein:Hiking course, lake and neck scenery where you can feel the essence of the Swiss Alps
Farm experience:Fresh milk, cheese, and traditional agricultural experience on local farms
