⚠️ Denver travel precautions

🏔️A altitude disease and highland

Altitude altitude:Denver is located at 5,280 feet above sea level (1,609m)

  • History symptoms:Headaches, nausea, dizziness, sleep disorders, and fatigue may occur
  • Incidence:In Denver, 8-10%of visitors experience altitude disease, and when they go up to the mountain, they increase to 25-30%.
  • preventive:Drink a lot of water for a day after arrival and avoid intense activity and excessive drinking
  • How to deal with:Sufficient rest and moisture intake, severe medical staff counseling

  • Strong ultraviolet light:It is easy to ride in the sun in winter due to the pale air of the highlands, so sunscreen required
