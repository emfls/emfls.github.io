⚠️Mari Paula Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, Zika virus

  • malaria:Start taking prevention drugs two weeks before departure, mosquito nets required
  • yellow fever:Implied vaccination before entering the country, bringing in a certificate of inoculation
  • insect bite:Powerful insect repellents, long -sleeved long pants
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, use integer tablets
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Accessibility:Only access to airplanes or Maroni river boats
  • flight:45 minutes from Cayen, canceling according to the weather
  • Kang Transportation:The possibility of stopping operations according to the Maroni River level
  • road name:Unpaved road construction until Papi City Tong
  • fuel:Local fuel supply instability, requiring enough preparation
