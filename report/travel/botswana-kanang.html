⚠️ Kanang travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever

  • malaria:Essential to take preventive drugs as a mosquito -mediated disease, wear long -sleeved clothes and insect repellent
  • yellow fever:Implied vaccination before entering the country, bringing in a certificate of inoculation
  • Wildlife:Dangerous wildlife such as lions and hyenas, strict guide instructions
  • Snakeism:Viper habitat, worn safari boots and pool forestism
  • Emergency situation:Lack of medical facilities
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Extreme heat:Due to a daytime temperature of 40 degrees or more, enough water intake and rest in the shade
  • Daily crossroads:Daily and night temperature differences of 20 degrees or more, preparation
  • Dryness:The humidity is also very low, moisturizers and lip balm required
  • Sand Storm:Sudden sand storms can occur, mask and goggles prepare
  • ultraviolet rays:Applying burns with strong ultraviolet rays and sunscreen from time to time
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • 4WD required:Unable to operate a regular vehicle due to the desert terrain, use a 4WD car rental or tour
  • Water search:GPS signal instability, compass and map preparation
  • fuel:Gas station interval is far away and extra fuel preparation
  • tire:Preparation of punk risks due to sand and thorns, spare tires and repair tools
  • Driver's license:International driver's license required, local traffic laws
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:Very expensive tourist attractions, enough budget preparation
  • Safari Tour:1 day tour $ 200-500, more expensive when including accommodation
  • Fuel cost:Fuel price is high and fuel cost is paid due to long distance travel
  • Tip Culture:Tips for $ 20-50 per day for guides and drivers
