⚠️ Welchman Hall Travel Precautions

🚨 Safety and wildlife care

Main precautions:Wild Monkey contact, tropical rainforest safety, cave exploration

  • Monkey Observation:Do not feed the wild monkeys directly, bag or food storage attention
  • Tropical rainforest:Sleeping walking attention, proper shoes must be worn
  • Cave exploration:Restrictions on shooting inside Harrison Cave, observing guide instructions
  • Zip Line Safety:Confirmation of weight restrictions, required to wear safety equipment
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car use:Barbados is required for left -handed and international driver's license
  • Taxi fee:About 40 Barbados Dollar from Bridge Town to Welchman Hall
  • Tour bus:Time observance when using hotel pickup service
  • Public transportation:Preparation of accurate charges when using local buses
  • Road situation:Possibility of flooding of roads in the rainy season
