🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Near the Syrian border, late at night, remote mountainous areas

  • Political anxiety:Avoid demonstrations or assembly areas due to changes in political situations in Lebanon
  • Religious conflict:It is essential to grasp the local situation when moving between Christianity and Islamic regions
  • Mountain area:Recommended for local guides when trekking alone in mountainous areas
  • Going out late at night:Even in downtown Ekden, be careful to go alone after 10 pm
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Lack of public transportation:Ekden is limited to public transportation, rental car or taxi
  • Mountain road:Observed mountain roads, preparation for motion sickness and safe driving required
  • Taxi fee:About $ 50-70 from Beirut to Ekden, preliminary fee negotiations
  • Rent -a -car:Recommended for international driver's license, mountain road driving experience recommendation
  • fuel:Limited to gas stations in mountainous areas, and fully prepared

  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 25-35 degrees, very hot, dry, enough water intake
