⚠️ Artemida travel precautions

🌡️ Mediterranean climate precautions

Summer:The temperature from June-September is high and dry, so enough water intake and UV protection required

  • Prevention of heat stroke:Outdoor activity restraint between 12 pm and 4 pm, rest in the shade
  • Dry climate:Low humidity, risk of dehydration, enough water
  • UV protection:Use high SPF sunscreen, hats and sunglasses are required
  • Winter:December-February is gentle, but it rains a lot of umbrella required
🏄 Marine sports safety precautions
  • Water Sports:Surf, windsurfing, kite surfing safety equipment required
  • Beginner education:Beginners must be active with professional instructors
  • Check the weather:Check the weather and sea status before marine sports
  • Life jacket:Wearing life jackets for all water activities
🏖️ Beach safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:Check the waves and algae status when swimming on the beach
  • UV protection:Seon cream frequently applied when exposed to the beach for a long time on the beach
  • Personal items:Pay attention to the management of valuables on the beach
  • Snorkeling:Maintain safety distance and protect marine life during snorkeling
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Airport access:A convenient approach to Athens Airport (about 30km)
  • Rent -a -car:Recommended for using car rentals for local exploration
  • Public transportation:Check the schedule in advance when using the bus line
  • Yacht Tour:Recommended for pre -booking tours departing from Laut City Marina
🍽️ Food and drinking water safety

Artemida's food is generally safe, but attention is needed to prevent upset stomach while traveling.
