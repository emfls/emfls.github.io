⚠️Tebes travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists, including the Tebeth Train Station and Archaeological Museum

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Conscious attention:When walking on the road or taking pictures,
  • Bag management:Put the bag in front and lock the zipper
  • Tourististism:Especially in famous tourist destinations and public transportation
🎫 Preliminary reservation required
  • Tebes Archaeological Museum:In the peak season, the waiting time may be longer, so pre -online reservation is recommended.
  • Delphi linked tour:The day tour from Tevez to Delphoy is required for advance reservations.
  • Winery Tour:Local winery field trips and tasting programs are required for advance reservations.
  • Cooking experience:Cooking workshops in local homes need to make a reservation at least two days ago.
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Athens connection:Tevez is 1 hour and 30 minutes by bus from Athens, 1 hour by train
  • Tebes-Delphi:It is necessary to check the timetable because the bus is about 2 hours away from the bus, and the dispatch interval is long.
  • Recommended for using car rentals:Rent -a -cars are the most convenient for surrounding ruins and natural attractions.
  • Taxi:Check the meter when using taxi from downtown Tebes to major tourist attractions
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Mediterranean Climate:The Mediterranean Climate, which is mild in winter and very hot in summer
  • Summer preparation:In summer (June-August), it is very hot, so sunscreen, hat, enough water required
  • Winter preparation:Winter is gentle, but it can rain, so prepare an umbrella
  • Ruins: Ruins:We recommend arrival at 9 am and avoid strong sunlight and watch pleasant viewing
