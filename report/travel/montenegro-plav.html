⚠️Plave travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Near the Albanian border, late at night, mountainous areas

  • Border area:Near the albanian border, ID card is always possessed, illegal menstrualism
  • Mountain safety:Hiking alone, Hassan immediately when the weather changes rapidly
  • Wildlife:Bear and wolves, food sealing is required
  • Going out late at night:Due to the nature of mountain villages, refrain from going out after 9 pm
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation limit:Plaves are restricted by public transportation, rental cars or tours recommended
  • Mountain road:Need to experience the winding mountain trails, steep slopes, and driving experience
  • Fuel supply:There is a rare gas station, so it is required to secure fuel margin
  • Fordori tea connection:About 2 hours and 30 minutes to the water, check the road status
  • Winter traffic:The possibility of closing roads due to snow in December-March
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature of 15-25 degrees, afternoon showers frequently, umbrella required
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature -5-5 degrees, heavy snow and strong winds, and winter products required
  • Spring/Autumn:15 degrees or more, morning and evening very cold
  • Mountain climate:Rapid temperature change
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong UV rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash payment:Most of them are limited to cash payment, card use
  • Activity cost:Rafting 50-80 euros, hiking guide 30-50 euros
  • Accommodation:Hut and Guest House 20-60 euro
  • Food expenses:Traditional restaurant 15-30 euros, cheap local ingredients
