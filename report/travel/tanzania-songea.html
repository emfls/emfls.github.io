🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Essential prevention medications, mosquito repellents are always used, long -sleeved clothes
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, peel off fruits

  • Emergency situation:Local hospital location in advance, emergency contact storage
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, and the road condition is very deteriorated during the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Careful pickpockets when using Dala Dala (minibus)
  • flight:DarS Salam required, domestic flight delays
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, accompanied by experienced local driving experiences
  • Night movement:Remove moving after 10 pm, risk of wild animals
