⚠️ Jacutome travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the market, late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at the central market, bus stop, packing bags in the future and locking zipper
  • Motorcycle Taxi:Gemijang (motorcycle taxi) required to wear helmets, prior negotiations
  • Fake guide:Watch out for self -proclaimed guides in tourist attractions and use only official guides
  • The night shade goes out:Don't go alone after sunset, move to group
  • Photo shooting:Request for permission before taking a photo at the voodoo consciousness or the temple
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Buses and minibuses may be waiting because there are no fixed schedules.
  • Road status:There are many dirt roads and mud roads in the rainy season
  • Taxi fee:Prior negotiations are required because there is no fare meter
  • Kotonu connection:About 2 hours from Jakutome to Kotonu, bus or taxi
  • Lack of fuel:Sometimes it can be stopped by means of transportation due to lack of fuel
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (11-3):25-35 degrees of temperature, dry and hot, enough water intake
  • Rainy (4-10):Temperature 22-30 degrees, humid and scores rainfall frequent
  • Harmatan:Dry breeze from the Sahara Desert in December-February
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
  • humidity:Difficulties in dry clothing with high humidity year -round
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:High risk of developing year -round, prevention drugs and mosquito prevention required
  • drinking water:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • food:Street foodism, ingest only enough cooked food
  • Medical facilities:Insurance required for local medical facilities limited and emergency situations
  • yellow fever:Request for vaccination, checking certificate of vaccination when entering Korea
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Price inflation attention for foreigners, accompanied by locals
  • Market bargaining:In the traditional market, bargaining starts with 30-50% of the prices of presentation.
  • Tip Culture:Provide small tips if you have a guide or help
  • Exchange:Official currency exchange, avoiding street currency exchange
