⚠️Sapporo travel precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Optimal travel period:This is the best month to travel to Sapporo from June to September

  • Summer:It is comfortable because it is less humidity than Korea, but sometimes it can be hot
  • Winter:It gets colder and it can come to snow, so warm clothes and waterproof boots are required
  • Weather fluctuations:Sapporo is that the weather changes big.
  • Battery consumption:Because of the cold temperature, the cell phone battery is worn out very quickly
❄️ Winter safety precautions
  • Ice Gil:It is frozen and melted, and it becomes an ice, so you have to walk like a penguin.
  • Check shoes:Make sure that the outsole has not worn out, the old boots are risk of separation of the sole separation.
  • Prohibited hand pockets:On the ice road, do not put your hands in your pocket and wear gloves
  • Snow accumulates:Note that the snow accumulates thickly on the umbrella
🦝 Wildlife precautions
  • Raccoon and fox:You can meet raccoons and foxes at night in the city park.
  • Prohibition of contact:Ekinokocus (ponchocic) has parasites, so you should never touch it.
  • cure:If you are infected, there is only a method of resection with surgical surgery, so prevention is best.
  • Prohibition of feeding:Do not feed even if you come with you
🍽️ Food and cultural precautions
  • Cash use:In Japan, which still mainly uses cash, coin wallet is required.
  • Tip Culture:Japan has no tip culture, so you don't have to give a tip.
  • Meal etiquette:How to use chopsticks and eat without sound
  • Food characteristics:Japanese food can be sweet and salty with soy sauce and sugar base
🚇 Precautions when using transportation
  • Transportation etiquette:Avoid telephone in silent mode and long calls
  • punctuality:In Japan, the time is very important, so compliance with the appointment time
  • Food in the subway:It is polite not to eat or drink in the subway.
  • Luggage management:If you move by train, the luggage left for a while may soon disappear.
🏨 Precautions for accommodation
