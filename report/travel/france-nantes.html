⚠️ Nantes Travel Precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Around the island of the machine, Nantes Central Station, Tram stop, and tourist attractions

  • Tourist Target:Pickpocketing in a bag or pocket when focusing on photography in a tourist destination
  • When using public transportation:Try to pick up a crowded gap when boarding trams or buses
  • Market area:Risk of pickpockets when distributed during shopping during the Talang Market, etc.
  • How to deal with:The bag is carried in the future, the valuables are distributed, and the cash is at least only
🚖 Traffic use precautions
  • Recommended for purchasing trams and bus passes in advance (1 day or 3 days pass)
  • Only official taxi or Uber app when using taxi
  • Wearing helmets and compliance with traffic rules when bicycle rental
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • May-September is required for optimal travel or temperature change
  • Sudden rain possibility due to the influence of the Atlantic Ocean
  • Even in summer, it can be chilly in the evening, so long clothes are prepared
🚫 Time to avoid

Winter (December-February) is inadequate for tourism due to the cold weather and short sunshine time
