⚠️Ebeye travel precautions

🌊 Natural disasters and climateism

Main risk factors:Typhoon, sea level rise, scall, strong ultraviolet light

  • Typhoon season:June-November typhoonism, weather information occasional confirmation required
  • Sea level rise:In case of high tide, it can be flooded in some areas, and the 2020s breakwater construction is underway.
  • Scol:Sudden heavy rains and strong winds, raincoats and waterproof products
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays near the equator, SPF 50+ sunscreen occasionally apply
🏥 Medical and safety precautions
  • Medical facilities:Only basic medical facilities, Hawaiian or Guam transfer when severe diseases
  • drinking water:Recommended bottled water, avoid drinking tap water directly
  • mosquito:Mosquito diseaseism, such as dengue fever, required to be avoided
  • Marine safety:Snorkel protection of coral reefs, toxic marine lifeism
  • Emergency situation:Ebeye Emergency Operation Center Contact contact information in advance
🚢 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • flight:Reservation in advance because it is required to face diesel and flights limited
  • Boat movement:The possibility of stopping the flight according to the weather, required to be relaxed
  • bicycle:Encourage of major transportation in the island, wearing helmet
  • Walking:32 hectares small islands, most of them can be moved by walking
  • Quarzalain Base:Restrictions on access to US military bases, prohibiting access without permission
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:2-3 times expensive compared to Korea due to dependence on imports of goods
  • Limited products:Fresh fruits, difficulty finding vegetables
  • delivery fee:Mail delivery up to 2 months
  • Cash -oriented:Card payment limit, enough cash preparation
