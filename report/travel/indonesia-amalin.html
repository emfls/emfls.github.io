⚠️ Amalin travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Late at night, beach, rare trekking courses

  • Marine safety:Encourage activity with birdism and local guides during snorkeling
  • Motorcycle accident:Wearing helmets when driving scooters
  • pickpocket:Put in front of a bag at the market or tourist attractions and lock the zipper
  • Night activity:Be careful to go alone at night
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Scooter Rent:International driver's license required, driving after familiarity with traffic laws
  • Taxi:Meteren check or preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Boat Tour:Reservation after checking the weather, required to wear life jackets
  • Public transportation:Local bus timetable irregularities, securing free time
  • Road situation:Possibility of flooding roads during the rainy season, checking bypass route
