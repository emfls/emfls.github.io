⚠️ Kyoto travel precautions

🚌 Precautions on transportation

Transportation convenience:Kyoto consists of a bus -centered public transportation system, so it is important to be familiar with how to use the bus.

  • Kyoto City Bus 1 Day:It is 600 yen for unlimited use of city buses all day long, so it is economical for tourism.
  • Bus congestion time:8-9 am and 5-7 pm is very crowded with commute time, so it is good to avoid it.
  • Back Gate Ride:Kyoto city buses are ride back to the back gate, get off and get off and pay the fee when getting off.
  • Tourist destination congestion:It is good to visit popular tourist attractions such as Fushimi Inari and Kiyomizuera early in the morning.
🏨 Precautions when booking
  • Reservation in advance:Kyoto Station, Temperature, and Arashiyama are very popular, so there may be no rooms in the peak season 2-3 months ago.
