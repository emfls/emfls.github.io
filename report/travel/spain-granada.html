⚠️ Granada travel precautions

🚨 General safety precautions
  • Pickpocket attention:Thorough management of bags and belongings at Albaiin and Sakromonte tourist attractions
  • Hill Road Safety:Note on the Albaixin Area Stone Road and Line Photo Road
  • Night activity:Sacromonte area, don't go alone, use group tours
  • Storage of valuables:Use a large bag storage box when watching the Alhambra Palace
🚗 Traffic safety precautions
  • Rent -a -car use:Narrow alleys, parking spaces restrictions
  • Public transportation:Check the bus schedule in advance, use Granada traffic cards
  • Taxi:Only official taxis are used, checking the operation of the meter
  • Walking:Wear appropriate shoes on the stone roads of Albaisin and Sakromonte area
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Summer heat:Over 35 degrees in July-August, enough water intake and shade rest
  • Winter cold:In December-February, the Sierra Nevada Mountains Prepared for chilly, warm clothes
  • UV protection:Strong ultraviolet, sunscreen and hat required
  • Siesta time:2-5 pm, closed shops and museums, considering the schedule
🗣️ Cultural precautions
  • mealtime:2-4 o'clock, evening at 9-11 o'clock in lunch
  • Dress Regulations:Wearing clothes covered with shoulders and knees when visiting Granada Cathedral and Royal Chapel
  • Photo shooting:Prohibition of shooting some zones inside the Alhambra Palace, no use of flash
  • Flamenco Show:Prohibition of photography and noise during the performance
  • Tip Culture:5-10% tips in the restaurant, degree of change from Tapas bar

💡 Granada Travel Tips

  • Alhambra Palace Reservation:At least 2-3 months ago, online pre-booking required, almost impossible to enter the day
  • Use Granada Card:Entrance to major tourist attractions and tourist passes containing public transportation
  • Tapas Culture:Saving food costs with free tapas provided when ordering drinks
  • Mirador San Nicholas:When you visit at sunset, you can enjoy the golden night view of the Alhambra Palace
  • Optimal travel period:The best in terms of April-May and September-October is the weather and the number of tourists
  • Flamenco show reservation:Visit with dinner at Sakromonte Cave Restaurant with dinner
  • Albaizin Tour:If you visit early in the morning, there are few tourists and relaxed photographs
  • Local language:Basic Spanish greetings and thanksgiving expressions
