⚠️Gorde travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:All areas around the market, outskirts, late at night

  • Political instability:Somali is a politically sensitive area, so it is essential to grasp the local situation.
  • Foreign targets:The possibility of crime for foreigners, always accompanied by local guides
  • Prohibition of going out at night:After the sun, never go out alone
  • Valuable goods management:Passports and cash are stored in hotel safe
  • Photo shooting:Military facilities, absolute prohibition of shooting government buildings
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, and the presence of intervals that cannot be passed in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Regular bus operation is limited, preliminary reservation required
  • flight:Use domestic flights from Adis Ababa to Gorde, and canceled due to weather
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, accompanied by local drivers
  • fuel:Gas station is limited, so securing sufficient fuel
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (10-5):Temperature 25-35 degrees, very dry, hot, enough water intake
  • Woogi (June-September):Risk of floods due to temperature 20-30 degrees, concentrated heavy rains
  • Daily crossroads:The temperature drops significantly at night, so it is required
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
  • dust:In the dry season, severe dust, mask recommendation
🏥 Health -related precautions
