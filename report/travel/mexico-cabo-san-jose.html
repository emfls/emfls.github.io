⚠️ Cabosan Jose Travel Precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Outside the central area, late beaches, rare alleys

  • pickpocket:Pickpocket attention at the airport, bus terminal, and tourist attractions
  • Taxi/Transportation:Non -recommended taxis without meters recommend, hotel pickup/official shuttle recommended
  • Fake guide/ticket:Unofficial guide in tourist attractions, ticket seller attention
  • Going out late at night:Be careful to go alone after 10 pm beaches and outskirts
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Airport movement:Airport pickup/shuttle pre -booking required, taxi fare preliminary confirmation
  • Public transportation:Recommended for bus routes, rental cars or tours
  • Road situation:Traffic lights/pedestrian crossings
  • Beach approach:All beaches are not possible, so check whether they can be obtained
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-September):Temperature 28-36 degrees, very hot, dry, ultraviolet rays, water intake required
  • Winter (12-2):Green, mild, morning/evening chilly
  • Spring/Autumn:A large, light outerwear preparation
  • Hurricane Season:August-October, checking weather information
  • ultraviolet rays:Sunlight is very strong, sunscreen/hat required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Marina, restaurant around the beach/bar price 2-3 times expensive
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 10 ~ 15%, bar $ 1-2, hotel housekeeping $ 2-3
  • Seafood dishes:Fresh seafood prices are high
  • souvenir:Purchase after price comparison at local market/supermarket
