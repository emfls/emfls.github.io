🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Spring (March -May):8 ~ 15 ° C, tulip season, rain and windy
  • Summer (June -August):15 ~ 25 ° C, the festival and outdoor activities optimal, frequent showers
  • Autumn (September -November):10 ~ 18 ° C, autumn leaves and canal scenery, raining often
  • Winter (December -February):0 ~ 7 ° C, cold and humid, warm clothes required
  • Umbrella and waterproof jacket:Recommended for bringing all year round
