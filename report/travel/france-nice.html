⚠️ Nice travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:British promenade, Nice station, old town, beach, public transportation

  • Beach pickpocket:Personal belongings while swimming or sunbathing on the beach
  • Public transportation:When using a tram or bus, carry a bag forward and lock the zipper
  • Tourist destination:Pay attention to the surrounding situation when focusing on photography
  • How to deal with:Valuits are also carried by hotel safe, minimal cash and cards.
🌊 Beach safety precautions
  • Aqua shoes required because it is a gravel beach
  • Attention at swimming because the current may be strong
  • Use sufficient sunscreen when sunbathing
  • Do not leave personal belongings on the beach
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • It is very hot and dry in summer due to the Mediterranean climate
  • Eat sufficient water to prevent dehydration
  • Wearing sunglasses and hats because the direct sunlight is strong
  • In the evening, the temperature goes down, so prepare a light outerwear
🚖 Traffic use precautions
  • Trams and buses can be transferred within 74 minutes
  • Only official taxis when using taxi
  • Check the parking space in advance when using the car rental
  • Recommended for purchasing transportation rights when using public transportation
