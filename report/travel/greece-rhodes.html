📅 Rodos 2 nights 3 days travel course

Day 1: Examination of the old town and the Middle Ages

Rhodes Old Town → Knights 'Palace → Knights' Street → Mandraki Port

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:Explore traces of the 14th century Crusaders from Rodos Old Town, one of the world's best -preserved medieval cities. In this ancient city, designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, see the walls and stone buildings of the Middle Ages perfectly and experience the glorious history of Rhodes Knights at the Knights' Palace. At the Mandraki Port, check out the place where there was a statue of Rodos Giant, one of the seven wonders of the ancient world.

Day 2: Lindos Acropolis and Beach Tour

Lindos Village → Lindos Acropolis → St. Paul Bay → Lindos Beach → Traditional Greece Taberna

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:Watch Lindos Acropolis, the most beautiful ancient ruins on Rodos Island, and view the magnificent architecture of the Athena Rindia Temple and the Hellenistic era. In the ancient fortress, located on the cliff of 116 meters high, enjoy the fantastic view of Lindos Village, which consists of the blue sea and white houses, and experience the beautiful landscape of the apostle Paul in St. Paul Bay. Enjoy the Mediterranean Romantic on the Beach of Lindos, which combines clear sea and golden sandy beaches.

⚠️Rodos travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists such as Rhodes Old Town and Mandraki Port

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Conscious attention:When walking on the road or taking pictures,
  • Bag management:Put the bag in front and lock the zipper
  • Tourististism:Especially in famous tourist destinations and public transportation
🎫 Preliminary reservation required
  • Lindos Acropolis:In the peak season, the waiting time may be longer, so pre -online reservation is recommended.
  • Butterfly Valley:In the butterfly observation season (June-September), there are many visitors, so early visit
  • Rhodes Old Town Guide Tour:Korean guide tours are required for advance reservations
  • Beach activity:Water sports or boat tours can be canceled depending on the weather, so secure the schedule
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Athens connection:Rhodes is 1 hour 20 minutes by flight from Athens and 12-15 hours in Perry
  • Rhodes-Lindos:It is necessary to check the timetable because the bus is long by bus and the interval between the train is long.
  • Recommended for using car rentals:Rent -a -cars are the most convenient for the entire island.
  • Taxi:About 25 euros from Rhodes Airport to downtown and about 60 euros from Lindos
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Mediterranean Climate:The Mediterranean Climate, which is mild in winter and very hot in summer
  • Summer preparation:In summer (July-August), it is very hot, so sunscreen, hat, enough water required
  • Winter preparation:Winter is gentle, but it can rain, so prepare an umbrella
  • Windism:Preparation of windproof jackets because the Meltemi winds of the sun can be strong
🗣️ Language communication

Although Greek is official language, English can be communicated in tourist attractions. Knowing the basic Greek greetings will help you communicate with locals.
