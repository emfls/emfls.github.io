⚠️Ustiric travel precautions

🚨 Note that safety and emergency situations

Main precautions:Lack of medical facilities, communication restrictions, weather changes

  • Medical facilities:Emergency medical facilities are very limited and required to be transported in case of serious emergency situations
  • communication:Unstable on the Internet and telephone connections, relying on satellite communication
  • weather:Sudden scalls, strong windism, avoiding typhoon season
  • Risk of isolation:It may be isolated on the island for several days when the weather deteriorates
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • flight:Move from Maju -ro to a small aircraft, and canceled according to the weather
  • Boat movement:Movement interstee is required to use small boats, waves and weather conditions.
  • Reservation confirmation:All transportation pre -booking required, almost impossible to book the day
  • Luggage limit:Small aircraft weight limit strict, recommended less than 15kg
  • waiting:Waiting for a long time when delayed due to weather
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (5-November):Strong scalls and high humidity, waterproof equipment required
  • Dry (12-4):Relatively dry but still wet
  • Typhoon season:Typhoon risks in August-October
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays near the equator, sunscreen occasionally
  • Sea level rise:In the case of high tide, it is possible to flood the area
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:Depending on the import of all goods, 2-3 times more expensive than Korea
  • Cash payment:Limited credit card use, enough cash preparation
  • foodstuffs:Fresh food is very expensive, canned food
  • Fuel cost:Boat tour costs are high
