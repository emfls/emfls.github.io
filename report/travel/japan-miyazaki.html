⚠️ Miyazaki travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Nissettachi (Nishitachi) and secluded outskirts, late at night

  • pickpocket:In tourist attractions, stations, and downtowns, the bag is carried forward and the zipper lock
  • Outer movement:It is recommended to use taxi and rental cars when returning home due to rare public transportation
  • Hot Spring etiquette:Tattoo (tattoo) access restrictions, required for shower before bathing
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The interval between bus and train is long, so it is required to check the timetable.
  • Left traffic:Japan's left -side traffic, attention to driving
  • highway:Check whether ETC cards (high pass)
  • Transportation Pass:JR Kyushu Rail Pass, SunQ Bus Pass, etc.
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June -August):Up to 32 degrees, high humidity, sunscreen required
  • Winter (December -February):At least 2-3 degrees, the coast is mild, and the mountain is chilly
  • Spring/Autumn:A large number of days, cherry blossoms and autumn leaves season popular
  • Rainy and typhoon:Umbrella/Woobi preparation for rain and strong winds from August to July, August ~ September
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Café and restaurant prices around major attractions
  • Hot Springs admission:500 ~ 1,500 yen (Award for each facility)
  • Tip Culture:Japan has no tip culture, pay only when calculating
  • Cash use:Small restaurants and hot springs
