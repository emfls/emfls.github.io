⚠️ Adapoa travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, bowl

  • malaria:Essential to take preventive drugs as mosquito -mediated diseases, wear long -sleeved clothes and mosquito repellents
  • yellow fever:Essential vaccination before entering the country, portable certificate of vaccination
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, raw fruit peel
  • Beach safety:Strong birdism, prohibition of swimming without a local guide
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Trotro:12 -seater minibuses by local public transportation, no stop stop
  • taxi:If there is no meter, the preliminary fee negotiations are required
  • Road situation:There are many dirt roads and worsening road conditions in rainy season
  • drive:Right traffic, international driver's license required
  • Boat Tour:Wearing life jackets, using the weather after checking the weather
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (11-3):Temperature 24-32 degrees, dry and hot, Harmathan
  • Rainy (4-10):The temperature is 22-28 degrees, wet and rainy often
  • Daily crossroads:At night, the temperature may drop, so you need a light coat
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong sunlight, sunscreen must be near the equator
  • humidity:Wearing well -ventilated clothes with high humidity throughout the year
💰 Price related precautions
  • Local prices:Tourist destinations are 2-3 times more expensive than the general region
  • Bargaining culture:In the market, bargaining is common, 30-50% of the first price
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 10%, Guide Tour $ 10-20 a day
  • Exchange:Using banks or official currency exchange, avoiding street currency exchange
