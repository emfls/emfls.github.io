⚠️ Barcelona travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket precautions

Barcelona is famous for its pickpockets.According to the Barcelona police, 48.1%of the crimes in Barcelona in 2023 were pickpockets.

  • Main dangerous areas:Especially in tourist attractions such as Ramblas Street, Sagrada Familia, and Guell Park
  • Bag management:Always close your bag near your body. Crossback makes the bag come to the front, and the backpack is used.
  • Mobile phone management:Prepare a cell phone strap and keep it invisible
  • Public transportation:If you stand near the subway door, you can catch your belongings as soon as the door closes.
🚗 Traffic safety precautions
  • Public transportation priority:Buy the T-10 card and use the subway and bus efficiently
  • Real -time information:Check real -time information using a public transportation app
  • Night movement:Avoid chestnuts alone, especially remote spots
  • Taxi:Use only official taxis and check the meter
🍽️ Food and restaurant precautions
  • Restaurant selection:Chosen a place for locals to go rather than a restaurant right in front of the tourist destination
  • Menu check:Order after checking the menu with the price
  • Waterism:You can drink tap water, but recommend bottled water
  • Tip Culture:5-10% tips are common in the restaurant
🏛️Cautions for use of tourist attractions
  • Pre -reservation required:Major attractions such as Sagrada Familia and Guell Park are required for advance reservations.
  • Avoid peak season:In the peak season in July-August, there are so many tourists
  • Admission discount:Use of discount benefits for admission fees as an international student ID card
  • Photo shooting:Some museums and interior of the cathedral are prohibited
🌤️Chires and costumes
  • UV protection:Sunscreen and sunglasses are required because the sunlight is strong due to the Mediterranean climate.
  • Sudden rain:Preparation of waterproof jackets or umbrella
  • Comfortable shoes:Wearing comfortable shoes because most sightseeing is on foot
  • Preparation for day crossing:It can be chilly in the morning and evening, so prepare a light outerwear
