⚠️ Bilbao travel precautions

Bilbao security:Overall, in a safe city or a specific area, nighttime attention is needed.

  • Lechendakari Agari Street (Deussto):At night, drugs and prostitutes are active areas and do not stop taxis.
  • Bilbao La Bieha (San Francisco area):The area where the underprivileged lives and risks at night or alone
  • Prohibition of exposure to money and valuables:Especially in dangerous areas, cash or valuable exposure
  • Night movement:As much as possible, the lights are as bright as possible and move to a lot of people
🚗 Traffic and driving precautions
  • Rent -a -car driving cost recommendation:There are many one -way roads, making it easy to get lost and risk of accidents
  • Recommended for using public transportation:The city movement is safe and efficient
  • Water search:When you get lost, request help from locals
  • Parking problem:Urban parking space is very limited
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Foody weather:Even though it is clear, it may rain suddenly, so the umbrella must be required
  • Maritime Climate:Even in the summer, it can be chilly in the evening, so prepare long -sleeved clothes
  • Winter mildness:It is a gentle winter, but the humidity is high and the temperature is low
  • wind:Strong wind caution when visiting the coastal area
🗣️ Cultural precautions
  • Basque:Local use combination of Basque (Euskira) and Spanish
  • Finnoths culture:In Pincharusba, it is common to discard the napkin on the floor.
