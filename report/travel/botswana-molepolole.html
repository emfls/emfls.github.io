⚠️Mallepole Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever

  • malaria:Mosquito activity time (evening-dawn) Wearing long-sleeved, insect repellent required, taking prevention drugs
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, peel raw fruit peel

  • Emergency situation:Local emergency room contact information check in advance, travel insurance required

  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, recommended 4WD vehicles
