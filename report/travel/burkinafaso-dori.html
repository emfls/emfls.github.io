⚠️Tori travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Northern border areas, around the night market market

  • Terrorism:The northern part of the Burkina Paso is a terrorist dangerous area and issued a travel alarm of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • Armed intensity:Armed robbery on the road, prohibited from moving at night
  • checkpoint:Soldiers and police checks are frequent and fully armed
  • After 6 pm:Restrictions on traffic and restraint
  • Local guide:Accompanied by a reliable local guide
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:There are many non -packaging roads and cannot be traffic in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Regular bus operation is irregular and safety is required
  • Rent -a -car:4WD vehicle required, recommended to accompany local driving experiences
  • fuel:There is a rare gas station, so it is necessary to secure enough fuel
  • Border pass:Complex for submitting Carne and issuing traffic permit certificates
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (11-4):The temperature is 15-40 degrees, very dry and dusty
  • Woogi (May-October):25-35 degrees of temperature, increasing risk of wet and malaria
  • Harmatan:Dry wind blowing from the Sahara Desert in December-February
  • Daily crossroads:Day and night temperature difference 15 degrees or more
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen required throughout the year
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • yellow fever:Required vaccination required, confirming certificate when entering Korea
  • malaria:Over the risk of annual occurrence, preventing prevention drugs and preventing mosquitoes
  • Water -person disease:Recommended for vaccinations such as cholera and typhoidus
  • drinking water:Only drinking water, no ice intake
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical infrastructure, it is necessary to transfer cities for emergencies
