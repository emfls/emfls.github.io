🌊 Natural disasters and safetyism

Main precautions:Typhoon, strong wind, coastal safety

  • typhoon:Typhoon season in July-September, the possibility of canceling flights and ships, and confirmation of real-time weather information required
  • gale:Due to the nature of Jeju Island, the winds are stronger year -round, raincoat rather than umbrella
  • Coastal safety:Compliance with safety ships in coastal cliffs such as Jusangjeolli University and Jeongbang Falls
  • Hallasan Climbing:Weather change rapidly, checking weather information before climbing and preparing appropriate equipment
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car:Essential for traveling in Jeju Island, required for peak season reservations, international license unnecessary needs
