⚠️ Herbi Bay Travel Precautions

🌊 Marine safetyism

Main precautions:Jellyfish, strong birds, ultraviolet rays

  • jellyfish:November-May Jellyfish season, checking local safety notice before swimming
  • Strong birds:Since the birds around the Fraser Islands are strong, only swimming in the designated swimming area
  • ultraviolet rays:UV rays are very strong due to the destruction of the ozone layer, wearing sunscreen and hat required
  • Dingo:In Fraser Island, don't feed you when you encounter wild dingo.
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car:Australia is required for left -handed and international driver's license
