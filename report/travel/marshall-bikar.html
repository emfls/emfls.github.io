⚠️ Bikar Book Course Precautions

🚨 Note that safety and emergency situations

Main risk factors:Isolated Colossem, Low Medical Facilities, Communication Restrictions

  • Medical emergency situation:The nearest hospital is facing each other (579km), emergency helicopter structural system
  • Communication:No mobile phone signal, preparation for satellite communication equipment required
  • Weather change:Sudden storms and high waves, weather information preliminary confirmation required
  • Marine life:Shark, jellyfish, toxic fish, and guide accompaniment
🚢 Traffic and access precautions
  • Airlines limit:Only a small aircraft that operates twice a week,
  • Boat access:Only access to narrow waterways in the west, requiring skilled captains
  • Tidal Liver:Lagoon access is not possible at low tide, requirement of assistant table
  • Fuel supply:Local fuel is not available, enough fuel preparation
  • Emergency evacuation:Establishment of evacuation plans for bad weather, establishing an emergency contact network
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Tropical climate:The year-round temperature 24-30 degrees, high humidity and strong ultraviolet rays
  • Woogi (5-November):Heavy rains and typhoon risks, and care for travel time
  • Dry (12-4):Relatively safe but strong trade wind
  • Sea level rise:Increased risk of flooding due to climate change
  • Lack of freshwater:Preparation of rainwater dependence, drinking water
🐢 Environmental Protection
  • Sea Turtle Protection:Restrictions on beaches, no use of flash
  • Bird protection:Prohibition of new habitats in breeding season, minimizing noise
  • Coral reef protection:Prohibition of coral contact, use of eco -friendly sunscreen
  • Garbage discharge:All garbage export obligations, prohibition of use of disposable items
