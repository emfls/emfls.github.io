📅 Mountain 2 nights 3 days travel courses

Day 1: History and Culture Exploration

San Moscow → Sancore University → Team Buku Archaeology Museum → Central Market

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:In the 14th century, Sudan-Sudan-Sahel Architectural Mud Brick Architecture and Islamic Culture Experience, exploring the traditions of Islamic theology and law at Sankore University, the best academic center in the medieval Africa, and watching the Mali Empire and Songga Empire's relics and ancient Arabic manuscripts at the Team Buku Archaeology Museum. In the central market, you can see local handicrafts, traditional fabrics, and spices.
📍San Mosque 📍Sanore University 📍 Team Buku Archaeological Museum 📍Angang Market

Day 2: Nature and Traditional Experience

Niger River Boat Tour → Visit Traditional Village → Desert Sunset Tour → Traditional Music Performance

Recommended time:10 hours
Core points:In the Niger River, the life shortage of West Africa, you can see the riverside landscapes and fishermen's daily lives, meet hippo and diverse birds, experience the mud architecture and traditional life in the traditional villages of the Dogon and Fullani, understand the African tribal culture, and watch the sunset of the minister with a camel tricking at the Sahara Desert. In the evening, you can see the traditional Mali music performances using the Gembe drum and the Kora Half to experience the West African rhythm and melody.
📍Niger River 📍 Traditional village 📍 Desert Tour 📍 Music venue

Day 3: Visiting and Culture Experience (optional)

Zenne Day Tour → Bandiagara Cliff → Dogon Village → Traditional Craft Experience

Zenne:5 hours, the world's largest mud building, Zenne Daemosk and Monday Market, West African commercial culture experience
Bandiagara Cliff:It takes 4 hours, exploration of ancient rock villages and ancient rock paintings in the Holy Land of Dogon, designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Dogon Village:Takes 3 hours, the Dogon Family Culture Experience with a unique space view and a traditional architectural style
Traditional crafts:2 hours, participate in the process of making mud dyeing and traditional fabric processing process
