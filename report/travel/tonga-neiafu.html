⚠️ NAPU travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Late at night around the port, the outer island area

  • Marine safety:When observing humpback whales, only government permit companies are used to prohibit maximum 10 people and 4 or more people at the same time.
  • Natural disasters:The possibility of earthquake occurrence in the ring area of the Pacific Fire, and the evacuation path in case of emergency
  • Medical facilities:There is a NAPUE PUE Hospital, but the facility restrictions are limited, and in a serious emergency, Nukualopa transfer
  • communication:Signal of mobile phones in the outer island area, preparing for emergency contact means
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • flight:90 minutes flight from Nukualopa to Ney Afu, the possibility of cancellation depending on the weather
  • perry:Nucualopa runs out all night, stop operating when the waves are high.
  • taxi:There are many cases in the absence of a meter, required to negotiate before boarding
  • Sunday restrictions:Most transportation and store operations on Sundays
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, the road condition is poor
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Tropical climate:Mild but humidity, highly moisture intake
  • Woogi (11-4):Cyclone season, strong wind and heavy rain
  • Dry (5-10):Whale observation optimal period, relatively dried
  • ultraviolet rays:South Pacific strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
  • Maritime weather:Cancellation of boat tours can be canceled due to sudden weather changes
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:Most of them are imported and 2-3 times more expensive than Korea
  • Humpback Whale Tour:$ 200-300 on a daily tour when using a government license company
  • Consumption tax:Add 15% consumption tax to all products and services
  • Tip Culture:Tips in the service industry, 10-15% level

  • Human Whale Season:This is the most optimal period of June-November, especially in August-October
  • Tongga traditional food:Okaika (pickled fish), fresh lobster, crustaceans, etc.
  • Optimal visits:The dry season in May-October is the best for the weather and sightseeing
  • Exchange:Take the US dollar or Australian dollar and exchange it from Fanga to Panga
  • Babau Islands:The second largest city and activity center in Tonga
