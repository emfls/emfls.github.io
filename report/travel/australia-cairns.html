⚠️Cairns travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Seeing downtown, night beach, crowded tourist destination

  • pickpocket:Liberalism, bags in the night market, airport, public transportation, etc.
  • Night movement:Recommended to use bright roads, public transportation, official taxi when moving alone at night
  • Natural disasters:Tropical cyclones and floods between November and May, checking local weather information
  • Emergency situation:Australian emergency number 000, Emergency+ app installation recommendation
🌊 Marine and Wildlife Safety
  • Jellyfish (Stinger):Wearing Stinger Suits when swimming in November -June, and prohibiting swimming outside the designated area
  • Crocodile / Snakeism:River, wetlands and tropical rainforests compliant with warning signs, prohibit access to wildlife
  • Snorkeling/Diving:Completion of safety training, use of certified tours, lifed guard information
☀️ Weather and healthism
  • ultraviolet rays:Ultraviolet rays throughout the year, SPF50+ sunscreen, hats, sunglasses required, 10 to 14 o'clock outdoor activityism
  • Dehydration, heat:If you have enough water intake, relax, and are vulnerable to the heat, avoid excessive certain
  • Rainy (November -April):Sudden scall, umbrella and right preparation
