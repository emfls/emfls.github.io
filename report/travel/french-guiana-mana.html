⚠️ Mana travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, Zika virus

  • Yellow fever vaccination:Essential inoculation, inoculation certificate, required before entering the country
  • Malaria prevention:Doctors' prescription prevention medications, always use of mosquito repellents
  • Anti -mosquitoes:Wearing long -sleeved clothes, using mosquito nets, outdoor activity of dinner time
  • Drinking water safety:Only drink bottled water, Ice or fresh fruit intake
🌊 Natural environmental precautions
  • River Tour:Swimming alone without torrents, crocodile habitats, and guides
  • Jungle trekking:Toxic plants, wildlife, must be moved with guides
  • Beach activity:Sea turtle spawning period (May-August) Night beach restrictions
  • Weather change:Sudden tropical showers, rainfall (12-7) flood caution
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
