  • Airport movement:Fleepslan Airport ~ City Lightrail (approximately 45 minutes), airport bus (about 30 minutes)

🌧️Checks related to weather
  • The average annual rainfall number 239 days:Umbrella, waterproof jacket required
  • Summer (June -August):13 ~ 19 degrees, a lot of cool and clear weather
  • Winter (December -February):0 ~ 6 degrees, rain or snow, road freezing
  • Spring/Autumn:A large number of days, sudden showers
  • ultraviolet rays:Even if there are a lot of clouds, UV rays are strong, sunscreen
