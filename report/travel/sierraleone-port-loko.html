  • Prohibition of solo behavior:Always travel with local guides or companions
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, so 4WD vehicle recommended use
  • Rainy movement:In the rainy season in May-October, the road condition deterioration
  • Public transportation:Safety confirmation required for local buses or taxis
  • Fuel:Preparation of fuel when moving long distances
  • GPS use:Opponent map download required

  • Woogi (May-October):Temperature 24-30 degrees, moist, rain, rainy equipment required
