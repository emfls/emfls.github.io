⚠️Kirkira (Corpu) Travel Note

🌡️ Mediterranean climate precautions

Summer:It is essential to apply sunscreen and take a hat and sunglasses because the sun is strong.

  • UV protection:Use high SPF sunscreen to protect the skin from strong sunlight in summer
  • Moisture intake:Dehydrate prevention with enough water in hot weather
  • Proper dress:Recommended for wearing light and breathable clothes
  • Winter:It is gentle but it can rain, so prepare an umbrella
🏊 Award -winning safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:When swimming, it is good to wear a life jacket for safety.
  • Children accompanied:If you are with your children, more attention is needed
  • Snorkeling equipment:Enjoy snorkeling safely in the Ionian Sea with borrowed equipment
  • Boat Tour:Compliance with safety rules when exploring caves
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car use:It is recommended that the traffic in the island is inconvenient, so it is recommended to rent a vehicle.
  • Mountain road driving:There is a mountain road on the island, so it is somewhat rough, but the drivers are kind.
  • parking:Most of them are free and easy to find, but some roads do not have street lights, so weekly driving is recommended.
  • Moped:Moped rentals are also available to explore the island
🌊 Environmental Protection
  • Nature protection:It is essential to protect nature without abandoning garbage on the beach
  • Marine ecosystem:Protection of marine life during snorkeling or diving
  • Olive orchard:Environmental protection awareness when visiting traditional olive orchards
  • Cultural property protection:Protection of cultural property in UNESCO World Heritage Area
🏪 Business hours and holidays
  • Sunday Closed:Most shops and markets are closed on Sunday
  • Siesta time:At around 2-5 pm, many shops rest time
  • Museum confirmation:Operating hours and holidays prior confirmation before visiting tourist destinations
  • Peak season:It can be longer waiting for summer tourists in summer
