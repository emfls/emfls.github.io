⚠️ Barberry travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the station, late at night

  • pickpocket:Reverse, major tourist attractions, pickpockets on the beach, bags in the future and zipper lock
  • Bicycle theft:Listening to locks when bicycle rentals
  • Going out late at night:Be careful to go alone after 10 pm around the beach
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transport strike:Due to the nature of Sweden, sudden traffic strikes can occur, alternative means of transportation
  • Train ticket:Pre -purchase at the station ticket machine, fines for free rides
  • Cycling:Recommended for using coastal bicycles, wearing a safety hat
  • parking:Free time for parking in the city parking lot, there is a paid section at night
  • Walking:Most of the city and beaches can be walked away, but the wind can be strong.
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 18-25 degrees, windy, high amount of sunshine, UV protection required
  • Winter (12-2):0-7 degrees of temperature, low body temperature with sea breeze, preparation for winter clothes
  • Spring/Autumn:Daily crossing, preparation of umbrellas and outerwear compared to sudden showers
  • sea breeze:Due to the nature of the coast, the wind is strong, so the hats and the windbreak are essential.
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Fort, cafes around the beach, restaurant prices 2-3 times expensive
