🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the central market late at night, the outside area

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketism in the central market and tourist attractions, the bags are carried in the future and the valuables are distributed.
  • Going out late at night:Risk of going alone after the sun, recommending group moving
  • Local conflict:Tourists can be dangerous during conflicts or political demonstrations
  • Drinking related:Avoid drinking with locals and increase crime risk when excessive drinking
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Bus operation is irregular and safety standards are recommended, taxi recommendation
  • Taxi fee:There are many cases where there is no meter,
  • Rent -a -car:The road is in poor road and there is a shortage of traffic signals.
  • Domestic flights:The cancellation depending on the weather is frequent, securing the relaxation schedule
  • Boat use:Essential to confirm safety equipment, stop operating in case of deterioration of weather
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:High risk of mosquito -mediated diseases, taking prevention drugs and using repellents
  • Dengue:Due to the nature of the tropics, the risk of dengue fever
  • drinking water:Do not directly eat tap water, drink only water or boiled water
  • food:Be careful when eating street food, only cooked enough food
  • Medical facilities:Limited medical facilities are required for insurance for emergency situations
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (11-4):Strong showers and cyclone risks, indoor evacuation plans
  • Dry (5-10):Relatively stable but strong ultravioletism
  • humidity:Destructive risk, sufficient moisture intake with high humidity throughout the year
  • Temperature:Maintaining 25-30 degrees throughout the year, interfering with sleep due to tropical nights
  • Maritime weather:Cancellation of boat tours can be canceled due to sudden weather changes
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:Most of them are imported 2-3 times as expensive compared to Korea
