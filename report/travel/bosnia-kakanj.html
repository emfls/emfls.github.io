⚠️Cacans travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Outside Mountain area, some of the old towns in the late night

  • Tunnel safety:Be sure to accompany the guide when exploring the Labenal Tunnel, prohibit personal exploration
  • Mountain safety:Wear appropriate equipment when climbing the pyramid, the weather confirmation required
  • Mine risk:Absolute entry and exit outside the hiking trails displayed, the existence of war remnants
  • Wildlife:Mountain bears, wolves, food properly stored
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation limit:Cacans are limited in public transportation, recommending rental car or tours
  • Mountain road:Keeping the winding mountain road, speed control and safe distance when driving
  • Sarajevo connection:About 1 hour by car from Kakanz to Sarajevo
  • parking:Restrictions on parking spaces around the pyramid, recommending arrival early
  • Road status:Some unpaved roads, rainy weather caution
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature of 15-25 degrees, the mountainous area is large
  • Winter (12-2):Gion -5-5 degrees, snowy eyes, tunnel entrance freezing attention
  • Spring/Autumn:Big and sudden showers, umbrellas and outerwear preparation
  • Inside the tunnel:Keeping 14-15 degrees throughout the year and high humidity
  • altitude:More than 700m above sea level, altitude disease attention
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist attraction:Pyramid Tour 25-35BAM, Tunnel Tour 15-25BAM
