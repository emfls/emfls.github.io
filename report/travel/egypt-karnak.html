🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Taxi/Transportation Costs Rag:Taxi and Bongo cars are required to negotiate prices. Many cases of calling higher than list price
  • Uber/Indive:Luxor has no Uber, so it is recommended to use local apps such as Indrive
  • Jim test when entering:Prohibition of bringing nails, knives, nails, etc. Can be seized at the entrance
  • Walking:There are many wide streets, stone roads, and sand roads, so comfortable shoes are required
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (May -September):35 ~ 45 degrees, very hot and dry. Hat, sunscreen, water required
  • Winter (December -February):10-25 degrees, a big deal. Morning/evening chill
  • Spring/Autumn:20 ~ 35 degrees, sand breeze (carmecin) caution, mask/scarf preparation
  • Sunlight, Ultraviolet Rays:Short and sunshine strong. Sunglasses, sunscreen, moisture supplement
💰 Price and security precautions
  • Tourist destination:Temple, museum, cafe, souvenir store, etc.
