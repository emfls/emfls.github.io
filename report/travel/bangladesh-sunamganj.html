⚠️Shuam Kanji Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, dengue fever, diarrhea, hepatitis

  • malaria:Mosquito repellent required, long -sleeved clothes, mosquito nets use
  • Water -person disease:Only drinking water, avoid ice, prohibit raw intake
  • Dengue:Mosquitoism, which is active during the day, visits the hospital immediately when high fever
  • Flood risk:Monsoon period (June-September) Sudden floods can occur
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, unable to pass from rainy season
  • Boat safety:Wearing life jackets, prohibited boarding of overload boats
  • Public transportation:Bus fast driverism, seat belt wear
  • Night movement:Late time moving late at night, many lighting areas
  • communication:Weakness of cell phone signals in the outskirts, preparing for emergency contact means
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Monsoon period (June-September):Intensive heavy rains and flood risks, travel schedules can be changed
  • Dry (10-3):The temperature of 15-25 degrees, the best time to travel
  • Heat (April-May):30-40 degrees of temperature, enough water intake
  • humidity:High humidity, mold prevention attention year -round
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash Society:Most of them are limited to cash payment, credit card use
  • Exchange:Exchange the dollar to Taka, use banks or official exchange offices
  • Bargaining:Price bargaining in the market, 30-50% of the initial presentation price
  • Tip Culture:Guide, small tips for boat drivers
