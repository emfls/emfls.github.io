🌊 Marine safety precautions
  • birds:Strong birdism around the initials, prohibits swimming alone without guides
  • coral reef:Wearing aqua shoes required when it comes to coral contact
  • Marine life:The presence of dangerous living things such as sharks and stingrays, maintenance of safe distances
  • Diving Safety:Only participate in certification holders, compliance with birdie system
  • Boat safety:Wearing life jackets, departure after checking the weather
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (5-November):Typhoon and heavy rain risk, travel schedule change possibility

  • typhoon:Typhoon season cancellation and evacuation preparation
  • Sea level rise:Influence of sea level rise due to climate change
