⚠️Nonaburi travel precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Nona Buri is a hot and humid tropical climate of the year, especially in summer

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of the riverside area, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquito repellents and long -sleeved clothes
🚤 Precautions when using water market
  • Boat safety:Seat belt is required when using boats in the Nonaburi Water Award Market
  • Personal belongings:The belongings that can fall into the water when using the boat are stored in the waterproof bag.
  • Bargaining:In the water market, bargaining is common and required to negotiate appropriate price
  • Early time visit:If you visit early in the morning, there are few tourists and a more true water market.
🚗 Precautions when using transportation
  • Access from Bangkok:In Bangkok, BTS, MRT, and buses easily accessible
  • Zao Praya Express:The best way to move from Bangkok to boat and Nona Buri
  • Local transportation:Move in the city using Song Tae -woo or tuk -tuk, use after negotiations
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, local traffic laws and regulations required
🍽️ Food and hygiene precautions
  • Street food:Select a place with a good hygiene when eating local street food
  • Bottled water drinking:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • Iceism:It is safe to avoid the ice of drinks sold on the street
  • Spicy food:Thai food can be very spicy, so you can order it at first
  • Water market food:Most of the foods sold in the water market are fresh and delicious, but consumption after hygiene confirmation
🛕 Religion and cultural courtesy
  • When visiting the temple:Wearing clothes covering your shoulders and knees, taking off your shoes and entering
  • Buddhist shooting:Taking a picture with a Buddha statue is considered a rude behavior.
  • Prohibit the touch of the head:In Thailand, the head is sacred, so it prohibits the touch of others' heads
  • To the toes:It is rude to make the toes to the Buddha or the person
  • Royal Respect:Essential expression of respect for the royal family of Thailand, especially when visiting the palace
🏺 Precautions when experiencing the Mon's culture
  • Respect for cultural:Respect and understanding of the Mon Culture and Tradition of Ko Kret Island
  • Pottery Purchase:The Mon tribe is a traditional craft, and the price is reasonable and the quality is excellent.
  • Photo shooting:Find permission when shooting residents in the Mon village
  • Environmental protection:Do not throw away garbage to protect the natural environment of the island
