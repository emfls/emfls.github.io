⚠️ Buyeo travel precautions

🚨 Safety and viewing attention

Major areas:Nakhwa Rock Cliff, Busosanseong Promenade, White Horse Riverside

  • Nakhwa rock cliff:Do not go beyond the safety railing, and the risk of falling when taking pictures
  • Trail:Slide attention in the promenade such as Busosanseong and Seongheungsanseong Fortress, wearing appropriate shoes
  • Baekma River cruise ship:Compliance with safety rules when boarding, special attention when accompanied by children
  • Museum view:Do not touch the exhibits, prohibit flash shooting
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Buyeo is limited to the bus line, so check the timetable in advance.
  • parking:Major tourist destinations are recommended to arrive early because the parking space is narrow.
  • taxi:Since the number of taxis in Buyeo is small, you can make a reservation in advance or consider waiting time
  • Rent -a -car:Narrow roads and one -way traffic attention, navigation required
  • Walking:There is a distance between tourist destinations, so enough time assignment
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 25-35 degrees, moist and hot, enough water intake and shade rest
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature -5-5 degrees, dry, cold, winter supplies required
  • Spring/Autumn:Big, fine dustism, mask and outerwear preparation
  • Rainy season:Restrictions on outdoor activities in heavy rains in June-July
  • ultraviolet rays:There are many outdoor tourist destinations, so sunscreen and hat required
💰 Precautions related to rates
  • entrance fee:National Buyeo Museum Free, Baekje Culture Complex 3,000 won, Jeonglimsaji Adults 1,500 won
  • pleasure boat:Baekma River One weekly course adult 15,000 won, round trip 10,000 won
  • Hot air balloon experience:About 150,000 won for adults, canceling according to the weather
  • Parking fee:Paid parking lot of major tourist attractions 1,000-2,000 won per hour
