⚠️ Eun Kata Bay Travel Note

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, typhoids

  • malaria:Use of mosquito repellents, wear long -sleeved clothes, prevention drugs
  • drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • Water Activities:Risk of infection of blood absorption from Malawi Lake, immediately shower after swimming
  • Emergency situation:Restrictions on local hospital facilities, in serious cases, South African transfer
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, deterioration of road conditions during rainy season
  • Public transportation:Speed ​​and overloading when using minibus
  • Rent -a -car:License on the left side, the international driver's license required
  • Boat use:Checking the life jacket, weather condition check
  • Night movement:Avoid as much as possible and use a reliable driver if necessary
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (11-4):Rain and flood risks, road traffic difficulties
  • Dry (5-10):Dry and dusty, enough water intake
  • Daily crossroads:Morning evening, chilly, hot during the day
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen near the equator
  • Lake weather:Sudden wind and waves change
💰 Price related precautions
  • Foreigners:Apply different rates from locals in tourist destinations
  • Bargaining culture:Price bargaining in the market
