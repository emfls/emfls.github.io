⚠️ Tsua Bowl Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever

  • malaria:Mosquito -mediated diseases required to take preventive drugs, constant insect repellent
  • yellow fever:Implied vaccination before entering the country, bringing in a certificate of inoculation
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, prohibit food intake
  • Wildlife:Don't go out of the car in Safari
  • Snake and Scorpion:Confirmation after wearing shoes, inspection of tents
🌡️ Climate -related precautions
  • Dry (5-10):Temperature 10-25 degrees, night's very cold, thick clothes required
  • Woogi (11-4):20-35 degrees of temperature, moist and hot, malaria risk increase
  • Daily crossroads:Due to the nature of the desert area
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
  • Sand Storm:Preparation of masks and goggles available during dry season
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, 4WD vehicles required
  • fuel:Gas station spacing, extra fuel preparation
  • GPS:Easy to lose in the desert area, satellite GPS required
  • drive:Traffic on the left, the crosswalk of the wildlife road
  • communication:Neglect of mobile phones signal, satellite calls, consideration
💰 Price related precautions
  • lodgment:Safari Lodge is very expensive, reservation required in advance
  • Tour cost:Guide Tour 1 daily $ 200-500
  • Fuel cost:High fuel prices, fully preparation of the budget
  • Tip Culture:10-15% tip for guides and drivers
  • Cash payment:Cannot be used in rural areas, prepare cash
