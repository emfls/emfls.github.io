🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around JR Takamatsu Station, late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at the station, shopping streets and tourist destinations, the bag is carried forward and the zipper lock
  • Cash payment:There are many places that only receive cash such as some udon houses and markets
  • Going out late at night:Be careful to go alone after 10 pm in the station and the port
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation inconvenience:Outside and island areas have a long interval between bus dispatching and requiring a pre -confirmation of the ferry schedule
  • Transportation card:Preparation of transportation cards such as Ikoka and Suika in advance
  • Rent -a -car:Considering the use of rentals on the nearby and island tours
  • Business hours short:Shops and restaurants are at 7-8 pm, and udon restaurants are closed at 3-4 pm

  • Autumn (September -November):Pleasant temperatures, autumn leaves attractions
  • Winter (December -February):Mild but dry, little eye
