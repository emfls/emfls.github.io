  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of riverside and natural areas, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquitoes and long -sleeved clothes
