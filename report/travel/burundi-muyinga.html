⚠️ Muinga travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Borders around the border area,

  • Political anxiety:Burundi is prohibited from access to protests and rally as political instability continues.
  • Race Conflict:Restriction of sensitive themes due to historical conflict between the Hutu and Tutchi
  • Night going out:After the sun, do not go out alone and accompany the local guide.
  • Border area:In the Tanzania border area, photography is prohibited without permission
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The bus and taxi status may be poor, so the seat belt is required.
  • Road situation:There are many dirt roads, and the road situation is very deteriorated in the rainy season.
  • Lack of fuel:Fuel supply is unstable, so it is necessary to confirm fuel when moving long distances
  • Gitega connection:It takes about 4-5 hours to the capital gatega
  • Hill terrain:Muinga is a steep slope of the highlands
🏥 Health and medical precautions
  • malaria:Malaria occurring areas required to take preventive drugs and use insect repellent
  • drinking water:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • Medical facilities:Local medical facilities are poor and required for emergency situations
  • Hygiene management:Washing hands and personal hygiene management thoroughly
  • Foodism:Caution of raw food or street food
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (February-May, September-November):It is impossible to pass on roads due to heavy rains, umbrellas and raincoats required
  • Dry (June-August, December-January):Dry and dusty, enough water intake
  • Plateau climate:The average altitude 1,700m is large and chilly at night
  • Equatorial area:Seon cream and hat mandatory with strong ultraviolet rays
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash Society:Preparation of cash because of limited credit card use
  • Exchange:Exchange the US dollar to Burundi Franc, and other Euron and other currencies are difficult to exchange.
  • Inflation:The import price is very expensive and the transportation cost is high due to the increase in fuel costs.
  • Tip Culture:Tips for guides or drivers
