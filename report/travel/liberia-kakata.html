⚠️Kakata travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the market, late at night

  • Livelihood crime:Theft and robbery due to economic difficulties are highly likely to occur
  • Weapon possession:Criminals are very dangerous because they are likely to carry knives, jungle knives and firearms
  • Prohibition of going out at night:Absolutely prohibited to go out alone after the sunset, wait inside the hostel
  • Public power limit:Since the security power does not reach the province, it is necessary to manage its own safety management.
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:The road condition is very bad due to the lack of infrastructure maintenance
  • Prohibition of self -driving:Self -driving absolute refreshing self -driving using a car rental
  • Public transportation:Board after checking safety when using local buses or taxis
  • Rainy movement:Stop moving when flooding roads with tropical showers
  • Monrovia Connection:It takes about 2 hours to the capital, improving access to road improvement projects
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (May-October):Tropical showers frequent, road flooding and destruction risk
  • Dry (11-4):Respiratory diseaseism with hippopotanic dust wind
  • High temperature and humidity:Dressing of dehydration with high humidity 24-32 degrees throughout the year
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
  • malaria:High risk of malaria infection due to mosquito habitats
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash -oriented:Limited credit card use, US dollar cash required
  • Lack of power:Difficulty using ATM due to frequent blackouts
  • Inflation:The price of daily necessities is high due to high dependence on imports
  • Educational institution:Donation request for tours such as Booker Washington Research Institute
