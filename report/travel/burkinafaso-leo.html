⚠️ Leo travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the night market market, outskirts

  • Terrorism:Increased terrorist activities in the northern part of Burkina Paso, required to confirm the travel alarms of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • robbery:Restriction of night out at night, minimum cash and valuables
  • Photo shooting:Military facilities, prohibition of shooting government agencies, preliminary permission when shooting people
  • demonstration:Prohibition of access to political rally or protest areas
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, bad roads during rainy season
  • Public transportation:Bus operation irregularities, overload risk
  • taxi:No meter, preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, international driver's license required
  • Night driving:Risk of driving at night due to lack of lighting facilities
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Essential risks of year -round infections, prevention drugs and mosquito prevention required
  • yellow fever:Proposal for vaccination, presentation when entering certificate
  • drinking water:Only drinking water, no ice intake
  • food:Only intake of completely cooked food, street food attention
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical infrastructure, transport
