⚠️ Kiriti Mati Travel Precautions

🌊 Marine safetyism

Main precautions:Strong algae, mountaincho wounds, marine life

  • Algaeism:The sea around Kiriti Mati has a strong algae, so maintain a safe distance when swimming
  • Mountaincho Wound:Do not go into the sea barefoot, but be sure to wear aqua shoes
  • jellyfish:Woo Ki-cheol (November-April) Jellyfish Making, Instant Treatment
  • shark:Possibility of sharing sharks when fishing for large fish, observance of guide order
🏥 Medical and emergency situations
  • Lack of medical facilities:Kiritimati is only basic medical facilities, so it is necessary to transfer mainland in severe illness.
  • First emergency contact:Kiribashi Police Station and Medical Career Contacts In advance
  • Travel Insurance:Insurance required for medical expenses and emergency evacuation costs
  • Preparation for the pharmacy:Prepare for personal doses (difficult to buy local)

  • Tropical climate:Desisement at 24-31 degrees throughout the year, high humidity

  • Dry (May-October):Relatively dry but strong ultravioletism
  • Equatorial climate:Similar to the year and year -round weather
  • Typhoon season:Evacuation indoor during the typhoon alarm in November-April

  • Internet limit:The Internet speed is very slow and unstable, and important tasks are handled in advance
