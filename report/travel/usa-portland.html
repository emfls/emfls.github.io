⚠️ Portland travel precautions

🌧️ weather and climate precautions

Main features:Mild maritime climate, winter frequent rain

  • Woogi (October-April):It rains often, but the strength is weak, the hooded jacket recommends rather than an umbrella
  • Summer (June-September):Dry and comfortable, average temperature of 20-25 degrees tourism
  • Daily crossroads:It can be chilly in the morning and evening, so light outerwear is required
  • ultraviolet rays:Summer cream required with strong sunlight in summer
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation development:Max light rail and bus system well builds up, rental cars unnecessary
  • Portland International Airport (PDX):Max red line 40 minutes to the city, $ 2.50
  • Hop card:Public transportation card, pre -purchase recommendation
  • Bicycle -friendly:Bike road well maintenance, use of bike sharing systems
  • Uber/Lift:About $ 8-15 when moving in the city
🏙️ City safety precautions
  • Downtown:Safe during the day, but don't go alone at night
  • Old Town:Areas with many homeless people, carefully moved
  • Personal belongings:Management of belongings when emptying seats at a cafe or restaurant
  • Emergency situation:911 Report, Korea Consulate Contact Preview
🌿 Environment and cultural precautions
  • Eco -friendly culture:Restriction of disposable supplies, recycling separation collection
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 18-20%, cafe 10-15% tips
  • No smoking:Smoking only in the indoor smoking and the designated smoking area
  • Local courtesy:Quietly in public transportation, pedestrian priority culture
