⚠️ Mitirini travel precautions

🌡️ Mediterranean climate precautions

Summer:The heat is severe, so it is necessary to prepare items such as sunshine blockers or hats

  • Prevention of heat stroke:In summer, the heat is severe, so enough water intake and rest
  • UV protection:It is necessary to prepare items such as sunscreen or hats
  • Moisture intake:Dehydrate prevention with enough water in hot weather
  • Optimal period:Spring and autumn are especially recommended
♨️Checking hot springs
  • Eat enough water:It is important to eat enough moisture when you have a hot spring bath
  • Dehydration prevention:If it is in the hot water of the hot spring for a long time, dehydration may occur, so be sure to drink plenty of water.
  • Personal hygiene:Before the hot spring bath, you must wash your body through a shower
  • Operating time confirmation:Each hot spring may have different operating hours, so check in advance and visit
🏖️ Beach safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:Check the waves and algae status when swimming on the beach
  • UV protection:Seon cream frequently applied when exposed to the beach for a long time on the beach
  • Personal items:Pay attention to the management of valuables on the beach
  • Marine Sports:Wearing safety equipment for water sports
🏔️ Hiking safety precautions
  • Proper equipment:Hiking comfortable shoes and enough water preparation
  • Check the weather:Be sure to check the weather situation before hiking
