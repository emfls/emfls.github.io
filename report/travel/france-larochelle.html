⚠️ La Rochell Travel Note

🌊 Marine weather precautions
  • Due to the nature of the beach, the rain falls with the wind, so the umbrella is useless
  • It is difficult to predict exactly the rain due to a lot of showers
  • Woo cost windbreaker is much more useful than an umbrella
  • The windshield is essential because the coastal wind is strong
🚢 Caution for cruise usage
  • Boyard Fort Cruise is required for advance reservation
  • You need to go early and have a good place to secure a good place
  • The side of the ship can be seen close to the sea, but the waves
  • Recommended for taking motion sickness
🏛️ Museum and tourist attractions
  • La Rochelle Aquarium is large, so it secures enough time
  • Tour de La Langtern is a historical place where each floor was used as a prison
  • Mandatory costumes when entering the cathedral
🍷 Food and beverage precautions
  • Prohibition of driving when tasting Pino de Sharinte
  • Be careful if there is a seafood allergic
  • Check the expiration date when purchasing local specialties
