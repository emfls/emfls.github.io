⚠️Moyo Bamba Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and diseaseism

Main dangerous diseases:Malaria, dengue fever, Zika virus, yellow fever

  • Yellow fever vaccination:Mandatory vaccination at least 10 days before entering the Amazon area, an international certification certificate
  • Malaria prevention:Doctors' prescription prevention medications, always use of mosquito repellents
  • Drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice drinks
  • Foodism:Story food, fresh fruit, raw vegetables
  • Anti -mosquitoes:Long -sleeved, long pants, and mosquito nets use
🌿 Amazon jungle safety rules
  • Guide accompanied:When exploring the jungle, you must go with local professional guides
  • Toxic flora and life:Do not touch with plants and animals that you do not know
  • Preparation for emergency situations:Preparation of satellite telephone or emergency signal
  • Weather change:Preparation for sudden heavy rains and temperature changes
  • Sense:Bring GPS devices and compasses, losing attention
🏔️ Prevention and response to altitude disease
  • Growth:Adapt to Machu Picchu 2-3 days before visiting Machu Picchu
  • Sufficient moisture:3-4 liters per day
  • An alcoholic ban:Avoid drinking during the high adaptation period
  • Is it symptoms:Headaches, nausea, and dizziness immediately Hassan
  • Coca tea:Relieves altitude disease with local coca leaf tea
🚌 Traffic and security precautions
  • Night travel:Avoid moving long distance buses late at night
  • pickpocket:Market in front of a bag at the market, bus terminal
  • Fake police:Check at the tourism police station when requesting ID card
  • Taxi:Use of the official taxi company, check the meter
  • Cash management:Large amount cash dispersion storage
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (11-4):Heavy rain and risk of landslides, roads can be blocked
  • Dry (5-10):Strong ultraviolet rays, large crossovers
