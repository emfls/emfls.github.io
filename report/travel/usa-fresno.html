⚠️ Freso travel precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Rising to 34 degrees, very hot and dry. Sufficient water intake and rest in the shade
  • Spring (February-March):Blossom trail season in full bloom. Preparation
  • Autumn/Winter:It's a gentle weather, but it's chilly at night, so warm clothes are prepared
  • Throughout the year:California's unique sunlight required sunglasses and sunscreen
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car required:Public transportation is limited, so strong use of car rentals is highly recommended
  • Freeso Airport:About 20 minutes from Freszo Yosemite International Airport (FAT) to downtown
  • Major City Street:3 hours from San Francisco, 3 hours from Los Angeles
  • Yosemite access:99.8km to Yosemite National Park, about 1 hour and 30 minutes
  • parking:Free parking at most tourist attractions
🐿️Precipalists related to wildlife
  • Woodward Park:There are so many squirrels, so be careful if you are afraid of rodents
  • Duke:Maintaining the street because the work of the park is freely around
  • Wildlife food:Prohibition of feeding wild animals in the park
  • Farm visit:Because it is a pesticide spraying period, it does not touch fruit trees or flowers
🚫 Farm and orchard precautions
  • Respect for private land:Most of them are personal lands, so take pictures only on roads
  • Pesticideism:Blossom trails are time for pesticides, so plant contact is prohibited
  • Yu Pic Farm:Participation in fruit picking experiences only on licensed farms
