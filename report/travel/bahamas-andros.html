⚠️ Androstown travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Late beach, late night unmanned area

  • Isolated environment:Andross has a very low population density, making it difficult to ask for help, so always go to the group
  • Marine safety:When diving and snorkel, be sure to wear a professional guide and wear safety equipment
  • Emergency situation:As medical facilities are limited, first aid kit and emergency contact details are prepared
  • Wildlife:Crocodile and toxic marine biologicalism in the Mangrove area
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Limited transportation:Public transportation is very limited, so rental car or tour reservation required
  • flight:Use of small aircraft from Nasso to Andros, and the possibility of cancellation depending on the weather
  • Boat traffic:Use boats when moving between islands
  • Road status:There are many dirt roads and 4WD cars recommended
  • Fuel supply:Confirmation of enough fuel because the gas station is limited
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Hurricane Season (June-November):Strong winds and crowds of heavy rain, weather information before travel checks
  • Dry (12-5):20-28 degrees of temperature, low humidity and good time to travel
  • Rainy feature:Sudden scalls and thunderstorms, knowing indoor evacuation places
  • Maritime weather:The possibility of cancellation of boat tours when the wind is strong
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, sufficient UV protection required
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:All items depend on imports and 2-3 times more expensive than Korea
  • Diving cost:Professional diving tour 1 daily $ 150-300
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 15-20%, guided tour 10-15%
  • Cash use:In small shops, only cash is received
