📅 Dammam 2 night 3 -day travel course

Day 1: Modern culture and petroleum industry exploration

King Abdul Azes Center → Saudi Aramco Exhibition Hall → Dammamconishi → King Pad Park

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:In the central city of the city, the King Abdul Aziz World Cultural Center (Itara) experiences modern Arab culture and art, explores the history and technology of the oil industry at the exhibition hall of Saudi Aramco, the world's largest oil company, and enjoys the coastal walk and sunset in Dammam Konishi, which is built along the Persians, and experiences the daily life and family culture of locals in King Pad Park
📍 King Abdul Azes Center 📍 Saudi Aramco Exhibition Hall 📍Damamconishi 📍 King Pad Park

Day 2: Coast and Natural Experience

Half Moon Bay → Coral Island → Damam Regional Museum → Al Marina Mall

Recommended time:10 hours
Core points:At the Halfmun Bay, 20 km south of Dammam, you can enjoy the beautiful beaches and clear sea water, explore the marine ecosystem of Persia by snorkeling and diving in Coral Island, learning the history and Bedouin culture of the eastern part of the Damam area, and experiencing shopping and local foods in modern Al Marina Mall
📍 Half Moon Bay 📍 Coral Island 📍Damam area museum 📍Almarina Mall

Day 3: Visiting and Traditional Culture Experience (optional)

Al Kobar → Al Catiphysis → Tarut Island → Dammam Traditional Market

Al Kobar:3 hours, high -end shopping and international restaurant experience in modern commercial cities
Al Catiphysis:It takes 4 hours, explores Jude Night Farm and Traditional Agricultural Culture in Oasis, which is thousands of years old
Tarut Island:2 hours, exploration of archaeological sites on historical islands where traces of ancient civilization remain
Damam Traditional Market:1 hour, spice, fabrics, handicraft shopping and local culture experience at traditional suk

⚠️ Dammam travel precautions

🚨 Culture and religiousism

Main precautions:Respect for Islamic culture, conservative costumes

  • Dress Regulations:Both men and women wear clothes that cover their shoulders and knees, women are recommended to be loose clothes
  • evensong:Shop and restaurant temporary leave at 5 times a day
  • Ramadan period:Prohibition of public intake of public food during the daytime
  • alcohol:Alcohol sales and intake completely prohibited
  • Photo shooting:Local people, especially women, permission when shooting women
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Extreme heat:Summer (June-September) temperature 40-50 degrees, enough water intake required
  • Desert:Very dry and important skin and neck care
  • Sand breeze:Sudden sand storm can occur
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and hat
  • Winter (12-2):The best time to tour with a temperature of 15-25 degrees
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Buses and taxis are mainly used, no subway
  • Uber/Karim:Convenience of using local taxi app Karim and Uber
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, recommended desert driving experience
  • Road situation:Good development on highways, severe traffic jams in the city
  • Fuel cost:Very cheap gasoline price
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:High import and tourist attraction prices
  • Museum entrance fee:King Abdul Azes Center Free, Other Museum 20-50 Riyal
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 10-15%, hotel service 5-10
  • Seafood dishes:Persiaman fresh seafood price appropriate
