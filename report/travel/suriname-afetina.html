⚠️Afetina travel precautions

🚨 Health and medical attention

Main dangerous diseases:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, Zika virus

  • Yellow fever vaccination:Essential vaccination 10 days before departure, an international certified vaccination certificate
  • Malaria prevention:Preventive drugs from a week before departure, always use mosquito repellents
  • Drinking water safety:Only drink bottled water, avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • Medical facilities:Limited to local medical facilities, transfer to Paramaribo in case of emergency
🌊 Natural environmental precautions
  • Woogi (April-August):Riverside, rising water levels, unable access to some regions
  • Dry (September-March):Relatively good travel, but the water level is low, restrictions on boat operations
  • Wildlife:Dangerous animal forms such as vipers, jaguar, and Kaiman, requiring guides
  • Kang:Talking section, swimming skills, life jacket wearing
  • Tropical rainforest:Easy to get lost, prohibiting trekking alone
