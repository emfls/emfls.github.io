⚠️Abi A travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Main dangerous areas:Border zones, around the genetic facilities, all over the night time zone

  • Dispute area:The possibility of armed conflict through territorial disputes between Sudan and South Sudan
  • Armed Forces:Kidnapping and robbery due to armed group activities in the region
  • Mine risk:In the past civil war
  • Night traffic prohibition:Absolutely prohibited to go out after sunset
  • UNISFA Cooperation:Preliminary consultation with peacekeeper for all movements
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Most of the unpaved roads, no traffic in the rainy season
  • 4WD required:It is impossible to move with a general passenger car

  • Guide required:Prohibition of moving alone without local guides
