⚠️ Nakonna bath precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Nakona bath is a hot and humid tropical climate of the year, especially in summer

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Waterfall:The waterfall is the most beautiful in the rainy season in June-October, and the waterfalls can be small or dried up in April-May.
🚣 Rafting safety precautions
  • Safety equipment:Be sure to wear life jackets and helmets when rafting Nakonna Bath
  • Guide Direction:Strictly observing safety instructions for rafting guides
  • Swimming ability:Recommended only people with basic swimming ability
  • Check the weather:Rafting may be stopped on the day with a lot of rainfall.
  • Sales:Kun Dan Pracan Village Dam is especially strong, so beginners need attention
