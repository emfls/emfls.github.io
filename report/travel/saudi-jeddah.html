⚠️This travel precautions

🚨 Culture and religiousism

Main precautions:Respect for Islamic culture

  • Dress Regulations:Long -sleeved, long pants required when visiting the mosque, women need to cover their heads
  • evensong:During the 5th prayer time a day, shops and restaurants are temporarily closed.
  • Ramadan period:Prohibition of public intake of public food during the daytime
  • alcohol:Sales and intake of alcohol in Saudi Arabia
  • Photo shooting:Local people, especially when shooting women, must be permission
🌡️ Climate and health precautions
  • Summer (June-September):Temperature 35-45 degrees, extremely hot, enough moisture intake required
  • Winter (12-2):The temperature of 15-25 degrees, the best time for sightseeing
  • Desert:Big and dryness, moisturizers and lip balm
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required throughout the year
  • Dewatering:Fast dehydration due to desert climate, drinking water often
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Buses and taxis are mainly used, under the construction of subways
  • Uber/Carim:Utilization of local taxi apps, meter checks required
  • Rent -a -car:Need for international driver's license, knowing local traffic laws
  • Airport movement:1 hour from King Abdul Aziz International Airport to downtown
  • Road situation:Russian hour time zone traffic jams severe
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Alvalad, attention to tourists around Cornish
