⚠️Kalamata travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists such as Kalamata Central Plaza and Beach

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Conscious attention:When walking on the road or taking pictures,
  • Bag management:Put the bag in front and lock the zipper
  • Tourististism:Especially in famous tourist destinations and public transportation
🎫 Preliminary reservation required
  • Olive Farm Tour:In the peak season
  • Ancient Messiny Guide Tour:Korean guide tours require pre -booking, and English tours are recommended during peak season
  • Beach activity:Water sports or boat tours can be canceled depending on the weather, so secure the schedule
  • Traditional cooking experience:Cooking workshops in local homes need to make a reservation at least two days ago.
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Athens connection:Kalamata is 3 hours by bus from Athens and 4 hours by train
  • Kalamata-ancient Messini:About 30 minutes by bus, long interval intervals,
  • Recommended for using car rentals:Rent -a -cars are the most convenient for surrounding ruins and natural attractions.
  • Taxi:About 15 euros from Kalamata Airport to downtown and about 25 euros from ancient Messini
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Mediterranean Climate:The Mediterranean Climate, which is mild in winter and very hot in summer
  • Summer preparation:In summer (July-August), it is very hot, so sunscreen, hat, enough water required
  • Winter preparation:Winter is gentle, but it can rain, so prepare an umbrella
  • Windism:The coastal area may be strong, so prepare windproof jackets
🗣️ Language communication

Although Greek is official language, English can be communicated in tourist attractions. Knowing the basic Greek greetings will help you communicate with locals.
