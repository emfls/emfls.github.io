  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads, and the road condition deteriorates during the rainy season, recommended to use 4WD vehicles
  • Public transportation:The bus and taxi status is not good, checking the seat belt wearing
  • Lack of fuel:There are rare gas stations and fuel shortages frequent, securing sufficient fuel
  • Crossing the river:Requiring safety equipment when using the Niger River Line
  • Mobile time:It can take longer than expected, so a schedule plan
