  • jellyfish:Summer jellyfish requires safety notice confirmation before sea bath
  • Algaeism:Swim only in safety zones to prevent accidents due to sudden change of algae
  • Sunrise view:Dreaming of slippery rocks and waves when moving at dawn beach
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The interval between the Donghae city bus is long, so the timetable is required in advance.
  • Taxi:The distance between tourist destinations is far from the burden of taxi costs, recommended car rental
  • Parking Space:Possibility of parking lot of major tourist attractions in the peak season, recommending early visits
  • Road situation:Some of the coastal roads are narrow and bent, so safe driving required
  • Train connection:No KTX directly from Donghae Station to Seoul

  • fog:Dawn and evening time coastal fog occurrence, attention to driving
