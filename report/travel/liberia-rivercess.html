🏥 Health and hygiene precautions
  • malaria:Use mosquito repellents, wear long -sleeved clothes
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice
  • food:Only intake of completely cooked food, street food attention
  • Personal hygiene:Hand disinfectant occasional use
  • Emergency situation:Local hospital location and contact information in advance
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (May-October):Heavy rain and flood risks, and worsening road conditions
  • Dry (11-4):High temperature drying, enough water intake
  • humidity:Dealing attention with high humidity throughout the year
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays and sun creams near the equator
  • garments:Wearing a well -ventilated cotton clothes
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, unable to pass in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Check the seat belt when using the bus and taxi
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, local driving habits
  • fuel:The spacing of the gas station is far, so the fuel is well prepared
  • Night driving:Avoid as much as possible and move the daytime
