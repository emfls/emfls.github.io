⚠️Murlusa travel precautions

🏛️ Museum entry precautions
  • The National Automobile Museum and the Train Museum are recommended in advance
  • Large backpacks or carriers are limited to limit
  • Prohibition of use of flash when shooting automobiles and train exhibits
  • Museum discount benefits with the Liguz City Pass
🌍 Border precautions
  • When you visit Basel (Switzerland) and Freiburg (Germany), you are required to bring your passport
  • Switzerland is not a euro zone, so Swiss franc is ready
  • Schedule plan considering the border passage time
  • Checking between borders when using public transportation
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Due to the nature of the Alsace region, the temperature change is severe
  • Umbrella required because it rains often
  • Even in the summer, it is cool in the morning and evening, so it is necessary
  • Very cold in the winter Christmas market season
