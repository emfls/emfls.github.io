⚠️ Heres de La Frontera Travel Precautions

🚨 General criminal precautions
  • Pickpocket attention:Carry your bag in front of a tourist destination or a lot of people
  • Winery Tour:Special attention to the management of valuables after drinking
  • Passport management:Passport copy and 2 photos (passport size) always prepared
  • Storage of valuables:Use hotel safe and only possess minimal cash
🚗 Traffic safety precautions
  • Public transportation:1 hour 10 minutes by train from Seville, 1 hour 15 minutes by bus
  • After the wine tour:Absolutely prohibited from driving after drinking, using public transportation or tour buses
  • Walking:The old town is good to watch on foot
  • Taxi:Use only official taxis and check the meter
🌡️ Climate and environmental precautions
  • Strong sunlight:Summer UV rays are very strong, so sunscreen required
  • Daily crossroads:Due to the nature of inland cities, summer is hot and winter is cold
  • Wine Tour:Health care with proper drinking
  • Moisture intake:Dehydration prevention with enough water intake in summer
🗣️ Cultural precautions
  • Siesta time:Many shops are closed at 2-5 pm
  • mealtime:2-4 o'clock for lunch and 9-11 o'clock in the evening
  • language:English can be used in Spanish and tourist attractions
  • Tip Culture:5-10% tips are common in the restaurant

💡 Heres de La Frontera Travel Tips

  • Flamenco Festival:Check the flamenco festival schedule held every year at a certain period
