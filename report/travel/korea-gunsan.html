⚠️ Gunsan travel precautions

🌊 Marine safetyism

Main precautions:Tidal flats, tidal flats, maritime weather

  • Tide only:Due to the characteristics of the west coast, the difference between the tide is large, so it is necessary to check the water table
  • Tidal flat experience:The feet may be missing from the tidal flats, so wear safety equipment and accompany the guide.
  • Maritime weather:In the windy weather, the ship operation may be stopped
  • Swimming safety:At the beach, you can only swim in the designated area according to the safety personnel instructions.
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The time table may be checked in advance because the city bus may be long interval.
  • Rent -a -car:The island area is narrow and the parking space is limited
  • Ship Time:Pre -confirmation required for ship schedules such as Seonyudo
  • parking:Parking rates around tourist attractions, free parking lot location in advance
  • taxi:City taxis are enough, but in the island area, pre -booking is recommended
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 25-32 degrees, wet and hot, ultraviolet rays
  • Winter (12-2):-5-8 degrees, lower haptic temperature due to strong winds in the west coast
  • Spring/Autumn:Daily cross -crossing and wind can be strong
  • Rainy season:In mid-July-early August, heavy rainfall
  • West Coast Wind:The windscreen is essential because the wind is strong throughout the year
