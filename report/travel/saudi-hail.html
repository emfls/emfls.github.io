⚠️Hile travel precautions

🚨 Culture and religiousism

Respect for Islamic culture

  • Dress Regulations:Both men and women wear clothes that cover their shoulders and knees, women are recommended to be loose clothes
  • evensong:Shops and restaurants temporarily closed at 5 times a day
  • friday:Aslamic Sabbath Closed many shops and tourist attractions
  • Ramadan period:During the daytime public food intake
  • Photo shooting:Local people, especially when shooting women, must be permission
🌡️ Climate -related precautions
  • Summer (June-September):35-45 degrees of temperature, extremely hot, early morning or late sightseeing
  • Winter (12-2):Green 5-20 degrees, Daily Insulation, Thermal Clothing Essentials
  • Dry climate:The humidity is very low throughout the year, enough moisture intake and moisturizing required
  • Sand breeze:Spring sand storms can occur, masks and sunglasses prepare
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and hat
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car:International driver's license required, local traffic laws
  • Taxi:Uber, Karim app recommendation, meter check
  • Public transportation:Restrictions on bus routes, recommendations for using rental cars or taxis
  • Desert Tour:Be sure to prepare enough water and fuel with the guide
  • GPS:GPS signal in the desert area can be unstable
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination price:The price of restaurants and shops around major tourist attractions is high
  • Museum entrance fee:Most of them are free or inexpensive, recommended pre -booking
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant 10-15%, hotel service 5-10
  • Cash use:Prefer cash payment in traditional markets
