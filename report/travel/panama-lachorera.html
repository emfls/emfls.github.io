⚠️Rachoera travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Main risk factors:Tropical disease, wildlife, strong ultraviolet light, high humidity

  • malaria:High risk of mosquito -mediated diseases, use of preventive drugs and insect repellent
  • yellow fever:Essential for vaccination, obligations to present certificate of vaccination when entering Korea
  • Dengue:Egyptian forest mosquito mediation, insect repellent during the daytime
  • Wildlife:Dangerous animal formats such as vipers, scorpions, spiders, etc.
  • River:Parasite risk, prohibiting water intake without purification
🌧️ Climate -related precautions
  • Woogi (November-April):Daily strong showers, waterproof equipment required
  • Dry (May-October):Relatively dry but still high humidity
  • Temperature:25-30 ° C per year, not going below 20 ° C at night
  • humidity:80-90% high humidity, dehydration
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong near the equator, SPF 50+ sunscreen required
🚁 Traffic and access precautions
  • flight:Only SATENA airlines operate from San Hiscetel Guville, takes 1 hour 40 minutes.
  • Weather delay:The possibility of cancellation of flights in bad weather
  • Land access:The road condition is very poor, no traffic in the rainy season
  • Kang Transportation:Only canoes and boats can be used, required to wear safety equipment
  • communication:Mobile phone signal instability, recommended satellite communication equipment
🏛️ Cultural and social precautions
  • Aboriginal culture:Request for permission before taking pictures, compliance with cultural courtesy
  • Environmental protection:Garbage export, wild animal food prohibition
  • Local guide:Prohibition of moving alone, must accompany local guides
  • language:Spanish basic conversation needs, limited English communication

💡 Rachoera Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:May-October dry season is suitable for trekking and wildlife observation
  • lodgment:Eco Logina Local Guest House, and pre -booking required
