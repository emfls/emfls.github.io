⚠️ Pattaya travel precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Pattaya is a hot and humid tropical climate of the year, especially in summer

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of the tropics, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquito eradication and long -sleeved clothes
🏖️ Beach safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:In Pattaya Beach, swimming only in the safety zone when swimming
  • Jellyfishism:Jellyfish may appear in certain times, so local information is confirmed
  • Algaeism:Prohibition of swimming in areas with strong birds or waves
  • Health disease prevention:Rest in the shade for a long time in the beach activity
  • Water Sports:Wearing safety equipment when using water sports such as jet skiing and parasesiling
🚗 Precautions when using transportation
  • Song Tae -woo use:Pattaya's representative public transportation is used after price negotiations
  • Taxi:Be careful when requesting a meter and using it late at night
  • Use Tuk Tuk:Negotiation of the price in advance, wearing seat belts, suitable for short distance movement
  • Motorcycle Taxi:It is necessary to wear helmets, avoiding as much as possible for safety
  • Rent -a -Car/Scooter:Need for international driver's license, local traffic laws and regulations required
🍽️ Food and hygiene precautions
  • Street food:Select a place with a good hygiene when eating local street food
  • Bottled water drinking:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • Iceism:It is safe to avoid the ice of drinks sold on the street
  • seafood:After checking freshness, especially in summer
  • Spicy food:Thai food can be very spicy, so you can order it at first
🛕 Religion and cultural courtesy
  • When visiting the temple:Wearing clothes covering your shoulders and knees, taking off your shoes and entering
  • Buddhist shooting:Taking a picture with a Buddha statue is considered a rude behavior.
  • Prohibit the touch of the head:In Thailand, the head is sacred, so it prohibits the touch of others' heads
  • To the toes:It is rude to make the toes to the Buddha or the person
  • Royal Respect:Essential expression of respect for the Thai royal family
🌃 Night Life Safety Precautions
  • Working Street:Don't go alone in the night, and care for your valuables thoroughly
  • Excessive drinking:In an unfamiliar environment, excessive drinking can be dangerous, so moderately
  • Rewards:Rewards in Bana Club, checking prices in advance
  • Personal safety:When you go back to the hotel late at night, use safe means of transportation
