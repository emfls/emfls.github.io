⚠️San Francisco travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket and security attention

Major areas:SFO Airport, Union Square, Tender Earth, Tourist Density Area

  • Tender Earth:It's a downtown center, but there are many homeless people and drug addicts, so it's good to avoid it at night.
  • Vehicle theft:Never leave the luggage in the car when using the car rental, especially in the parking lot of the tourist attraction.
  • Police:The tourist destinations are always arranged in the tourist destination, so it is relatively safe, but it is dangerous to go around late at night.
  • How to deal with:My belongings are always on my body, and especially in places where people are crowded
🚖 Traffic and airport precautions
  • SFO Airport Transportation:The use of BART trains is the most economical and convenient, Uber/Lift is also safe
  • Cable Car:Since it is for tourism, it is used for experience rather than practicality.
  • Hill road parking:When parking on a steep slope, the wheel must be turned toward the curb.
  • Public transportation:Clipper cards can be used both buses, trains and cable cars
  • Uber/Lift:It is widely used because it is cheaper and safer than taxi
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature is 15-20 degrees cool, the fog is often caught and the wind is strong, the long-sleeved must be required.
  • Spring/Autumn:The windshield jacket is required because there is a large crossover and a lot of wind.
  • winter:It is gentle but rainy, so prepare an umbrella or waterproof jacket
  • Mark Twain Pote:"The coldest winter I experienced was the summer of San Francisco" -Surpse in the summer
🚫 Airport Security Search Precautions
  • If it is not a TSA approval carrier, the carrier may be torn when searching for security.
  • Thorough luggage test to prevent terrorism, cooperation without friction as much as possible
  • Easily prepare to find when lost by attaching a name tag
