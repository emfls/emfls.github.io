⚠️Corch Cors Travel Precautions

🚨 altitude disease and healthism

Main danger altitude:Songkul Lake 3016m, Color Lake 3050m

  • History symptoms:Headaches, nausea, and dizziness immediately descend
  • Eat enough water:Easily occurs dehydration in the highlands, eating more than 3 liters of water a day
  • Adaptation slowly:Avoid rapid high altitude and take enough rest
  • No drinking:Alcohol intake in the highlands
🐎 Horse riding and activity safety
  • Helmet wear:Make sure to wear safety mothers during horseback riding, compliance local guide directors
  • Horse approach:Do not suddenly approach from the back of the horse and slowly approach the side
  • Beginner attention:If you have no horseback riding experience, you must start slowly with the guide
  • Proper dress:Wearing long pants and sneakers, prohibiting skirts or sandals
  • Check the weather:Stopping horseback riding activities in the rain or strong wind
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-September):20-25 degrees daytime, 5-10 degrees at night, and very big
  • Sudden weather change:Sudden rain and wind due to the nature of the highland area
  • UV rays:Strong UV rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
  • Dry air:Preparation of the nose and lip drying, lip balm and moisturizer
  • Night cold:The inside of the Eurate can also fall below 5 degrees
🍽️ Food and hygiene precautions
  • Dairy productism:Adaptation time for fermented milk products such as Kumis and Iran
  • Bottled water intake:Recommended for eating bottled water rather than tap water
  • Cooked enough meat:Intake only enough cooked, such as lamb and horse meat
  • Personal hygiene:Washing hand cleaning agent always carried before and after meals
