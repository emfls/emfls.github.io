⚠️Sanmigel travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:All areas around the bus terminal, interior of the market, night time zone

  • Violent crime:El Salvador is one of the most unstable countries in Latin America, murder and robbery.
  • Market area:The market is a dangerous area where violent crimes frequently occur.
  • Prohibition of going out at night:After the sun, never go out and stay in the hostel
  • Transportation:Prohibition of use of city buses, only the official registered radio taxi (yellow body, a license plate starting with A)
  • Cash possession:Prohibition of holding a lot of cash or valuables, only minimal cash
🏔️ Volcanic climbing precautions
  • Guide accompanying:When climbing San Migel Volcano, it must be moved with a certified guide
  • Active volcanoes:As an active volcano that works intermittently, it is necessary to confirm the weather situation.
  • Preparation of fitness:Preparing enough health with high-strength hiking that takes 4-6 hours to the summit
  • Check the weather:During the rainy season (May-October), the risk of climbing also increased
  • Equipment preparation:Climbing, sufficient water, snacks, raincoats
