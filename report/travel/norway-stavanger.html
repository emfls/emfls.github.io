⚠️Starbang Er Travel Precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Late at night, around the port, downtown

  • pickpocket:Sightseeing at tourist attractions, bus stops, museums, pickpockets, bags, and zipper lock
  • Fake guide/ticket:Informal guides or ticket sales attention at tourist destinations
  • Going out late at night:Be careful when moving alone, avoid quiet alleys
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transport strike:Due to the nature of Norwegian, sudden strike can be generated, alternative transportation check
  • Bus/Perry Ticket:City bus, ferry, cruise ticket pre -purchase recommendation
  • Taxi fee:Airport-about 400 ~ 600 nok, meter confirmation required
  • Train/Bus connection:Oslo (7 ~ 8 hours), Bergen (5 hours)
  • Hiking Safety:Meteorological confirmation when hiking, such as Frey Cestolen, Mountaineering and Waterproofing Dress
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature of 15-22 degrees, clear and cool, sudden rain
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature 0-7 degrees, rain and wind, waterproof jacket required
  • Spring/Autumn:Preparation
  • Coastal Wind:It can be low on the body temperature
  • ultraviolet rays:Northern Europe is also UV strong, sunscreen required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Port, Museum, Café and Restaurant Prices 2-3 times expensive
  • Museum entrance fee:Petrolium Museum 150 nok, canned museum 120 nok, etc.
  • Tip Culture:Norway is not a tip culture, included in the bill
  • Seafood dishes:Fresh but high price
