Major areas:Around the train station, outskirts of the city late at night

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing, bags, bags in the train station, major tourist attractions, plaza, etc.

🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Busy buses such as buses and trains are high, but it is necessary to check the interval between weekends and holidays
  • Cycling:Development of downtown and nearby bicycles, bicycle rentals
  • Taxi fee:Material confirmation required, cash and card payments are available
  • Train connection:Connect with Malmo (1 hour 30 minutes), Lund (1 hour), Copenhagen (2 hours), etc.
  • Natural terrain:Wetlands, rivers, and some promenade can be submerged in water, so check the bypass
🌡️Checks related to weather

  • Spring/Autumn:Moody weather, umbrella and outerwear required
  • Wetland influence:Recommended for waterproof shoes when visiting wetlands such as Baten Ricket
  • ultraviolet rays:Summer sunlight strong, sun cream required
