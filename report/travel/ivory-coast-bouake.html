  • Road status:There are many unpaved roads and mud to rain, 4WD car recommended
  • Public transportation:Bus time irregular and overload risk, preliminary negotiations for charges when using taxis
  • Lack of fuel:Difficulty in securing fuel due to lack of gas station, preparing for enough fuel
  • Avijang connection:Bow Ake-Abijang takes about 3-4 hours, prohibits nighttime operation
  • Traffic accident:Very complaints for traffic laws, wearing seat belts

  • drinking water:Prohibit tap water, drink only bottled water and avoid ice
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required near the equator
