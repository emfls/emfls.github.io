⚠️ Omaha travel precautions

🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car required:Omaha is a car -centered city, and public transportation is limited, so it is recommended to use car rental.
  • parking:Downtown and Old Market areas have a lot of paid parking, so the parking fee is prepared in advance.
  • Winter driving:There is a high risk of accidents caused by snow and ice, so careful driving is required
  • GPS required:Navigation is required because the city is wide and the lattice road structure
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature is 32 degrees, hot and humid, so enough water intake, sunglasses, and hats are required.
  • Spring/Autumn:Because of the large crossover and the windy, the clothes to overlap with the windbreaker
  • winter:It goes down to -7 degrees and snow is a lot, so thick coats, gloves, hats, and winter equipment required
  • Tornado Season:Torney in April-June may also occur, so carefully checked weather information
