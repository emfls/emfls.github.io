⚠️ Kahi travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Borders, night mountainous areas

  • Border area:Near the Russian and Georgia border, prohibit access without permission, observe the border guard
  • Wildlife:Wildlife such as bears and wolves in nature protection zones, guided accompanying mandatory
  • Mountain safety:As the weather changes are rapidly, enough preparation and local guides are accompanied by climbing.
  • Going out late at night:Due to the nature of the mountainous region, risk of going alone after 9 pm
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Lack of public transportation:Kachi is required to use car rental or taxi because public transportation is limited.
  • Mountain road:There are many winding mountain trails, preparing for motion sickness and wearing a seat belt
  • Fuel supply:Since there are rare gas stations, the fuel status is checked occasionally
  • Baku connection:It takes about 4-5 hours from Baku to Kachi, the middle rest area
  • Winter traffic:December-March, the possibility of road control due to eyes
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature is 15-25 degrees, but the mountainous region is cool
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature -5 ~ 5 degrees, snow falls a lot and cold
  • Spring/Autumn:Big and sudden showers, raincoats and outerwear are required
  • Mountain climate:Temperature difference is severe according to altitude,
  • Precipitation:Umbrella is required because it is 300-1600mm per year.
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash payment:Most of them are only available for cash payments, enough cash preparation
  • Guide cost:Natural Protection Zone Guide 50-100 Manat a day
  • Accommodation fee:Guest House 20-40 Manat, Hotel 60-120 Manat
  • Food expenses:Local restaurant 5-15 manat, 20-30 manats at tourist attractions
