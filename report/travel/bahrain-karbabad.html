⚠️ Carbabad travel precautions

🚨 Culture and religiousism

Islamic culture:Essential for conservative costumes and actions

  • Dress Regulations:Long pants, long sleeves, and women are required to wear hijabs when visiting the mosque
  • Friday Worship:Most of the stores are closed at 1-3 pm on Friday
  • Ramadan period:Prohibition of food intake in public places, changes in operating hours of restaurant
  • Drinking restriction:Can be drunk only in hotels and licensed places
  • Photo shooting:Local people, especially women, permission when shooting women
🌡️ Climate and health precautions
  • Extreme heat:Summer (May-September) temperature 40-50 degrees, enough water intake required
  • humidity:High humidity temperature is higher, recommended indoor activities
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and hat required
  • Desert:Dewatering caution due to dry air
  • sandstorm:Spring sand storms can occur
