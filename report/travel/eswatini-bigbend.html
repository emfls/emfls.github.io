⚠️ Big Bend Travel Precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Border area, late at night, city center center

  • Wildlife:Guide order in the nature protection area strictly observed, prohibit feeding to animals
  • Border pass:South Africa-Thorough preparation of passports and documents at the Border of Eswatini
  • Cash management:Restricted high cash restraint, hotel safe use recommendation
  • Going out late at night:Avoiding alone after sunset and recommending group activities
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Rent -a -car:Left traffic, international driver's license required, 4WD vehicle recommended
  • Public transportation:Speed ​​and overloading when using minibus taxis
  • Road status:Difficulties in traffic in the road in the rainy season, and the weather is required
  • fuel:The spacing of the gas station is far, so the fuel is well prepared
  • GPS:Recommended offline map download
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (11-3):The temperature is 20-30 degrees, the rainfall is high, the humidity is high due to the rainy season.
  • Winter (May-September):The temperature is 5-20 degrees, the dry weather with the dry season.
  • Preparation for the rainy season:Waterproof clothing and umbrella required, river water level rise
  • Stilly feature:Dust and dry, enough water intake
  • ultraviolet rays:UV strong, sunscreen is required due to the high altitude
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Malaria risks, prevention drugs and mosquitoes prevention
  • drinking water:Purchase of bottled water or boiled water, ice care
  • food:Street foodism, intake of food well -cooked
  • Medical facilities:Big Bend Hospital location check in advance

💡 Big Bend Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:The dry season in May-September is optimal for wild animal observation and outdoor activities
  • Safari outfit:Khaki color and beige clothing are not threatened by animals
  • Local language:Recommended for using Swahizer and English, basic greetings learning
