Day 1: Nature and History and Culture Exploration

Daedunsan Provincial Park → Wanju Hyanggyo → Sambong Seowon → Wanju Traditional Culture Experience Center

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:At Daedunsan, the representative mountain of Wanju -gun, Jeonbuk, the view of the magnificent rocky mountain through the Geumgang Bridge and Samseon Stairs, the history of traditional Confucian culture and education in Wanju Hyanggyo, which was founded in 1398, Joseon Dynasty, and explored the beauty of Korean traditional architecture in Sambongseowon in honor of the Tattoo of the Joseon Dynasty. Experience traditional craft culture in Jeonbuk, such as making Hanji and experiencing ceramics
📍Daedunsan Provincial Park 📍Wanju Hyanggyo 📍Sambong Seowon 📍 Wanju Traditional Culture Experience Center

Day 2: Natural Recreation and Healing Experience

Gosan Natural Recreation Forest → Wibong Falls → Wanju Wild Food Festival → Moaksan Provincial Park

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:At Gosan Natural Recreation Forest, located in Gosan -myeon, Wanju -gun, you can experience forest baths and natural healing in the cypress forest road and clear valley, and at 60m high Wibong Falls, cool the summer heat with cool water, and enjoy the magnificence of nature. At the Moaksan Provincial Park, you can experience mental training through Buddhist culture and mountain trekking with Geumsansa Temple.
📍 Gosan Natural Recreation Forest 📍Wibong Falls 📍 Wanju Wild Food Festival 📍Moaksan Provincial Park

Day 3: Rural Experience and Tour

Wanju Local Food Store → Samrye Culture and Arts Village → Wanju Cheese Village → Wanju County Office

Wanju Local Food Store:It takes 2 hours, purchases fresh agricultural products and processed foods produced by Wanju -gun farmers at a reasonable price
Samrye Culture and Arts Village:3 hours, participate in exhibitions, performances, and experience programs in a complex cultural space that renovated the old Yanggok warehouse
Wanju Cheese Village:2 hours, European cheese manufacturing process tour and direct cheese making experience
Wanju County Office:It takes 1 hour, exploring regional development awards and future plans in the administrative center of Wanju -gun
