  • Motorcycle Taxi:Wearing helmet when using Gemijang (motorcycle taxi)

  • fuel:The spacing of the gas station is far, so the fuel is well prepared
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (11-3):25-35 degrees of temperature, dry and hot, enough water intake
  • Rainy (4-10):Temperature 22-30 degrees, wet and heavy rainfall
  • Harmatan:Dry wind blowing from the Sahara Desert in December-February
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and hat
  • dehydration:High risk of dehydration due to high temperature and humidity

  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical infrastructure, moving to Kotonu, the capital of serious illness.
