⚠️ Lepkada travel precautions

🌡️ Mediterranean climate precautions

Summer:The temperature from June-September is high and dry, so enough water intake and UV protection required

  • Sunlight burnism:When you play on the beach, drink a lot of water and be careful with sunlight burns.
  • UV protection:It is essential to apply sunscreen and take a hat and sunglasses because the sun is strong.
  • Moisture intake:Dehydrate prevention with enough water in hot weather
  • Winter:December-February is gentle, but it rains a lot of umbrella required
🌊 Marine activity safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:Check the waves and algae status when swimming on the beach
  • Snorkeling:Maintain safe distances when meeting various marine life
  • Boat Tour:Wearing safety equipment when using cruises
