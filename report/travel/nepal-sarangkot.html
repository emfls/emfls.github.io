🚨 altitude disease and healthism

Main precautions:1,600m above sea level, alpine zone, rapid change in temperature

  • Altitude disease:Headaches, nausea, dizziness symptoms immediately descending, enough water intake
  • Temperature change:It is required for insulation because it is 5-10 degrees dawn and 20-25 degrees daytime.
  • ultraviolet rays:Sweet cream and sunglasses are required with strong ultraviolet rays
