⚠️Mandera travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:All areas within 60km of Somalia (3 stages of travel restrictions)

  • Terrorism:It is extremely dangerous, absolute prohibition of visiting tourism purposes in terrorist group activities such as Al Shabab
  • Armed intensity:Armed with automatic rifles such as AK-47, the risk of life when resistance
  • Kidnapping:Provision of the abduction case for foreigners, absolute prohibition
  • Night going out:Absolutely prohibited to go out after sunset, atmosphere in the accommodation
  • Local guide:Be sure to accompany government official guides, prohibit individual action
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:There are many unpaved roads and cannot be passed in the rainy season, 4WD vehicle required
  • Public transportation:High risk of theft and accident when using Matotu
  • flight:Nairobi-Mandara direct flight limited, pre-booking required
  • Fuel supply:Difficulty securing fuel due to lack of gas station
  • checkpoint:Many border checkpoints, ID cards are always portable
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (12-3):Temperature of 25-40 degrees, extremely dried, dehydration
  • Woogi (April-May, October-November):Flood risk, road blocking
  • Desert:Big and sand breeze
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays, burns near the equator
  • dust:Dust climate, dusty, respiratory diseaseism
💰 Price related precautions
  • High prices:Due to the nature of the border area, the price of daily necessities is high
  • Cash payment:Limited credit card use, enough cash preparation
  • Exchange:Lack of currency exchange, exchanging in advance in Nairobi
  • Tip Culture:Tips required for guides and drivers
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Malaria occurring areas, prevention drugs required
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical facilities, transportation of Nairobi for emergencies
  • drinking water:Prohibition of drinking tap water, only bottled water intake
  • food:Street food intake, food poisoning danger
