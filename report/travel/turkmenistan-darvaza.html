Darbaza gas craterIs one of the world's most mysterious natural phenomena located in the central part of the Turkmenistan Desert. This huge pit, discovered by Soviet geologists in 1971 during natural gas exploration, has been burning for more than 50 years, gaining the nickname "Gate to Hell."

  • location:Turkmenistan Ahal, Darbaza Village 10km north of Village
  • size:About 70 meters in diameter, 20 meters in depth
  • distance:260 km north of Ashigarvart
