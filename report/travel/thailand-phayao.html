⚠️Payao travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night market, bus terminal, dawn and night lakeside

  • pickpocket:Liberalism, bags in the market, night market, and bus terminal
  • Motorcycle accident:Wearing a helmet when renting a motorcycle
  • Night movement:Rare place, do not go alone in the lake around the lake
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Bus, minivan, motorcycle taxi, check in advance
  • Motorcycle rental:International license required, accident and stolenism
  • Traffic strike/delay:Local buses sometimes delay, secure preliminary schedules
  • Walking:Around the lake, there are many plains, outskirts and mountainous lands on hills and dirt roads.
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (November -February):15 ~ 28 degrees, morning evening chrysanthemum, cloud and trekking optimal
  • Hot Chul (March -April):22 ~ 35 degrees, day heat, Songlan Festival
  • Rainy (May -October):20 ~ 32 degrees, showers frequent, umbrella and raincoats required
  • ultraviolet rays:Sunlight strong, sunscreen and hat required
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Café and restaurant prices around the lake 1.5 ~ 2 times expensive
  • Employees and park admissions:Most free, some national parks and waterfalls 100 ~ 200 baht
  • Tip Culture:Restaurant / Hotel 10 ~ 20 Baht Small Tips Recommended
  • Market food:It's cheap but hygienism

💡 Payao Travel Tips

  • Free tourist destination:Kwan Payao Walk, Wat Seacom Cam, Pocun Ngam Muang Statue, Van Bua Village
  • Northern food:Khaoso, sweat, grill fish, local noodles, etc.
  • Optimal visits:November -February (dry season, cloud, festival), April (Songlan)
  • Bicycle/Motorcycle:Recommended for riding around the lake and rural road
  • Employee dress:Shoulder and knee covering clothes, shoes take off and enter
  • National Park Accommodation:Pre -booking camping and bungalow
  • Market use:Morning market and night market, fresh fruits, snacks, and handicraft shopping
  • Photo spot:Kwan Payao Sunrise, Sunrise, Puranca Observation Deck, Wat Nantaram Wooden Architecture
