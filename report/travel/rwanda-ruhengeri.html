⚠️ Lu Hengri travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, typhoids

  • malaria:Essential to take prevention drugs as mosquito -mediated diseases, wear long -sleeved clothes and mosquito repellents
  • yellow fever:Essential submitted when entering a vaccination certificate, 10 days after inoculation
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice, peel off fruits
  • Altitude disease:Areas 1,500m or more, enough water intake and rest
🦍 Gorilla trekking precautions
  • permit:Pre -booking required, only 8 people per day allowed
  • Minimum distance:Gorilla is required to maintain more than 7m
  • No flash:Absolute prohibition of camera flash
  • Cold symptoms:If you have a cold or body, you can't participate
  • Age limit:Only 15 years of age can participate
