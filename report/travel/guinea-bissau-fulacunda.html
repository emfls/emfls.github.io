⚠️Full Racunda Travel Note

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the market, late at night

  • Political instability:Guinea Beach Sau is a country with frequent coups and political instability.
  • Drug trafficking:Notorously avoiding suspicious situations,
  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing, distributed valuables in markets and tourist attractions,
  • Night going out:Prohibition of going alone after the sun, recommending group activities
  • Local guide:Recommended to travel with reliable local guides
🏥 Health and medical precautions
  • malaria:Malaria dangerous areas throughout the year, taking prevention drugs and mosquito prevention
  • yellow fever:Essentials for vaccination of yellow fever disease, certificate test when entering Korea
  • drinking water:Prohibition of drinking tap water, drinking water or purified water
  • food:Street foodism, only intake of completely cooked food
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical infrastructure, and in case of serious illness needs to be transferred to a nearby country
  • cholera:Risk of cholera generation, thorough hygiene management
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, difficulty moving in rainy season
  • Public transportation:Lack of public transportation facilities, use of personal vehicles or taxis
  • fuel:Unstable fuel supply, securing sufficient fuel
  • communication:Mobile phone signal instability, consideration of satellite calls
  • Boat safety:Wearing life jackets for mangrove tours
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Rainy (June-October):Rain and flood risks, restrictions on moving
  • Dry (12-5):High temperature drying, enough water intake
  • humidity:Over the year, hot and humidity, heat stroke
  • Harmatan:December-March Sahara Desert sand breeze, respiratory attention
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet, UV protection required near the equator
💰 Economic precautions
  • Cash -oriented:Limited credit card use, enough cash preparation
  • Exchange:Local currency exchange, euro is also available in US dollars
  • ATM:Lack of ATM facilities, preparation of cash in advance
  • price:Imported goods are expensive and low in local goods
