⚠️ Nokia travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night forest area, around the remote lake

  • Wildlife:The possibility of encountering wild animals such as bears and wolves in the forest, food sealing is required
  • Lake safety:Recommended water temperature change during swimming, recommended to wear life jackets
  • Lost prevention:GPS required for hiking, return before sunset
  • Emergency situation:Finnish emergency phone 112, English communications available
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:From Tampere to Nokia, about 20 minutes by train, on -time operation
  • VR ticket:Reservation in advance in the Finnish National Railway VR app
  • Taxi fee:About 40-50 euros from Tampere to Nokia
  • Rent -a -car:International driver's license required, winter snow tires obligations
