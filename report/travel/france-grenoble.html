⚠️ Grenoble travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Grenoble Station, Notre Dame Square, Cable Car Road, Tourist Destination

  • Pickpocketing tourist attractions:Cable Cable Car Waiting for photography in Bastille fortress
  • On the bicycle tour:Be sure to use lock when parking bicycles
  • Around the museum:Minimize wallet exposure when purchasing tickets
  • How to deal with:Valuits are also carried by hotel safe, minimal cash and cards.
🏔️ High and climate precautions
  • Cautions for breathing due to a high altitude difference of 214 meters when climbing the Bastille Fortress
  • Temperature changes are very severe near the Alps
  • Mandatory winter products when visiting ski resorts in winter
  • Even in the summer, it is chilly in the evening, so prepare a long outer clothes
🚴‍♂️ Bicycle Tour Precautions
  • Mvélo+ obligation to wear a helmet when renting a bicycle
  • Some of the 450km bicycle roads are steep slopes.
  • Cycling time return time (additional fee for extension)
  • Used as a traffic rules compliance and bicycle -only
🚡 Caution for cable cars
  • Leskul cable car can be stopped depending on the weather
  • In the peak season, the waiting time may be longer
  • In case of prior phobia, it is necessary to consider
  • You can also use the walking course when you descend from the Bastille Fortress.
🍽️ Meals related precautions
  • Recommended for reservation for historical cafes such as Café de la Table Ronde, which has been operated since 1739
  • Prior survey of eco -friendly restaurants such as vegan restaurant Le Ro Cafe
  • Allergies related to Grenoble Walnut (AOC)
