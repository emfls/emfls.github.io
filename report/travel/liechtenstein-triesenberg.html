⚠️ Trisenberg travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night mountainous areas, hiking trail remote sections

  • General security:Liechtenstein is one of the safest countries in the world and has a very low crime rate.
  • Mountain safety:Hiking should be careful about changes in the weather and do not climb alone
  • Emergency situation:Emergency call 112, Mountain Rescue Team 1414
  • Wildlife:Rarely, but the possibility of appearance of bears or wolves, food storage attention
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The bus operation is long, so the schedule is required in advance.
  • LIEMOBIL Bus:Liechtenstein's national bus integrated ticket
  • Taxi fee:It is very expensive and recommended to use public transportation
  • Rent -a -car:Additional insurance confirmation when passing the Swiss-Austrian border
  • Mountain road:Winter chain obligations, steepness attention even in summer
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature is 15-25 degrees, afternoon showers frequently occur
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature -5-5 degrees, snow down a lot, winter supplies required
  • Spring/Autumn:More than 15 degrees of cross -day crossing, preparation of layered costumes
  • Highland characteristics:The temperature is 6 degrees low from 1000m above sea level
  • ultraviolet rays:Highlands strong ultraviolet rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
