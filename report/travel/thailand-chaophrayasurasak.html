🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Zao Praya Sura Sura is a hot and humid tropical climate all year round, so it is especially important in summer.

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of the riverside area, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquito repellents and long -sleeved clothes
🚤 Precautions when using water traffic
  • Chao Fraya Express Boat:Seat belt is required as a major means of transportation
  • Check the boat timetable:Check the boat operation time and route in advance and pay attention to the last train
  • Life jacket:Be sure to wear life jackets when participating in water activities
  • Personal belongings:The belongings that can fall into the water when using the boat are stored in the waterproof bag.
  • Long Tail Boat:Use after checking safety equipment during private tours
🍽️ Food and hygiene precautions
  • Street food:Select a place with a good hygiene when eating local street food
  • Bottled water drinking:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • Iceism:It is safe to avoid the ice of drinks sold on the street
  • Spicy food:Thai food can be very spicy, so you can order it at first
  • Riverside food:Consumption after checking freshness when using a riverside restaurant
🛕 Religion and cultural courtesy
  • When visiting the temple:Wearing clothes covering your shoulders and knees, taking off your shoes and entering
  • Buddhist shooting:Taking a picture with a Buddha statue is considered a rude behavior.
  • Prohibit the touch of the head:In Thailand, the head is sacred, so it prohibits the touch of others' heads
  • To the toes:It is rude to make the toes to the Buddha or the person
  • Royal Respect:Essential expression of respect for the royal family of Thailand, especially when visiting the royal palace
💰 Shopping and bargaining precautions

When shopping in Chinatown and traditional markets, bargaining is common, and it is a good idea to start from half of the first offering price. When purchasing expensive goods, check carefully whether it is genuine.

🚨 Safety precautions for riverside area
  • Night safety:Riverside areas do not go alone at night, but use bright lights.
  • Pickpocket attention:Royal Palace, where there are many tourists
  • Riverside walk:Pay attention to slippery section when using the riverside trails
