⚠️ Long Beach Travel Precautions

🌊 Beach safetyism
  • Waves:Watching waves and lifting guards on the beach and instructions for life guards
  • Swimming experience:If you lack swimming experience, take safety measures and wear life jackets
  • Shipmentism:Evacuate to the beach immediately in the event of a strong current or
  • Emergency situation:911 Report, Korea Consulate Contact Preview
☀️ Precautions for strong sunlight
  • UV protection:Long Beach is a city with a large crossover.
  • Protection equipment:Wear a hat or sunglasses to protect your eyes and head
  • Moisture intake:Eat enough water to prevent dehydration
  • Use shade:In the afternoon, take a break in the shade
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Long Beach is developing a public transportation system to avoid traffic jams.
  • Bicycle use:Provide a lot of bicycle rental services, wear protection hair and compliance with traffic laws
  • Free shuttle:Long Beach Transit's free shuttle bus can be used in downtown
  • Parking fee:Check the parking fee around the tourist attractions in advance
🔒 Precious crime precautions
  • Protection of belongings:Hide valuable objects from caring in the car
  • Many people:Keep your bags in tourist attractions and prohibit a lot of cash
  • Night time attention:Do not go alone for safety at night
  • Respect for local culture:It is a city where various cultures coexist, respect for the culture and customs of local residents
