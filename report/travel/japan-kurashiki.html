⚠️Kurashiki travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the station, rare alley at night

  • pickpocket:In the tourist attractions, stations, and markets, bags are carried forward and zipper lock
  • Bicycle/pedestrian accident:Japan has a lot of bicycles, so be careful on foot
  • Fake guide:Fake ticket sales at tourist attractions, illegal guide attention
  • Going out late at night:Avoid rare people, compliance with accommodation time
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transport strike/delay:Japan is sometimes delayed, alternative transportation
  • IC Card (Suika/Ikoka):Transportation card preparation, check the balance
  • Taxi fee:The basic fare is high, the meter check is required
  • Walking:Restrictions on entering vehicles in the aesthetic district, a lot of walking movements
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):25-33 degrees, moist and hot, enough water intake
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature 2-10 degrees, gentle but windy
  • Spring/Autumn:A large crossover, an umbrella and outerwear preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:Spring and summer sunlight, sunscreen required
  • Rainy/typhoon:The rainy season in June -July, the possibility of a typhoon in September -October
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Café, restaurant prices in the aesthetic district may be 2-3 times more expensive
  • Museum entrance fee:2,000 yen of Ohara Museum of Art, Folk Crafts Museum 1,200 yen, etc.
  • Tip Culture:Japan has no tip culture, but thank you for forgetting
  • Local specialty:Denim, paper tape, and backdo -related product prices check
