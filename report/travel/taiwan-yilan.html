⚠️ Iran travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night market, downtown, around the station

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing at night markets, train stations, etc.
  • motorcycle:Motorcycleism during the road crossing, walking inside India
  • Late at night:Late time alone moving alone
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Train/Bus:Reservation, use Easy Card in advance, and check alternative transportation when traffic strikes occur
  • drive:International driver's license required, motorcycle/vehicle driving attention to accidents
  • Hot Springs:Prior reservation recommendation, water temperature check
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-September):25-35 degrees of temperature, high humidity, water intake required
  • Winter (12-2):Gion temperature 15-20 degrees, non-frequent, warm clothes preparation
  • Spring/Autumn:A large crossover, an umbrella/outerwear preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:Sunlight strong, sunscreen/hat required
💰 Price and other precautions
  • Tourist destination:Night market, tourist spot cafe/restaurant price 2-3 times expensive
  • Tip Culture:Taiwan has no tip culture, including service charges
  • Tap water:Recommended for drinking water, recommendation of bottled water
  • Prohibited items:Prohibition of importing fruits, dairy products, meat, e -cigarettes, etc.
