⚠️Rautanakiri travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Main precautions:Malaria, dengue fever, and Zika virus

  • Malaria prevention:Receive malaria prevention drugs at hospitals before departure, use mosquito repellent required
  • Drinking water safety:Drink only bottled water, Iceism, avoid raw fruit and vegetables
  • Emergency situation:There is a hospital in Banlong, but the facility is limited, and if it is serious, it is necessary to transfer Phnom Penh.
  • Bug prevention:Long -sleeved, long pants, especially dawn and evening time zone attention
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are a lot of dirt roads, so I recommend using SUV vehicles, and worsening roads in the rainy season
  • Mobile time:From Phnom Pen to Van Long, about 6-8 hours, fluctuating depending on the road situation
  • Public transportation:Use a minibus or taxi, waiting for passengers without a fixed schedule.
  • Motorcycle rental:Need for international driver's license, mandatory helmet wearing, rough topography
  • Domestic flight:No domestic flight between Phnom Pen-Banlong, only land movement
