Day 1: Honey Old Town and Venetian port exploration

Venetian Port → Honey Old Town → Honey Central Market → Yanisari Moscow → Port Sunset

Recommended time:8 hours
Core points:Explore the Old Venetian port, where the traces of the Venetian era remain in Honey, a city of Crete. Enjoy the romantic atmosphere of the Mediterranean with a beautiful lighthouse, and experience the unique charm of the Venice's architecture and traditional Greek culture in the Old Town with a narrow alleyway. In the evening, spend a romantic time watching the fantastic sunset from the port.

Day 2: Ella Beach and Balos Beach Tour

Ella Ponishi Pink Sand Beach → Balos Beach → Grambusan Island → Coastal Drive → Ki Samos

Recommended time:9 hours
Core points:Enjoy the fantastic harmony between the unique pink sand and the emerald sea on the Ellaponish Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. On the Balos Beach, you will meet the most impressive Lagoon's scenery on Crete and experience the breathtaking landscape of the Mediterranean with the historical fortress of Grambusan Island. Create unforgettable memories from these beaches that can be approached or hiking through boat cruises.

⚠️Hania travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists, including the Central Market of Hania, around Venetian ports

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Conscious attention:When walking on the road or taking pictures,
  • Bag management:Put the bag in front and lock the zipper
  • Tourististism:Especially in famous tourist destinations and public transportation
🎫 Preliminary reservation required
  • Balos Beach Boat Tour:In the peak season, it is possible to cancel the flight depending on the pre -booking required and the weather
  • Samaria canyon trekking:Winter (November-April) Closed, pre-weather check
  • Ella Ponishi Beach Tour:Recommended for pre -booking bus tours, restrictions on parking spaces when using private vehicles
  • Cooking workshop:Experience at local farms needs to be reserved at least two days ago
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Athens connection:Hania is 1 hour from Athens and 9 hours by ferry
  • Hania-Ella Ponishi:The bus dispatch interval is long and the travel time takes about 2 hours
  • Recommended for using car rentals:Rent -a -cars are the most convenient for beaches and canyons access.
  • Taxi:About 10 euros from Soda Port to downtown Honey, and 1.7 euros for buses.
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Mediterranean Climate:The Mediterranean Climate, which is mild in winter and hot summer
  • Summer preparation:It's very hot in summer, so sunscreen, hat, enough water required
  • Winter preparation:Winter is gentle, but it rains a lot, so prepare an umbrella
  • Windism:The beach area may be strong, so prepare windproof jacket
🗣️ Language communication

Although Greek is official language, English can be communicated in tourist attractions. Knowing the basic Greek greetings will help you communicate with locals.
