📅 Annaba 2-Night 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Historical and Ancient Ruins Exploration

Hippo Regius Ruins → Annaba Old Town → Saint Augustine Basilica → Annaba Beach

Recommended Duration:9 hours
Key Points:Explore the ancient Roman-era Hippo Regius ruins in Annaba on Algeria's northeastern Mediterranean coast and experience the historical site where Saint Augustine served as bishop, admire the fusion of Arab-Berber culture with French culture in the old town where French colonial architecture remains, explore traces of Christian history at the 19th-century Saint Augustine Basilica, and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the North African coast at Annaba Beach with its blue Mediterranean waters
📍 Hippo Royalty 📍 Annaba Old Town 📍 Saint Augustine Basilica 📍 Annab Beach

Day 2: Nature and Coastal Culture Experience

Seraidi Beach → ENBIE → Annab Mhueum → La Corne Beach

Recommended Duration:10 hours
Key Points:Enjoy swimming and sunbathing with clear Mediterranean waters and golden sandy beaches at Seraidi Beach, 10km east of Annaba, explore Berber traditional village culture and beautiful natural mountain scenery at El Bouni near Annaba, view various historical artifacts from the ancient Numidian Kingdom through the Ottoman Empire to the French colonial period at Annaba Museum, and experience North African coastal culture by tasting traditional Algerian seafood dishes with Mediterranean sunset at La Corne Beach
📍 Seraidi Beach 📍 el bouni 📍 Annaba Museum 📍 La Corne Beach

Day 3: Nearby Exploration and Cultural Experience (Optional)

Taref Day Tour → Skikda → Jijel → Annaba Center Market

Taref:4 hours, admire Berber traditional culture and magnificent Atlas Mountains natural scenery in the mountain city 60km south of Annaba
In Skik:3 hours, explore the petroleum industry and Mediterranean trade center in the port city 100km west of Annaba
Jijel:2 hours, experience traditional fishing culture and Berber architectural styles in the small coastal town near Annaba
Annaba Central Market:1 hour, shop for Algerian specialties including local spices, traditional crafts, and Berber carpets, and engage in cultural exchange with locals

⚠️ Annaba Travel Precautions

🚨 Security and Safety Precautions

Main Areas of Concern:Old town late at night, outskirt areas

  • PickPocketing:Beware of pickpockets at central market and tourist sites; carry bags in front and keep zippers closed
  • Scam Alert:Beware of fake guides or merchants demanding excessive prices
  • Female Travelers:Extra caution needed for solo travel due to conservative Islamic culture
  • Late Night Outings:Avoid walking alone after sunset; group activities recommended
🚌 Transportation and Movement Precautions
  • Public Transport:Pre-confirm fares and negotiate when using buses and taxis
  • Car Rental:International driving permit required; be cautious of poor road conditions
  • Taxi Use:Confirm meter usage or negotiate fare in advance
  • Flight Connections:Domestic connections available to Algiers, Oran, Constantine
  • Road Conditions:Poor road conditions in some mountain areas; be cautious during rainy season
🌡️ Weather-Related Precautions
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 25-35°C, very hot and dry; adequate hydration essential
  • Winter (December-February):Temperature 10-18°C, mild but frequent rain
  • Spring/Fall:Large temperature differences and possible sandstorms
  • Mediterranean Climate:Dry with strong sunlight
  • UV Rays:Strong North African sunlight; sunscreen and hat essential
💰 Price-Related Precautions
  • Tourist Area Overcharging:High prices at cafes and restaurants near ruins and beaches
  • Museum Entrance Fees:Annaba Museum 500 Dinars, Hippo Regius 300 Dinars
  • Tipping Culture:10% at restaurants, 1000-2000 dinars for guides
  • Cash Usage:Mostly cash transactions; limited card acceptance

💡 Annaba Travel Tips

  • Free Attractions:Annaba Beach, old town street walks, Saint Augustine Basilica exterior free
  • Algerian Food:Must try North African traditional foods like Couscous, Tajine, Brik, Merguez
  • Best Visit Time:March-May, September-November are best for weather and sightseeing
  • Languages:Arabic and Berber are official languages; French is also widely used
  • Historic City:Annaba is the city of Saint Augustine and successor to ancient Hippo Regius
  • Coastal Culture:Unique cultural fusion region where Mediterranean and African cultures meet
  • Islamic Culture:Restaurant operating restrictions during daytime hours of Ramadan
  • Fridays:Islamic Friday prayer day with shortened operating hours for some shops and tourist sites
