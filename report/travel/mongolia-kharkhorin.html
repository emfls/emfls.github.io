⚠️Harhholin travel precautions

🚨 Safety and healthism

Main precautions:Highlands, rapid temperature changes, wild animals

  • Altitude disease:At 1,400m above sea level
  • Temperature difference:Even in summer, it can fall below freezing at night, insulation clothing required
  • Wildlife:Wolf, wild dogs, etc.
  • Horse riding safety:Mongolian horse can be rough, so guide instructions are thoroughly observed
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:350km from Ulaanbaatar to Harhrin, there are many unpaved roads.
  • Mobile time:5-6 hours one way, preparation for motion sickness
  • Fuel supply:Limited and sufficient fuel confirmation
  • GPS limit:GPS signal instability at the beginning, local guide accompanying
  • Vehicle failure:Preparation of risks of vehicles, preliminary parts and repair tool
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Day 25 degrees, 5 degrees at night, 20 degrees or more
  • Winter (12-2):Minus 20-30 degrees, extreme cold attention
  • Spring/Autumn:Sudden snow purple and sand breeze can occur
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong UV rays, sunscreen and sunglasses required
  • Dryness:Very dry climate, lip balm and moisturizer required
