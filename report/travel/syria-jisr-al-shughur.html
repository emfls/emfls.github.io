  • Ministry of Foreign Affairs Travel Alert:Currently, the entire Syrian region

  • Night traffic prohibition:Restriction on going out after sunset, accompanied by local guides
  • Photo shooting:Military facilities, absolute prohibition of shooting government buildings

  • Public transportation:Bus and taxi operation irregularities, preliminary reservations required

  • Lack of fuel:It may be longer at the gas station
