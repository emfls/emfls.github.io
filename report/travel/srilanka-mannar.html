⚠️Manar travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night time remote areas, near border

  • Wildlife:Dangerous wildlife, such as elephants and leopards in the national park
  • Marine safety:Wearing life jackets for boat tours
  • Religious consideration:Wear appropriate clothes when visiting the cathedral and temples
  • Night going out:Avoid staying alone in the late night and only near the hostel
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:The bus operation is irregular, so move the time to spare time
  • Use Tuk Tuk:Preliminary negotiations required, if there is no meter
  • Rent -a -car:Pay attention to the left traffic, road condition
  • Boat Tour:Can be canceled depending on the weather situation
  • Safari Tour:Wearing a seat belt
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (12-3):25-32 degrees, tourism optimal, enough water intake
  • Woogi (May-September):Temperature 24-30 degrees, sudden showers, umbrella required
  • Transition (April, October-November):Weather change
  • Strong ultraviolet light:UV strong, sunscreen required throughout the year
  • humidity:High humidity and high temperature
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Restaurants around tourist destinations, souvenir stores are high
  • Safari Tour:A day tour with a guide $ 50-80
  • Boat Tour:Dolphin Observation Tour $ 30-50
  • Tip Culture:$ 10-15 guide, $ 5-10 driver

💡 Manar Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:December-March is the dry season, optimal for wild animal observation and marine activities
  • Local food:Sri Lanka traditional food experience such as Fish Curry, Cour Loti and Hopper
  • Wildlife Observation:Early morning (6-8 o'clock) and late afternoon (4-6 o'clock)
  • Flamingo season:October-March this flamingo observation optimal
  • Dolphin observation:Tour, which departs early in the morning, is high
  • Baobab tree:If you visit at sunset time, you can take a more beautiful picture
  • Local guide:Recommended local guides for wildlife and history explanation
  • Religious culture:Multicultural areas where Hinduism, Buddhism, and Christianity coexist
