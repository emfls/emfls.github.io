Majorism:Malaria, dengue fever, yellow fever, water -person disease

  • Emergency situation:Local hospital location in advance, travel insurance contact information
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Many unpaved roads, the road condition deteriorates during the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Speed ​​and overloading when using minibus (Matotu)
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommended, local driving habits
  • Night movement:Avoid as much as possible, lack of road lighting and wildlife
  • fuel:Gas station spacing, well prepared for fuel
