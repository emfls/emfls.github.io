⚠️ Nickeli travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, dengue fever, Zika virus

  • malaria:Nicheli is a malaria dangerous area, requiring prevention drugs and mosquito repellents.
  • yellow fever:When entering Gaia, it is necessary to bring a certificate
  • drinking water:Avoid drinking water and avoid drinks with ice
  • Wildlife:Dangerous animalism such as snakes and crocodiles in mangrove forests and natural parks
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:There are many road flooding and portholes in the rainy season, 4WD car recommended
  • Public transportation:Speed ​​and overloading when using minibus
