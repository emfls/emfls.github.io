⚠️Meru Travel Precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Majorism:Malaria, yellow fever, typhoids

  • Malaria prevention:Take malaria prevention drugs from a week before departure, use mosquito repellent required
  • Yellow fever vaccination:Kenyan entry requirements, effective after 10 days after vaccination
  • Drinking water:Only drink bottled water, avoid drinks with ice
  • Wildlife:Do not go out of the vehicle in Safari, strictly observed guide instructions
🦁 Safari safety rules
  • Stay in the car:Never go out of the vehicle during the game drive
  • Noise:Loud no sound when observing wild animals or sudden movement
  • Guide Direction:Absolutely observed instructions in the experienced safari guide
  • Appropriate distance:Maintaining a safe distance of at least 25 meters with wild animals
  • No flash:Absolutely prohibited use of flash when shooting wild animals
🌡️ Climate -related precautions
  • Dry (June-October, December-March):Temperature 20-28 degrees, safari optimal time, animal observation

  • Equatorial climate:Ultraviolet strong, sunscreen and hat required throughout the year
  • altitude:Meru is 300-900m above sea level, low risk of altitude disease
