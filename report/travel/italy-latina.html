⚠️ Latina travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists such as Latina train station, tourist attractions, and beaches

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Bag management:The bag is carried forward and locked the zipper, especially in the crowds.
  • Beachism:Thoroughly managed belongings when swimming or sunbathing on the beach
  • Crossback use:Prevention of pickpockets using cross bags rather than backpacks
🎫 Prior reservation recommended
  • Palmarola Island Boat Tour:In peak season, pre -booking required, especially weekends and holidays
  • Ninpa's garden:Prior reservation is recommended because there is a restriction on the number of people
  • Paragliding Experience:It operates according to the weather, so preliminary confirmation and reservation required
  • Rome Vatican Museum:First of all, the waiting time is reduced by pre -booking
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Roman connection:Latina is about 1 hour by train from Rome
  • Coastal area moving:Spherlonga and Gaeta recommended bus or car rental
  • Boat Tour:Areas such as Palmarola Island can be accessible only by boat tour.
  • Summer traffic:In the peak season, the coastal road is very crowded, so leave time to leave
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):High temperatures and strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
  • Optimal period:Spring (April-June) and autumn (September-October) are the most pleasant travel time
  • Beach activity:Sufficient water intake and shade rest during summer beach activities
  • In preparation for rain:Preparation of umbrella in preparation for sudden showers
🗣️ Language communication

Italian is official language, but English can be communicated in tourist attractions. Since Latina is near Rome, there is an international atmosphere that creates an environment where you can communicate in various languages.
