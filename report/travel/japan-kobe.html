⚠️ Kobe travel precautions

🚇 Precautions on transportation

Transportation convenience:Kobe is a city located between the mountains and the sea, and there are many hill roads.

  • Kobe City Loop Bus:Economic when using a pass on a one -day pass on a bus that circulates major tourist attractions.
  • Cable Car use:Locosan cable cars can be stopped depending on the weather, so check
  • Hill Road Caution:Kitano Lee Jin -gwan area has many hills and stairs, so comfortable shoes are required.
  • Subway connection:As many railway companies such as JR, Hankyu, and Hanshin operate, check the route map.
🏨 Precautions when booking
  • Reservation in advance:Sanomiya and Moto Machi are very popular near Motomachi, so there may be no rooms in the peak season a month ago.
  • Hotel selection:Various options from business hotels to luxury hotels
  • Location consideration:Sanomiya, Moto Machi, and Harbor Land's lodging convenience for sightseeing
  • Arima Onsen:Reservation in advance when staying at hot spring ryokan and checking the rule of use of hot springs
🌡️Checks and costumes
  • Spring (April-June):Travel recommendation, light jacket preparation as the most pleasant time
  • Summer (July-September):UV protection required to be well ventilated with hot and humid weather
  • Autumn (September-November):Good to travel with cool and pleasant weather
  • Winter (12-3):The cold and windy winds are required for winter products and windscreen
🍽️ Food and cultural precautions
  • Cash use:In small shops and restaurants, there are many places where only cash is received.
  • Kobe Beef:Orthodox Kobe Beef is very expensive, so it is necessary to consider budget.
  • Restaurant reservation:Popular restaurants and Kobe beef specialty stores are required for advance reservations.
  • Tip Culture:Japan does not have a tip culture, so you don't have to give it a separate tip.
🗣️ Language and manner precautions
  • Japanese translation app:Tourist destinations are English, but for detailed communication, the translation app is prepared
  • Garbage processing:There is almost no trash can on the street, so prepare a small plastic bag
  • Public transportation manners:Do not speak loudly on the train, to give up priority
  • International atmosphere:Kobe is an international city, so understanding and respecting various cultures
💳 Payment and shopping precautions
  • Card payment:Large stores can be paid for cards, but small shops only receive cash.
  • Tax -free shopping:Duty -free tax exemption when purchased more than 5,000 yen, it is required to bring a passport
  • Business hours:Most of the shops are closed at 8-9 pm, so consider the shopping time
  • souvenir:Kobe's specialties include Kobe Wine, Kobe Pudding, and Kobe Chocolate
