⚠️ Matsuyama travel precautions

♨️ Precautions when using Dogo Hot Springs

Hot Spring etiquette:You must wash your body before bathing, and you shouldn't wear a swimsuit.

  • Towel use:Be careful not to put a towel in the water
  • Tattoo limit:If there is a tattoo, the position may be limited, so check on the official website before visit.
  • Towel preparation:Dogo Hot Springs is not provided, so it is paid or purchased for a fee from the stalls of each pipe
  • Yukata Experience:After the hot spring, it is common to walk around the surrounding streets with Yukata.
🏰 Precautions when visiting Matsuyama Castle
  • Comfortable shoes required:The length of the castle is steep and takes about 20-30 minutes on foot.
  • Staircase:There are many stairs inside the castle, so it is required to wear comfortable sneakers
  • Slippled floor:The interior of the castle can be slippery, so be careful when walking
  • Ropeway use:If you want to move conveniently, recommend using ropewei or lift
  • Temperature control:Preparing light outerwear on a strong windy day
🍊 Precautions when purchasing local specialties
  • Price comparison:Souvenirs sold at Matsuyama Airport or major tourist attractions are higher than city marts.
  • Tangerine specialty:Matsuyama is famous for citrus such as tangerine and sells a variety of souvenirs and desserts
  • Botte -chan product:Botte -chan -related products are also popular souvenirs, so purchase after checking prices and types in various places
  • Use of local marts:Price comparison at local marts or department stores before purchasing souvenirs
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • summer:It is hot and humid, so it is necessary to eat cool and enough moisture
  • winter:It can be cold due to the nature of the inland region, so prepare for winter clothes
