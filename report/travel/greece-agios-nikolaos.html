⚠️Agaos Nicolaos Travel Note

🌡️ Mediterranean climate precautions

Summer:The temperature from June-September is high and dry, so enough water intake and UV protection required

  • Prevention of heat stroke:Outdoor activity restraint between 12 pm and 4 pm, rest in the shade
  • Dry climate:Low humidity, risk of dehydration, enough water
  • UV protection:Use high SPF sunscreen, hats and sunglasses are required
  • Winter:December-February is gentle, but it rains a lot of umbrella required
🚢 Boat Tour Safety Precautions
  • Spinal Longga Cruise:Wearing safety equipment when using boats
  • Check the weather:The possibility of cancellation of cruise flights on strong windy days
  • Preparation for motion sickness:Preparation of motion sickness when traveling boats
  • Safety rules:Check the location of life jackets when boarding boats
🏖️ Beach safety precautions
  • Swimming safety:Check the waves and algae status when swimming on the beach
  • UV protection:Seon cream frequently applied when exposed to the beach for a long time on the beach
  • Personal items:Pay attention to the management of valuables on the beach
  • Marine life:Snorkel protection and safe distance during snorkeling
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Recommended car rental:Recommended for using car rentals to access tourist attractions such as Crusa Village
  • Public transportation limit:Clusa Village Local Bus runs only 6 times a day
  • Irlion connection:It takes 1 hour and 30 minutes by bus from Klion
  • Mountain road driving:Caution of 600 meters above sea level until Crusa
🍽️ Food and drinking water safety

Aegian Nicola's food is generally safe, but attention is needed to prevent upset stomach while traveling.

  • Bottled water purchase:Recommended for purchasing bottled water rather than tap water
  • Seafoodism:Choosing fresh seafood, avoid suspicious food
  • Local food:Experience Greek traditional dishes and crete special products
