⚠️Tripala travel precautions

🚨 General safety precautions

Supplies Management:Tripala is a relatively safe area, but attention is needed for pickpockets for tourists.

  • Note in major tourist attractions:Tricala Castle, Archaeological Museum, and Bus Terminal Management Thoroughly Management
  • How to deal with:Multi -cash or expensive valuables are stored in hotel fronts or safes, and bags are carried in the future.
  • declaration:Integrated emergency calls 112, police 100, tourism police No. 1571
🏔️ Caution during the Meteora Tour
  • Distressed accidents frequent area:Mt. Meteora, a northern part of Greece, where travelers frequently occur in a mountainous area
  • Use of climbing designated for safety
  • Departure destination, checking destinations and securing sufficient hike time
  • Climbing plan and accompanying information to acquaintances such as family members
  • Bring your phone and auxiliary battery
  • Download GPS Maps available offline on your phone
  • Compliance with the monastery dress regulations(Wearing clothes covering your knees and shoulders)
🌍 Natural disaster precautions
  • Earthquake frequency:Greece is a country where earthquakes occur frequently, and earthquakes can occur in tricalas, so special attention is needed for safety.
  • Need to be aware of behavior in the event of an earthquake
  • Forest fire risk:Note to outdoor activities due to the high risk of wildfires throughout Greece in summer
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Athens-Tricala connection:There is no direct bus from Athens to Tricala, so you need to use a train or transfer intermediate transfer.
  • Meteora Tour Bus:Buses from Tricala to Calam Bacca runs every hour
  • San Gil Caution:If you take a bus, you have to go around the mountain road round and round five hours.
  • Timetable confirmation:Set a travel plan by checking the bus and train schedules in advance
🏛️ Precautions when visiting monasteries
  • Dress Regulations:The monastery of Meteora is a holy place of the Orthodox Church, so it is required to dress properly.
  • male:Wear long pants, short pants or short pants
  • female:Wearing long skirts, pants, and wearing shoulders and arms
  • Shooting limit:Shooting can be limited inside the monastery, so check
  • Maintain a quiet atmosphere:Recognizing that it is a place of prayer and meditation, and maintaining a quiet attitude
