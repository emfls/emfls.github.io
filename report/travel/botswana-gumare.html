⚠️ Kumare travel precautions

🚨 Health and safetyism

Main risk factors:Malaria, wildlife, robbery

  • malaria:Gumare is a malaria dangerous area, a required to take prevention drugs
  • Wildlife:Dangerous animal formats such as elephants, hippopotamers, and crocodiles, strict guides
  • Water safety:Wear life jackets when touring moko, not soak in water
  • Night going out:Absolutely prohibited to go around alone outside the camp
  • Emergency situation:Lack of medical facilities, emergency evacuation plan required
🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, 4WD vehicles required
  • fuel:Gas station interval is far away
  • GPS:Signal weakness is also required for offline maps
  • drive:Pay attention to the left traffic, the wildlife road
  • Public transportation:Risk of use of public transportation such as buses and taxis
🌡️ Climate -related precautions
  • Dry (5-10):25-30 degrees daytime, 5-15 degrees at night,
  • Woogi (11-4):Wet, hot, road flooding
  • ultraviolet rays:Very strong, sunscreen should be applied frequently
  • Dryness:Low humidity, risk of dehydration, sufficient water intake
  • dust:Dust a lot of dust, preparation of masks
💰 Price related precautions
  • Safari Tour:1 day tour of $ 200-400, preliminary check
  • lodgment:Camp site $ 50-150, more than $ 500 luxury lodge
  • Food expenses:Local restaurant $ 10-20, including camp meals
  • Tip Culture:Guide $ 10-20 a day, Camp Staff $ 5-10
  • Cash payment:Most of them are cash payment, enough cash preparation

💡 Gumare Travel Tips

  • Optimal visits:May-September dry season is best for wild animal observation
  • language:Communication can be communicated in English official language, help if you learn greetings.
  • Local food:Sethwa (corn porridge), Boge (meat dish), Morogo (vegetable dish) experience

  • communication:Mobile phone signal only in Gumare city
