🌤️ Japanese climate feature

  • Four seasons:Japan is clearly distinguished by spring, summer, autumn and winter due to temperate climate.
  • North and South Length:It is long north and south, and Hokkaido (cold) to Okinawa (subtropical)
  • summer:It is hot and humid, and it rains a lot due to the rainy season (June -July) and typhoons (August -September)
  • winter:Hokkaido and Northeast are snowing and cold, and the Pacific coast is clear and dry.
  • Spring/Autumn:The natural scenery of the seasonal scenery such as cherry blossoms (March -April) and autumn leaves (October -November) is beautiful
  • Main current:Kuroshio (turbulence), Oyashio (Korean Wave) currents and monsters
