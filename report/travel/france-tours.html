⚠️Tour travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Tour train station, flue around the square, tourist attractions

  • Tourist Target:Pickpocket occurs when focusing on photography at Saint -Gatian Cathedral or Pure Art Museum
  • Winery Tour:Pickpocketing when attention is reduced after wine tasting
  • Market area:Risk of pickpockets when distributed during shopping in the local market
  • How to deal with:The bag is carried in the future, the valuables are distributed, and the cash is at least only
🚖 Traffic use precautions
  • One hour to use TGV in Paris, recommendation for advance reservation
  • Tour downtown can be enough to walk
  • Luir Goseong Tour is recommended to use a car rental or tour bus
  • Old town tours can be made with mini trains or bicycle rentals
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • May-October is the optimal travel time
  • Even in summer, it can be chilly in the evening, so long clothes are prepared
  • There are many outdoor activities during the winery tour, so sunscreen required
🚫 Time to avoid

In winter (December-February), the weather is cold and some wineries are closed and inadequate for tourism.
