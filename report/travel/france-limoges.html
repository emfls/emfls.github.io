  • Adrien Dumusu National Museum and Bernardo Workshop are recommended in advance

🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Due to the nature of the limit -fighting area, the temperature change is severe

🚗 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • 1 hour and 30 minutes from Paris to TGV
