⚠️ Jaute travel precautions

🚨 Note that safety and emergency situations

Main precautions:Lack of medical facilities, restrictions on communication infrastructure

  • Medical emergency situation:There are only basic clinics in the magnitude, so it is required to be transported in case of serious emergency.
  • Communication:The Internet and telephone connections are unstable, making it difficult to contact when emergency
  • Risk of isolation:In case of deterioration of the weather, it can be isolated to the island by cancellation
  • Marine safety:When snorkeling, it must be accompanied by local guides, swimming alone
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Airlines limit:Only 2-3 times a week of domestic flight from Maju-ro to magnetic
  • Boat movement:Make sure to check the weather and algae when moving between islands
  • Road situation:There are almost no packaging roads and most of the road roads
  • Public transportation:There are few buses or taxi services, walking or using bicycles
  • Lack of fuel:Be careful when using a car rental due to unstable gasoline supply
