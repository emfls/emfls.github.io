Major areas:Nearby mountainous areas, nearby borders

  • Hiking Safety:Hiking alone, must accompany guide or group activities

  • Weather change:Mountain climate change can be changed rapidly, weather information occasionally confirmed
  • Emergency situation:Mountain mobile phone signal weakness, emergency contact network secures in advance
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Baku-Cuba moves:It takes about 3 hours, caution
  • Local transportation:Recommended for using public transportation, car rental or taxi
  • Road situation:Mountain unpaved road section
  • Fuel supply:Limited to gas stations in the mountainous region, checking fuel in advance
