⚠️ Umeo travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Around the station, rare night road, crowded bus and train station

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing attention, bags, bags, etc. in the station, bus stop, museum, etc.
  • Bicycle theft:Essential locking time when using bicycles, compliance with returning rental bicycles
  • Going out late at night:Be careful not to go alone
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transport strike:Due to the nature of Sweden, strike can be occurred without notice, alternative transportation check in advance
  • Train and bus reservation:If you make a reservation in advance, it is cheaper and early sold out during the peak season.
  • Bicycle use:The bicycle road is good, but the winter freezing
  • Walking:Winter snow and ice roads in winter
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):Temperature 12-20 degrees, white night phenomena, sunscreen and sunglasses required, compared to mosquitoes
  • Winter (11-3):Temperature -12 ~ 0 degrees, very cold, snowy, winter clothes and wintering required
  • Spring/Autumn:A large crossover, preparation of outerwear and umbrella
  • Aurora:A clear night in winter, if you are lucky, you can observe the Aurora
💰 Price and security precautions
  • Tourist destination:Museum, cafe and restaurant prices are relatively high
  • Museum entrance fee:Most of them are 50 ~ 150 SEK, some free admission
  • Tip Culture:Tips are not required, 5-10% autonomy in the restaurant
  • shopping:Purchase after checking the price of Swedish specialties (birch, design props, etc.)
