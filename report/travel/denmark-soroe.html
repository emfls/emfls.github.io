⚠️Soren travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Helsing in the downtown and stations such as Helsinguar, Odense and Copenhagen

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing in the station, tourist attractions, buses, etc.
  • Fake police:ID card and wallet demand fraud attention
  • Going out late at night:Be careful to go alone after 10 o'clock in the station and nightlife
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Bicycle road:Pay attention to bicycle road invasion when walking
  • Public transportation:Payment of fines at the time of check, purchase in advance
  • Train connection:Convenience of transportation between major cities such as Copenhagen, Odense and Helsinguar
  • Weather change:Waterproof jacket required because it is wind and rain frequent
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Summer (June-August):The temperature of 15-22 degrees, windy and cool
  • Winter (12-2):Temperature 0-7 degrees, cold and rainy and frequent
  • Spring/Autumn:Moody weather, umbrella and outerwear preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:Sunscreen required even on cloudy days
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:The price of famous tourist destinations cafes and restaurants is high
  • Museum entrance fee:125 ~ 145dkk, Kronbor Castle, Funen Folk Village 145dkk
  • Tip Culture:Including service charges, no tips are not required
  • Cash use:Most of the card payments, only small cash preparation
