🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Guatemala City, a remote area late at night

  • robbery:Guatemala is unstable, so it is prohibited from wearing expensive items, cash is distributed.
  • Drugs:Since drug trading is a frequent area, it prohibits contact with suspicious people
  • Fake police:Since fake police may require money, you can check your ID card.
  • Going out late at night:Prohibited to go alone after sunset, recommend using group tours
  • Volcanic climbing:Be sure to climb with local guides, weather changes
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Chicken bus:Chicken buses, a local public transport, are cheap, but safety issues, tourists recommend using shuttle
  • Boat safety:Wearing a life jacket during the Attlake Lake Boat Tour
  • Road status:The mountainous terrain is rough and there are many unpaved sections.
  • Taxi:There are many taxis without meters, so preliminary fee negotiations are required
  • Border movement:Mexico, the possibility of bribery when moving the Belly Border Border
