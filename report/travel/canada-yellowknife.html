⚠️ Yellow Knife Travel Precautions

🌡️Extreme weather attention

Winter temperature:It can be lowered from -20 degrees to -40 degrees

  • Statue risk:Exposed skin is required to wear frostbite, complete winter clothes within 5 minutes

  • Outdoor activities:Limit outdoor activities in the event of extreme cold, identify the location of indoor shelters
