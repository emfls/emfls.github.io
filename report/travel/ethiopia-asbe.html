⚠️Asbe travel precautions

🚨 Extreme environmental safetyism

Main risk factors:Extreme high temperature, volcanic gas, high change

  • Extreme high temperature:The daytime temperature is over 50 degrees, the risk of heat stroke, enough water intake and shade rest required
  • Volcanic gas:Atta Alee Volcano Risk, Wind direction and gas mask preparation
  • Dewnity risk:Available in a dry environment, water can be driven rapidly, and more than 500 ml per hour.
  • Burn risk:Hot salt crystals and volcanic rocks, thick shoes and gloves are required
🏥 Medical and health precautions
  • malaria:High risk of mosquito -mediated diseases, prevention drugs and insect repellent use required
  • Medical facilities:Lack of local medical facilities, preparation of sufficient medicines for emergencies
  • drinking water:Only eat bottled water, avoid ice and raw field
  • Altitude disease:The possibility of altitude disease due to rapid altitude changes
  • Skin protection:Strong ultraviolet rays, dry environment, sunscreen and moisturizers required
🌡️ Climate -related precautions
  • Dry (10-5):Temperature is 40-50 degrees, very dry, tourism optimal, but extreme heatism
  • Woogi (June-September):35-45 degrees of temperature, high humidity, road situation worsening
  • Daily crossroads:Daily and night temperature differences of 20 degrees or more, required for thermal items
  • Sand Storm:Sudden sand storm can occur
  • Volcanic activity:Eta Alee Volcanic activity is required to check
