⚠️Gurre travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Borders around the border area,

  • Terrorism:Extremist group activities such as Boko Haram, Sahel, required to confirm the travel alarms of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • robbery:Prohibition of going out at night, minimum cash and valuables
  • Kidnapping:Don't go alone, accompany your local guide
  • Border area:Chad, prohibit access near the Nigerian border
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road status:Many unpaved roads, unable to pass in the rainy season
  • Public transportation:Bus timetable inappropriate, required to secure spare time
  • taxi:No meter, preliminary fee negotiations required
  • Rent -a -car:4WD car recommendation, accompanied by experienced local driving
  • fuel:Gas station spacing, extra fuel preparation
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Dry (10-5):Temperature 15-40 degrees, extremely dried, enough water intake
  • Woogi (June-September):25-35 degrees of temperature, increase in moist and malaria mosquitoes
  • Harmatan:December-February Sahara sand breeze, respiratory protection required
  • Daily crossroads:Daily and night temperature differences of 20 degrees or more, preparation
  • ultraviolet rays:UV rays in the desert area, completely blocked
💰 Price related precautions
  • Cash Society:Credit cards cannot be used, enough cash
  • Exchange:Recommended for copper after exchange in Niamei
  • Negotiation culture:Price negotiation required in the market
  • Tip Culture:Guide, Tips for Tip to the driver
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Mosquito repellent required, prevention drugs
  • drinking water:Only drinking water, avoid ice
  • food:Eat only completely cooked food
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical facilities, preparation for emergency situations
