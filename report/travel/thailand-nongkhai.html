⚠️ Nongkai travel precautions

🌡️Checks related to weather

Tropical climate:Nongkai is a hot and humid tropical climate of the year, especially in summer

  • High temperature and humidity:Summer temperature is very high and humidity is high, so there is a risk of dehydration.
  • Strong ultraviolet light:High SPF UV blockers required to prevent burns due to strong ultraviolet rays in the tropics
  • Preparation for the rainy season:During the rainy season in May-October
  • Mosquitoism:Due to the nature of the Mekong River, there are many mosquitoes, so prepare mosquitoes and long -sleeved clothes
🚗 Precautions when using transportation
  • flight:After moving from Bangkok to Udon Tani Airport, travel from buses or cars to Nongkai, Woodon Thani is 50km from Nongkai
  • Train:Train can be used daily at Central Train Station in Bangkok
  • Bus section:Depart from Bangkok Book Town (Mochit 2) to Nongkai every day
  • Road:Expressway from Bangkok to Saraburi, Nachachashima, Concan, and Udon Tani arrive at Nongkai
🍽️ Food and hygiene precautions
  • Street food:Select a place with a good hygiene when eating local street food
  • Bottled water drinking:Do not drink tap water directly, drink only water or boiled water
  • Iceism:It is safe to avoid the ice of drinks sold on the street
  • Spicy food:Thai food can be very spicy, so you can order it at first
  • Local special food:Starting with a small amount of special food experiences in the Nongkai area
🛕 Religion and cultural courtesy
  • When visiting the temple:Wearing clothes covering your shoulders and knees, taking off your shoes and entering
  • Buddhist shooting:Taking a picture with a Buddha statue is considered a rude behavior.
  • Prohibit the touch of the head:In Thailand, the head is sacred, so it prohibits the touch of others' heads
  • To the toes:It is rude to make the toes to the Buddha or the person
  • Royal Respect:Essential expression of respect for the Thai royal family
🌍 Border regional precautions
  • Laos Visa:Check whether a separate visa needs when entering Laos
  • Border procedure:When passing the border, compliance with the immigration process and prepare necessary documents
  • International Bus:17,000kip when using international buses from Vientiane to Nongkai, requires a passport when purchasing a bus table
  • Exchange:Recommended to use official currency exchange in the border area
🎪 Precautions when participating in the festival
  • Songlan Festival:It is a festival that sprinkles water, so prepare a waterproof bag or case
  • Roy Kratong Festival:It is a festival held by the Mekong River, so there are many crowds.
  • Naga Meteor Festival:Maintain safety distances for festivals that see meteor phenomena falling to the Mekong every year
  • Festival schedule:The festival schedule may change every year, so you need to check it in advance.
