⚠️Punaputi travel precautions

🌊 Environment and climateism

Main precautions:Sea level rise, strong ultraviolet rays, limited freshwater

  • Climate Change:Risk of flooding due to rising sea levels, preparation for moving in highland
  • Strong ultraviolet light:Very strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required to location near the equator
  • Lack of freshwater:Freshwater supply that depends on rainwater, water savings required
  • Typhoon season:November-April Typhoon Risk, Weather Information Occasional Check
🚢 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Airlines limit:2-3 times a week, only the flights of Fiji flights, preliminary reservation required
  • Airport location:Punaputi International Airport is located in the center of the village

  • Lack of fuel:Fuel supply unstable, establishment of public transportation plans
