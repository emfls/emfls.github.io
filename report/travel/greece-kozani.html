⚠️Kozani travel precautions

🚨 Pickpocket attention

Major areas:Areas with many tourists such as Kozani Central Plaza and Bus Terminal

  • Storage of valuables:Keep your passport or big money in a safe place, and only small cash
  • Conscious attention:When walking on the road or taking pictures,
  • Bag management:Put the bag in front and lock the zipper
  • Tourististism:Especially in famous tourist destinations and public transportation
🎫 Preliminary reservation required
  • Siaesita Ski Center:In the winter ski season, the equipment rental and lift reservation recommended pre -booking
  • Cooking workshop:Reservations required at least two days ago are required for cooking experiences in local homes.
  • Winery Tour:Guests and tasting in Kozani area are required for advance reservations.
  • Lake Activity:Water sports at Lake Polymill Ross can be canceled depending on the weather, so secure relaxation schedule
🚌 Precautions on public transportation
  • Thessaloniki connection:Kozani is 2 hours and 30 minutes by bus from Thessaloniki
  • Athens connection:In Athens, 5-6 hours by bus, train connection is limited
  • Recommended for using car rentals:Rent -a -cars are the most convenient for surrounding natural attractions and mountainous areas.
  • Taxi:Check the meter when using taxi from downtown Kozani to major tourist attractions
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Continental Climate:Continental climate with cold winter and mild summer
  • Summer preparation:In summer, it is gentle, but sunscreen and hat preparation
  • Winter preparation:Winter is cold and snowy, so warm clothes and winter products are required
  • Mountain climate:In high -altitude areas, the difference in temperature is large, so prepare clothes
🗣️ Language communication

Although Greek is official language, English can be communicated in tourist attractions. Knowing the basic Greek greetings will help you communicate with locals.
