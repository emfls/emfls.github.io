⚠️ Louis travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Borders around the border area,

  • Political anxiety:Burundi has a political instability, so avoid protests and assembly areas.
  • Border area:Smugglers or illegal tradingism
  • Night going out:After the sun, don't go alone, but go to the group
  • Local guide:Must be accompanied by local guides when trekking in mountainous areas
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Check the rate set in advance when using a bus or taxi
  • Road status:In the rainy season, it is recommended to use 4WD vehicles in the rainy season
  • Lack of fuel:There is a rare gas station, so the fuel is secured in advance
  • drive:Familiar with local traffic laws, international driver's license required
  • Mobile time:It takes about 3-4 hours from Bujum Bura to Louis
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (10-5):Intensive heavy rains and flood risks, raincoats required
  • Dry (June-September):Dry and dusty, enough water intake
  • Plateau climate:Big and chilly at night
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen near the equator
  • lightning:Lightning risks in the rainy season, avoiding high places
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:High risk of mosquito -mediated diseases, required to take preventive drugs
  • drinking water:Prohibition of drinking tap water, drinking water or boiled water
  • food:Avoid raw or less ripe food
  • Medical facilities:Lack of medical facilities, preparation for emergency situations
  • Altitude disease:Headache, nausea, nausea at high altitude
