⚠️Adado travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Outside area, late at night

  • Terrorism:Terrorist organizational areas such as Al Shabab, accompanying local guides
  • Armed intensity:Risk of armed intensity in remote areas, prohibition of night movement
  • Kidnapping:The abduction case for foreigners can occur, prohibit alone
  • Lack of conflict:Evacuate immediately in case of conflict between regions
  • checkpoint:Cooperation with the government forces and the militia checkpoint
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Road situation:Many unpacked roads, 4WD vehicles required
  • Public transportation:Recommended for lack of public transportation facilities, use of dedicated vehicles
  • flight:Domestic flights irregularities, preparation for the possibility of schedule change
  • Lack of fuel:Lack of gas station, need to secure sufficient fuel
  • Night driving:Absolute prohibition, only the daytime time
🌡️ Climate and health precautions
  • Dry (12-3):Temperature 25-35 degrees, very dry, enough water intake
  • Woogi (April-June, October-November):Flood risk, road blocking
  • malaria:High risk of mosquito -mediated diseases, required to take preventive drugs
  • Water -person disease:Cholera, Danger of typhoids, only boiled water
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong ultraviolet rays near the equator, thoroughly blocked
💰 Economic and living precautions
  • Infrastructure lack:Electricity and water and sewage facilities instability
  • communications network:Unstable unstable internet and telephone connection
  • Medical facilities:Lack of modern medical facilities and measures to prepare for emergency situations
  • drinking water:Prohibition of drinking tap water, only bottled water
