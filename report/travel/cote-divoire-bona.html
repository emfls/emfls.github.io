⚠️ Bona travel precautions

🚨 security and safetyism

Major areas:Night, outskirts around the port

  • pickpocket:Pickpocketing attention from the market and the port, the bag is carried in the future and the zipper lock
  • Night going out:Don't go alone after the sun, accompany you with local guides
  • Photo shooting:Prohibition of shooting government buildings, military facilities, and port facilities
  • Conflict with locals:Avoid religion or political topic dialogue
🚌 Traffic and mobile precautions
  • Public transportation:Local buses may not be safe, so recommend using taxi or car rental
  • Road status:There are many dirt roads and worsening road conditions in the rainy season
  • Taxi:Check the meter and preliminary negotiations
  • Rent -a -car:International driver's license required, local traffic laws
  • flight:Need to move to Bona from Abijang International Airport to Bona
🌡️Checks related to weather
  • Woogi (5-November):Rain and flood risks, umbrellas and waterproof products required
  • Dry (12-4):Occurrence of dust wind (harmatan), wearing masks
  • High temperature and humidity:The year-round temperature 25-35 degrees, enough water intake
  • ultraviolet rays:Strong sunlight, sunscreen and hat required
  • Current:The Atlantic Current is strong, so attention to swimming
💰 Price related precautions
  • Tourist destination:Price inflation of foreigners
  • Market bargaining:In the traditional market, bargaining is common
  • Tip Culture:Tip customs in the service industry
  • Cash use:The use of credit cards is limited, so prepare cash
🏥 Health -related precautions
  • malaria:Use of mosquito repellents and taking prevention drugs
  • yellow fever:Can't enter the country without a certificate of vaccination
  • drinking water:Drink only bottled water and avoid ice
  • food:Using a reliable restaurant rather than street food
  • Medical facilities:Because medical facilities are limited, travel insurance is required
