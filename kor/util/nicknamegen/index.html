🧪 What does this tool do?

It generates 5 random nicknames at once, based on your selected style. Use them for usernames, game IDs, or creative inspiration!

🎨 What styles can I choose?

You can select from styles like "Adjective + Animal", "Animal_Noun", or "Adjective + Noun" – all with random numbers at the end for uniqueness.

📋 Can I copy the names?

Yes! Each nickname has a “Copy” button next to it. Tap to instantly copy the name to your clipboard.

🔁 Can I generate new nicknames?

Just click the “Generate” button again or switch styles to get a fresh batch of 5 names each time.

🔒 Is this tool private?

Yes. It’s 100% browser-based, with no sign-up or tracking. Everything happens locally on your device.
